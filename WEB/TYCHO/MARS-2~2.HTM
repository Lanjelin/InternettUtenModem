Mars pictures from the seasons of 1992/1993, 1994/1995 and 1997

Since we received our Lynxx CCD camera in 1992 we have had the opportunity to observe Mars with it during three oppositions. The best of our images are collected here.

Images from the season 1992/1993

Image data: 8" Newtonian telescope, 9 mm. eyepiece projection.

Dates: aug 7, 1992 - nov 21, 1992 - dec 27, 1992 - jan 29, 1993

Images from the season 1994/1995

Dates: dec 16, 1994 (a,b,c) - march 5, 1995 - march 21, 1995 - march 29, 1995 (a,b) - april 08, 1995

The images of march 21, march 29 and april 09 are taken through a red filter. All other images are unfiltered, although heavily processed.

Images from the season of 1997

Jan 31st, 1997 at 01.00, 01.30, 02.00, 02.30, 03.00 and 03.30 MET - March 9th, 1997 at 22.00, 23.00, 00.00 and 01.00 (MET). - March 15th, 1997 at 23:20 (MET).
