Starshows

A Starshow centers around an aspect of astronomy.The star projector in the middle of the theatre generates over 9,000 stars and planets. Special effects, laser scanners, planet projectors and an advanced sound system all converge to make the Starshow a phenomenal experience. Lean back and enjoy the heavenly dome above you.
