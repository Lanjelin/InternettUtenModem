Over the past few years, Views of the Solar System has received a number of awards and recognition from different organizations. The following is a list of some of the these organizations:

4-Star Award from the Magellan McKinley Group

The Magellan Internet Guide has honored Views of the Solar System with several 4-star awards. This includes the Entire Tour, Saturn, and History of Space Exploration.

The Discovery Channel: Wonders of the Universe

This site explores the origins of Halley's Comet and features Comet Introducation from Views of the Solar System.

ZIA Review Site Award

The ZIA award goes to the top 2% of all the sites reviewed by ZIA. This is a family oriented Web site.

Fledge Seal of Approval

Views of the Solar System has been Fledge Approved! "What's Fledge," you ask. Well, Fledge is the moldable web experience for kids brought to you by McGraw-Hill Home Interactive. Views has survived the Fledge stringent review process and been added to its kid-appropriate database - passed with flying colors, in fact! Views of the Solar System can be found in Sleuth, the Web Detective on Fledge. Quite an honor, if we do say so ourselves.
