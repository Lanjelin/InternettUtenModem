Boserup Amateur Observatory

What is BAO?

Boserup Amateur Observatory, or BAO in short (pronounced like "SAO" in "SAOimage") is an observatory run on an amateur basis, located near the village of Boserup on southern Sealand. The site is quite dark so we are able to make some pretty good deep-sky images. The project was started in 1987 by Michael Andersen and Kim Birkelund , and soon after it was joined by Jacob Clasen , Søren Larsen . and Bjarne Lassen .
