Cosmic Voyage

In the everyday world we use a simple scale - our selves - to decide what's large and what's small. But what about the worlds that lies beyond, what is truly large, and truly smal? Now an amazing new Omnimaxfilm takes you on a Cosmic Voyage of discovery. In 42 steps, 42 powers of ten, from the vastnes of distant space, into the strange world inside the atom. Venture through time, to witness the evolution of galaxies, the cataclysmic forces that shaped the earth, and after billions of years, the emergence of awareness. And the beginning of our eternal quest for knowledge.

Cosmic Voyage - a breathtaking journey through time and space.
