Every weekend

the Planetarium has guided tours in the ESO exhibition from 12- 4pm - 10 minutes after the hour (avaiable in English ). The duration of the tour is approx. 30 minutes and it is free, if you have a ticket to the exhibition.

Driving the Mars Rover is possible from 12-4pm - this is also free if you have a ticket to the exhibition.

The 27th of March, 1st, 2nd & 3rd of April at 10pm

lecture of the Hale-Bopp comet (in Danish) in the Spacetheater. Ticket price dkr. 30,- per person - reservation at phone no. 33-121224. Observation from the lakeside of the Planetarium at 11pm.

The 4th & 5th of April

at 9pm the Planetarium holds piano concerts by Yul Anderson in the Spacetheater - duration approx. 1 hour and 40 minutes including 30 minutes break.

The 18th of March, 23rd of May, 20th of june, 15th of august and 19th of september

at 4:15pm there is a telescope lecture in the Auditorium (in Danish). Questions like, which telescope should I buy and how much should it cost, will be answered. Ticket price dkr. 30,- per person. Reservation at phone no. 33-121224.
