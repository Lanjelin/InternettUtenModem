Lasershows

The word LASER, Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation, designates a special kind of light with truly remarkable qualities. This light combined with exciting effects such as smoke, slides, stars and Omnimax-films evokes an experience that is incredibly powerful. Music and other sound effects unite the myriad elements of the show.
