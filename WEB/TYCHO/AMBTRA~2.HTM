Ambient LaserTrance - In the Dome

To the strains of soft and floating Ambient and hefty and swinging Trance, you see glowing colour spaces, 3D effects and abstract laser patterns emerge in the Planetarium's 1000 sq. metre dome.

New techniques and innovative technology makes it possible to use a mixture of laserlight, smoke, slides, Omnimax-film, music and sound effects. And with the 14,000-watt, six-channel genuine surround sound system there is only one thing to do: lean back in your seat and adjust your senses to the full power mode.

Join us on a tour you definitely considered to be beyond the bounds of possibility.

Showing Fridays (from January) at 9.00 pm.
