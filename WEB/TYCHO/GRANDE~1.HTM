Grand Canyon

An Omnimax film about the largest and deepest cleft in nature's creation. Over millions of years the Colorado River has bored its way a couple of kilometres down into the Arizona desert, creating a labyrinth of gorges and waterfalls. Go white-water rafting down the river rapids. Feel your stomach flop as you fly over dizzying precipices and through narrow ravines with the river running deep down below you.
