The Lion and the Swan

- a Starshow for small children

We take a closer look at our surrounding nature. Learn about the sun, shooting stars, planets, galaxies and constellations. See if Leo can make the Swan join him on a ride in the "Karlsvogn". And get answers to questions like: How is the rainbow made? Why is the Moon sometimes full and sometimes half? What makes the difference between summer and winter?

Showing on Tues. and Thurs. at 10.15 am and Sat. and Sun. at 12.00 noon.
