The Celestial Globe

- on general astronomy

Artwork: Loch Ness Productions

Earth, like the other planets, orbits the Sun. This is why the movements of the planets sometimes seem so surprising and complex. We look at the planets' positions and discover why they are always found in Zodiac constellations. Learn to locate, say, Taurus, Gemini and Leo in the night sky.

The presentation ends with a 3D Omnimax film entitled "We Are Born of Stars".

Showing Mon. - Fri. at 12 noon.
