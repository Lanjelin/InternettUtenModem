NBIfAFG Publications

Information for everyone:

The Niels Bohr Institute for Astronomy, Physics and Geophysics (NBIfAFG) maintains a database of publications, that may be viewed on-line:

Select department:

  • AO (Astronomy)
  • GA (Geophysics)
  • NBI (The Niels Bohr Institute)
  • ØL (The Ørsted Laboratory)
  • TAC (Theoretical Astrophyics Center)

Information for staff and students:

The staff at NBIfAFG are required to add all 1996 and newer publications to the database, and students are encouraged to do the same. Entries may be made using this fill-out form. Information for first time users is available here.

How to use the database in new papers:

Users of PC/Mac word processors may copy-and-paste the formatted entries from their Web browsers into the reference list of new papers.

LaTeX users may use the database directly in new papers (examples: short, long, "forskningsvogtning").
