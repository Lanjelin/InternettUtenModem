Exhibitions

In the hall below the space theater - the Tycho Hall - we have a permanent astronomy and spaceflight exhibition.

In the 380 m² exhibition area 44 exhibitions items have been produced. A great deal of the items are activating models.

Apart from the permanent exhibition, the planetarium offers changing exhibitions of different subjects in the Archade, surrounding the exhibition hall (the Tycho Hall).
