Omnimaxfilms

The Space Theatre shows extraordinary Omnimaxfilms on its 1,000 square metre dome screen. The spectator is encompassed by the picture, while the sound system provides a true surround effect - creating a total sensory experience of actually being there - right in the middle of events. Themes are often drawn from nature with awesome footage of mountains, volcanoes and towering ravines.
