NBIfAFG Publication Registration Form

This page allows the interactive management of publications in the NBIfAFG publication database.

To use it:

  1. Choose your department from the popup box.
  2. If you want to add a new publication, choose the publication type from the popup box.
  3. Press the appropriate button to add or edit a publication or to list or show publications.
To use this system, you need an account on the Unix system at your department. Please contact your system administrator or Ulrik Serges, if you need such an account.
