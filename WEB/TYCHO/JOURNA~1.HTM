Journal Club at the Copenhagen University Observatory

General Information


The Journal club is a bunch of people (staff and students) from the Astronomical Observatory who gets together every second friday to discuss a recently published article or two. We speak danish, unless there are non-danish-speaking persons present. Usually someone brings a cake. At the first meeting we plan who are to speak on which dates. The speaker then finds an article to discuss. The articles to be discussed at a given meeting can hopefully be found here at least a week before the meeting is held.

We meet in room k08 in the villa thursdays at 14.30
