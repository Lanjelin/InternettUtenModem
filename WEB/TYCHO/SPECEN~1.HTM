Special Effects

Special Effects - a brilliant dokumentary about the American trick-film industri. Scenes from both new and old films are shown - amongst others the Danish "Himmelskibet" from 1917. However, the emphasis is laid mostly on recent American featurefilms such as "Star Wars", "Independence Day" and "Jumanji". On top of that, the film shows some of the amazing computergraphics designed for the "Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition", which is opening in 1997. For the first time ever scenes from these films are shown in the large Omnimax format.

NB! English narration only.
