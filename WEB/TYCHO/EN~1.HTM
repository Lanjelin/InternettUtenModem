Astronomical Student Society (ASF) Skift til dansk

Welcome to the Astronomical Student Society (ASF)! We are a society of astronomy students and others with an interest in astonomy. The society is associated with the Astronomical Observatory at the University of Copenhagen. We work in collaboration with the Geophysical Student Society (GSF). Our purpose is to create and maintain a good environment at Rockefeller for students, faculty, and staff. The society is open to everyone with an interest in astronomy on a professional level. The annual fee is 30 Danish kroner (which will get you a discount on beer and soft drinks ;-)
