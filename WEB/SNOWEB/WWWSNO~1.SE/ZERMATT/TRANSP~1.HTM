Access and Transportation

Zermatt is in Southern Switzerland and easily accessible from Western, Notrhern or Southern Europe. It is within a few hours of airports in Geneva, Zurich and Milano in Italy.

The train will take you all the way to the village on the Glacier Express, which takes you on a 90 minutes long, steep and beutiful journey. International rail connection link up at Brig.

Information about railway connections

With car you can get as far as to Täsch (about 5 km/3 miles from Zermatt), where you have to park your car in one of many parkingspaces. From Täsch you either take the train or a shuffle taxi the last bit.

If the normal transport facilities doesn`t function, there is always the well equipped Air Zermatt with transportation helicopters.

Transportation in Zermatt

Since Zermatt is car-free there are some alternative transportation:

  • Electrobus
  • Electrotaxi
  • Horse taxi
  • Skilifts/Mountain railways
  • Helicopter
Zermatt map Skiing

Electrobus

There are 3 solardriven buses in constant rotation. They leave every 15 minutes approximately from the station and takes you to the main places and to the three different ski-systems. The fare is:

  • Adults - 2. Sfr
  • Children - 1. Sfr
A special skipass can be bought to include the bustransportation.

Electrotaxi

There are many small electrotaxis in traffic.

  • Imboden - (28) 67 15 67
  • Julen - 67 16 03
  • Jumbo - 67 22 44
  • Quick - 67 33 00
  • Schaller - 67 12 12
Children until 12 years are free of charge if attended by an adults. Skiers get a special rate when going to or coming from the lifts.
Nigth fares and extra charge for luggage is 50 %.

Horse Taxi

The horse-taxi works the same way as the electrotaxi but you can also take longer trips up the mountain.

Excursions to Furi max 4 persons

  • Zermatt-Furi - 100. Sfr
  • 1 h. stop - 120. Sfr
  • Each extra 1/2 h. - 30. Sfr
Night fare is extra 50 % and luggage extra charge is per suitcase - 1. Sfr, 2 bags - 1. Sfr.

Skilifts/Mountain railways

(se also "Skiing")

There are 73 lifts in Zermatt (including Cervinia).

  • 1 rack railway with 18 railcars; Gornegrat
  • 1 underground express railway; Sunegga
  • 17 arial cableways
  • 6 gondola skilifts
  • 14 chairlifts
  • 34 skilifts
Total length of mountain railways and skilifts in Zermatt: 24 km /39 miles

Helicopter/Heliskiing

Air Zermatt offers helicopter rides around the Matterhorn.

  • 20 minutes flight to the Theodul glacier. Price 160. Sfr /pers (min 4 pers)
  • Direct flight to Theodul glacier (Testa Grigia) Price 130. Sfr /pers (min 4 pers)
  • Direct flight to Unterrothorn Price 90. Sfr /per (min 3 pers)
Heliskiing
For heliskiing it is necessary to have a mountain guide. By helicopter you can reach very high peaks which gives you fantastic and long skiing. Some of the Destinations:
  • Monte Rosa 4250m - 310. Sfr
  • Alphubeljoch 3782m - 430. Sfr
  • Plateau Rosa 3479m - 290. Sfr
  • Unter Rothorn 3103m - 420. Sfr
  • Combined flights via Äschihorn (2 runs).
    Helicopter flight and mountain guide included.
