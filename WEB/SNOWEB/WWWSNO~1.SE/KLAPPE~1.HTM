Kläppen
Kläppen is a part of the Sälen area and is located in the middle of Sweden, Dalarna (see map).

The green and blue slopes are dominating the area. Many apartments and hotels lie right next to the slopes, and you can put on your skis the minute you walk outside. The season is quite long (26. Nov.-1. May).

For more information call +46 280 241 00
