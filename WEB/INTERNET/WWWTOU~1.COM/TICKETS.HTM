HOW CAN I SUBSCRIBE TO TOURBUS?

Easy! Just send an e-mail to:

LISTSERV@LISTSERV.AOL.COM

with the command 

SUBSCRIBE TOURBUS FIRSTNAME LASTNAME

in the body of the message, replacing FIRSTNAME and LASTNAME with your actual name. You'll get a message from LISTSERV within minutes asking you to confirm your subscription. Just follow the simple instructions in that message, and you're on the Bus!

NOTE: Your e-mail address will not be revealed to anyone other than the TOURBUS owners and the LISTSERV technical support staff. We hate spam as much as you, so your e-mail address is safe with us. We don't sell or rent our mailing list to anyone.

You can expect to receive TOURBUS postings twice a week - roughly Tuesday and Thursday.
