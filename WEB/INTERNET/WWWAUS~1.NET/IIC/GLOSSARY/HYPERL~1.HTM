Hyperlink

A hyperlink is a special formatted text displayed in a web-browser. Clicking on this hyperlink will usually load another page into the web browser. Hyperlinks are, if not overridden, formatted in blue color and are underlined

Tips:

  • To open a document resulting from a click on a hyperlink in a new window, click the hyperlink using the right mouse button and select Open in new windows. This might not work in your browser, how ever it does in Netscape Navigator and Internet Explorer.
