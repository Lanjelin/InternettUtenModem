Site Reviews
January, 12. 1997

If you are looking for the best web sites, then you should have a look at the directories below. These are either site of the day/week/month sites or other sites, that are rating and/or awarding other sites.

Showcase - Active Women on the Web
Address: http://www.web-publishing.de/showcase/
Showcase reviews each month 6 sites from women from around the world. You'll see for what a wide range of interests women use the Web. Women with interesting sites are invited to submit them.

Point Now !
Address: http://www.pointcom.com
Pointcom is a company, which reviews and presents lots of news sites to you every day. They also thought of users with a slow connection an have a text only page available. You can also submit a site for review by Point, however there is no guarantee they will.

Link it or Leave it!
Address: http://wwwcsif.cs.ucdavis.edu/~varmav/lili/lili.html
Truly a good idea. This site has a new site approx. each week for you to review with a single mouse click. You can choose if you should link the site or leave it. Quite a funny idea. I haven't found any similar on the net yet.

Hot 100 Websites
Address: http://www.web21.com/services/hot100/index.html
The 100 most popular sites on the Web. Updated weekly.

NBN Contents Award
Address: http://www.tricky.com/liz/
They are reviewing and rating web pages for their contents rather than for their layout and images, like many other do.
