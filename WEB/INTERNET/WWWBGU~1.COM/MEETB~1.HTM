Welcome to Beatrice's Web Guide. Please, call me B. I've been around the virtual block enough times to know what's good out there and what's a waste of time. With my team of crack reporters, I've collected links to some of the best sites on the web, and I'm always on the prowl for more.

My goal is to put together the ultimate guide to the web, or, as I always say, "Only the Good Stuff." As a rule, I'm ultra-picky. I only include sites that offer practical value you can't find off-line (no out-of-date, text-heavy epics), and I'm especially interested in what's on the web for women. If my reviews sound enthusiastic, it's because I don't bother with anything second rate.

Want to know more? Here's what you'll find on the B Guide:

    On my daily Hotlist, look for my latest web discoveries. Fab Finds are round-ups of my favorite sites of the moment.

    From any page on my site, you can always go to my reviews of outstanding web pages. There, I provide you with links to the top 3 sites in each category, and you can scroll down and click for "5 more sites worth a visit." Surf around. Check my serious News & politics picks, or the fun links at Arts or Relationships.

    Look for my virtual seal of approval, "Way Useful," throughout these pages. It marks only the sites that are the crème de la crème.

    Tech tip of the week is my quick take on what you can do now to make the most of your surfing. It's updated on Mondays.

    Where they surf is my weekly peek at the bookmarks of the stars, from Rosie O'Donnell to Dr. Ruth. Check back every Wednesday.

    My Guestbook is where you can sign in to stay in touch.

    Ask B is my in-box, my out-box and my lifeline -- to you. Suggest a review category, share (or diss) a new site and let me know what you think about my picks. Report, too, if you find a bug or a missing link.

    The Cafe is your hangout on my site. It's a chat area where -- to borrow a phrase -- you can "talk amongst yourselves" It's always open.

    So head back to my home page and surf around. And don't forget to email me if there's anything else you need.

    P.S. Be sure you don't confuse me with my pals at www.beatrice.com. I'm at www.bguide.com. Bookmark it!
