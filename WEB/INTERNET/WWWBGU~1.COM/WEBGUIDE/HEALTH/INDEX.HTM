These sites are good for what ails you. Search drug databases, find support groups or send a question to a doctor.


Atlanta Reproductive Health Center
With gems like Internet radio broadcasts and links to all things reproductive on the web (the link to the water births homepage got my attention), an Atlanta doctor turned cyberhealth guru has amassed an impressive collection of useful women's health links.

Mayo Health O@sis
What's in a name? In the case of the Mayo Clinic, a heck of a lot. A team of 15 Mayo doctors oversees the development of this web site, picking features of particular public interest or timeliness. Get your questions answered in "Ask the Physician."

Thrive
Five sections (eats, health, sex, shape and newstand) present voluminous amounts of health news -- although some is straight from the pages of Health magazine. Kudos for the interactive US pollen map and stress test, as well as the, er, downloadable advice on a better, safer sex life.
