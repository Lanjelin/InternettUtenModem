Coach the kids on Internet safety then keep 'em busy for hours here (while you surf from your laptop in the tub...).


Nye Labs Online
Pick Bill Nye the Science Guy's brain while you watch his head spin! There's tons of science info in the archives, including home experiments from each episode of the TV show and topical links to use the web for more research. Don't miss Bill's guided tour of other science web sites.

Sports Illustrated for Kids
A young sports fanatic's dream come true. Games, insider info on big sports stars and more. Don't miss Hoop-O-Matic: "It's the largest fantasy basketball league on the Internet. It's free and it's for kids," says editor Peter Kay.

Yahooligans!
Tour the sites dubbed "cool" by Yahooligans! Before kids venture out onto the rest of the web, the rules for online safety are a must-read.
