CNN Interactive
The grandma of major media online news coverage, CNN features live chats, TV tie-ins and political news in AllPolitics (a collaboration with Time Inc.) Plus: stock quotes, fashion coverage, health news, sports and weather.

The Doonesbury Electronic Town Hall
Get your daily political updates here. The familiar Doonesbury crew lights the way with daily briefings, ways to get involved in politics, flashbacks to past elections and, of course, that waffle.

Vox Pop
"Your Internet. Your Voice" -- the slogan says it all. Vox Pop goes beyond the usual resource catalog. Give "The Zipper" a spin: Enter your ZIP code and get info on your U.S. senators and representatives, or send a Netgram to your favorite (or not-so-favorite) politician.
