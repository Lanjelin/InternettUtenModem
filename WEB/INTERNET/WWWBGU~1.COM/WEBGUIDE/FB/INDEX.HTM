Angel of Fashion
The "angels" monitor other fashion- and beauty-related web sites, bestowing upon the creative the coveted Fashion Angel award. How do they pick their winners? "If on the first page we see a price, we move on. Blatant selling is rejected," says creator Rodney Dunetz. Cool.

Fashion Internet
A classic site for fashion buffs, with a keen focus on the runways, 7th Avenue, interviews with fashion insiders and clever ways of showing new looks.

Papermag's Stylin'
This NYC-based hipness manual brings street style online with a clean, colorful interface. "Fashion Schmashion" takes a wry look at fashion culture and "Street" covers the latest in youthquake style.
