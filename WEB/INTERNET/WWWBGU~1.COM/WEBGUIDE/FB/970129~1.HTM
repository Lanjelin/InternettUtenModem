Fancy free

Ah, freebies. Retail's best gimmick has hit the Net, and I'm surfing my way to a whole new look -- well, almost.

Add your "best and most creative" guestbook entry to Offthewallworld, home of the helpful jean-o-pedia. (Don't know your 501s from 575s? Neither did I.) Win a $35 gift certificate -- their jeans prices range from $50 to $90. You have until Feb. 7 to top my entry!

Armani Exchange gives away an outfit -- a sweater, hat and jeans -- to five winners of its weekly contest. Past questions included, "If I could resolve any social issue, I would..."

Every month, Aveda gives away a load of beauty products, including moisturizer, cleanser, an aromatherapy candle, shave balm, even tea! Click on feedback to get there; the survey takes about five minutes.

Photo: Aveda
Register as a visitor to Fashionmall.com and you've entered a drawing for a bottle of perfume.

Shopping for Valentine's Day? Try for a free tie at Softwear.com, maker of uber-cute techno-theme ties. Send 'em a reason why their neckties are so great (yeah, yeah) to win.
