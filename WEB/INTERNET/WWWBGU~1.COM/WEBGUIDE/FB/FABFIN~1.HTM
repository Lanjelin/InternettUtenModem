Go take a hike

Are you getting into the swing of spring? Wake up from that long winter nap with some new outdoors gear. Whether you spend your weekends actually hitting the trails, or just want to look like you do, the Net's sportiest sites are as good as "real" stores, with Internet extras to boot.

Find a bargain at the Woolrich Internet Outlet, with closeouts on classic outdoor wear. Amateur shutterbugs, there's a monthly photo contest for you.

Stock up on basics like turtlenecks at Eddie Bauer, then plan your next trip. Eddie Bauer Travel sells guided adventure-travel packages to India, Austria, Peru and the American Pacific Northwest, among other exotic locales.

Trail hounds and fishing fanatics can replace those worn out wool socks and Union suit at L.L. Bean, where you can order items securely online. Its U.S. "Park Search" gives you great info on where to go for camping and day trips.

The Intelligent Order Blank at Land's End allows you to order online using your browser, and offers an overstocks page with special deals for web customers.

If you love woolens, you'll get the warm fuzzies at Pendleton Woolen Mills. Order one of three ways (online, fax or phone), then enter a monthly drawing to win one of their famous blankets.

Eastern Mountain Sports sells rugged wear for everything from hiking to wall climbing to getting in and out of the Cherokee. The site is a work-in-progress, but promises to include an interactive trip planning guide and more. Get info on their Outdoor Skills clinics while you're there.
