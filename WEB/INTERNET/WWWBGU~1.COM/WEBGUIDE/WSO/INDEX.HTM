Sisters are doing it for themselves -- on the web, in a big way.


Feminist.com
Turn here for coverage of almost all things feminine and feminist. Got a question? "Ask Amy." She'll point out resources online and give you tips on the best way to go about finding answers.

Feminist Majority Foundation Online
This site has tons of useful info: news stories, resources on sexual harassment and domestic violence and a research center for ordering feminist journals. Bonus: The refreshingly up-to-date calendar of women-oriented events.

National Women's History Project
A showcase of the contributions of women in American history, with resources, history links and suggestions for creative ways to get involved. Test your women's history I.Q.
