Where to? The web's chock full of useful travel sites, so start planning your next getaway here and now!


Epicurious Travel
Search the destination database to dig up a vacation spot tailored to your desires. You'll be dazzled by the B&B finder, original features and the weekly list of bargain airfares, too.

Getting Around the Planet
Pathfinder (aka Time Warner) pulls together many original services to help you plan your trip, including obligatory airline reservations and destination info. But it's the extras that make this site a must-surf: currency converter, maps, weather, Zagat restaurant reviews, language tutorials and more.

Travelocity
With new airline reservation sites popping up faster than flies in summer, you've got to be choosy. Here, there are several different ways to search for the best fares and flights, plus bookings for car and hotel reservations and more.
