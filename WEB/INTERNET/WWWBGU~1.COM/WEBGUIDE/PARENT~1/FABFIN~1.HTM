Honey, I shrunk the web

Need ideas for fun with the kids? The web is brimming with stuff for parents, which can be a little overwhelming. Here are six great places you can go.

1. Check Family Planet's Parents Daily for news of interest to families, including product recalls and health and safety information.

2. Search a database of more than 2,000 summer camps, organized by price, location and activities, to find the right one for your camper.

3. Download a free immunization guide to keep track of your children's shots.

4. Help your kids plan a garden, and learn about horticulture and the environment in the process.

5. Get your children talking about issues that affect them, at Sympatico's kids-only forum, "What do you think?"

6. Click through this list of movies the whole family can enjoy, rated by other parents.
