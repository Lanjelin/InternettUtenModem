A picture really is worth a thousand words -- especially here on the web! Take a virtual tour of exhibitions all over the world with these links.


äda'web
This showcase of innovative artistic projects, each created specifically for the web, turns every notion of online art -- and traditional web site organization -- on its digital ear. (The site is named for Lady Ada Lovelace, the woman credited with authoring the first computer program.)

Dia Center for the Arts
This NYC-based museum is a conduit for great contemporary art -- both on- and off-line. Russian artists Komar and Melamid's "The Most Wanted Painting Survey" is a witty, interactive exploration of mainstream aesthetic preferences. Cast your vote!

Museum of Modern Art
The NYC institution's cleanly designed, easy-to-navigate site offers much more than the usual museum assortment of catalogs, ticket prices and gift shop hours.
