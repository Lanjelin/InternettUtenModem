DineNet Menus Online
Take a peek at restaurant offerings from more than a dozen U.S. cities. Look for two new cities each month.

Epicurious
"For people who eat" is the tag line from Conde Nast's beautiful but slow-loading site. The food section includes original articles from Bon Appetit and Gourmet contributors. Best bets? Keyword-searchable recipes, threaded discussion groups and the whimsical daily poll.

Virtual Vineyards
"Ask the Cork Dork" Peter Granoff anything about wine, and shop a good selection at reasonable prices. Virtual Vineyards is becoming a must-visit for oenophiles (you know, wine lovers!) because of its live audio and video feeds and online auctions.
