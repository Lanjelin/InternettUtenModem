Surfer:
Jennifer Tilly

The actress (nominated for an Oscar for her supporting role in Woody Allen's "Bullets over Broadway") made a splash this fall as the lovely lesbian Violet in "Bound." Her latest film, "Liar, Liar" with Jim Carrey, comes out March 21st.


    Web pick of the moment:

    The Sundance Channel
    (The web site for Robert Redford's cable film channel. Tilly was a host of the festival last month.)

    "I think the Sundance festival, channel and web site are doing a great job of showcasing young filmmakers who perhaps you won't be seeing at your local cineplex.

    "They have filmmakers' diaries. It's really personal. It's not a journalist's or reporter's idea of what it is like to be at Sundance. It's from their point of view, as people who are here for the first time.

    "People can go to the site for reviews of independent films, for what you can find on the Sundance Film Channel and interviews with independent filmmakers."
