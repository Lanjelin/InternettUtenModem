Cybersoaps galore

If daytime TV doesn't fit into your schedule or you can't get the 24-hour sci-fi channel, never fear. The web has plenty of its own steamy soaps, twisted mysteries and chilling thrillers. Last time I counted, there were 74. Some of them are even pretty good! Click on a category below.




101 Hollywood Blvd.
Young, funky filmmakers frolic in LA (isn't that what they always do?). This site is great for those who are crazy about the movie biz or brewpubs -- for some reason, both figure prominently.

East Village, The
Enter the last bastion of Bohemia through the trials and tribulations of 20something Manhattan hipsters living the life.

Geek Cereal
Lose yourself in someone else's drama through these short glimpses of San Francisco life. Sometimes truth is duller than fiction.

Lake Shore Drive
Create your own character, and play a cameo role in this Chicago-style "Melrose Place."

MelrosEast
Get past the unimaginative name, and find a funky, funny, well-written cybersoap about young hipsters in NYC that's a bit more -- well -- personal, with the characters' journal entries as narrative.

Pulse, The
Cute 20somethings find even more cute 20somethings in a NY brownstone. (Perhaps Orkin should address this problem...)

SF Blend
Follow the personal lives of young-and-beautiful San Francisco lifestylers.

Spot, The
Sexy babes of both genders living in a Hollywood beach house tell their tales of tangled affairs, failed careers, deceit and treachery.

Threads
Pick a story thread and see where it takes you in this cybersoap featuring -- surprise! -- young hipsters in NYC.

top


770 Oceanwalk
You, too, could become a character at the Fire Island time-share that houses this gay-themed drama. Send your comments, and get your beach clothes ready!

Dyke Street: A Soap
Dyke drama meets the cyberworld.

Gay Daze
We're here, we're queer, and we're fa-ha-habulous! See the saga of a doctor, a dancer, a lover, a loser, a father and a "natural" woman unfold in gay LA.

Java Jabber
Follow the drama of three gay guys and one straight girl living in a house in the suburbs with only one bathroom.

top


475 Madison Avenue
Word on the Ave. has it that the goings-on at this supposedly fictional Manhattan ad agency are on-target enough to have some *real* Madison Avenue players squirming and others snickering in amusement.

Cretins, Inc.
Giggle fiendishly as you find your way around the stunningly incompetent corporate headquarters of BilgeCorp.

Journal of a Short-Timer
Read the weekly journal of a disillusioned teacher in a hard-knock high school.

So@pNet
Real events allegedly influence the dramas that unfold at a fictional software company.

top


East Hampton Rag
Follow mysterious happenings and shady characters through the haunts of East Hampton, NY.

Pop. 89
Life in the tiny town of Coldwater, Ohio (pop. 89) centers around the Dreamland Movie Theater and the Hollywood flick that was never made.

Telluride Avalanche
Hot-to-trot ski bumbs turn snow into slush in this Colorado resort town (and, yes, you can get daily ski conditions, too).

top


III:am
Do you ever wonder what people do at 3 o'clock in the morning? Find out in these interviews with real people on the street at 3 am.

t@p Virtual Dorm
Peer into the lives of real college students via journal entries, CU-SEE-Me, screen grabs and chat sessions.

top


Above the Unicorn
Be part of a group that's genetically creating superhumans to replace corporate employees.

Brentwood
This Real Audio techno-murder mystery generously includes a script-like text version. (That way, you're boss won't know you're catching up on the action at work!)

Crystal Planet
Sci-fi adventure abounds on the planet Meridius.

EON-4
It's Earth after the discovery of extraterrestrial life, with plenty of technical wizardry and serious sci-fi.

Generation War
This cyberpunk sci-fi drama set in the very scary future, filled with live actors, digital sets and special effects has been called the first made-on-a-PC movie.

Lurker Files, The
The Lurker controls the minds of college students in an online chat room. Beware!

Media Secrets
A male/female team of reporters uses hackers to get info to create breaking headlines.

Root
An artificial life form who's been around for four millennia plays at games of chance as multimedia pushes his sci-fi adventures.

Techno3
Could this techno-thriller featuring three hot babes and the evil cyber-lord who controls them be the "Charlie's Angels" of tomorrow?

Cracks in the Web
Follow Flap Jack through this well written, detail-driven weekly spy thriller.

Heart<BR> Hotel, The
Enter different rooms of the VRML whodunit hotel.

Kapow
Snoop around the web and greater LA solving nutty mysteries with bumbling super sleuth Mace Broade and his sidekicks.

Letters from Abroad
Online crime-solvers get a shot at a London vacation.

Scrolling Mystery Theater
Creative use of frames showcases detective fiction and thrillers.

Strange Case of the Lost Elvis Diaries, The
Get your online comic mystery with overtones of "The Maltese Falcon."

Club MODE

Zoloft
