K-12 Workshop:
Internet Society School Networking Colloquium
24 June 1997, Tuesday
Registration Fee: US $95 (early registration)/US $105 (late registration)

This one-day K-12 colloquium illuminates the broad range of technical and non-technical issues surrounding deployment and use of the Internet in schools, emphasizing primary and secondary education settings.

Tentative Agenda (subject to change):
7:30 - 8:30 Registration/Continental Breakfast
8:30 - 8:45 Opening Session - Welcome Overview
8:45 - 10:15 Transformation of Teaching and Learning
10:30 - 12:00 Basic Network Applications
12:00 - 13:00 LUNCH
13:00 - 14:30 School Networking Technology
14:45 - 16:15 Implementation Issues
16:15 - 17:00 Closing Session
