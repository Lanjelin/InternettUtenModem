Registration for INET 97
Register Early and Save!

The INET 97 registration fee covers attendance at all INET 97 conference sessions June 24-June 27, 1997: Opening Reception, Gala evening, luncheons, coffee breaks, and all conference materials, including the conference program, book of abstracts and CD-ROM proceedings.

Accompanying Person/Guest Registration

The registration fee for an accompanying person/guest includes the Opening Reception and the Gala evening. Attendance at the conference sessions, luncheons, and conference materials are not included in the fee.

To Register

To register, complete the registration form. You may also print out the form and return to: INET 97, Internet Society, 12020 Sunrise Valley Drive, Suite 210, Reston, VA 20191-3429, U.S.A;. fax to (703) 648-9887; or e-mail: inet97@isoc.org.

Housing

The Internet Society has contracted with several hotels in Kuala Lumpur to provide housing accomodations for INET 97 attendees. Information about discounted hotel rooms will be available in mid-March, 1997.

Airline Reservations

The Internet Society has contracted with Malaysia Airlines to provide discounted airline reservations for INET 97 participants. To contact Malaysia Airlines, look for the office nearest you. Be sure to check this website in late March for the INET 97 official travel agency information.

Passport and Visa Requirements

All foreign visitors entering Malaysia must possess a valid passport or other internationally recognized travel documents, endorsed for traveling in Malaysia and with a validity period of least six months beyond the time of stay allowed in Malaysia. Participants requiring a visa should apply immediately to Malaysian consular offices or diplomatic missions in their countries in order to avoid delays in travel to the conference. Additional information is available from local travel agents.

Accommodations for Attendees with Disabilities and Special Needs

We are committed to making conference activities accessible to all attendees. Persons with disabilities or special needs should inform us so that we can make the appropriate arrangements. Contact the Internet Society at inet97@isoc.org or 1 (703) 648-9888.
