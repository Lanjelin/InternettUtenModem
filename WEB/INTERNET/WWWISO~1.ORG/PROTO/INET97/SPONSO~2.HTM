Sponsoring INET 97
Capture immediate market exposure and year-long recognition for your company by becoming a sponsor of
INET 97!

As a sponsor, take advantage of a high visibility sponsorship when the industry's most prominent internetworking professionals crowd the Putra World Trade Center at ISOC's Annual Conference in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, June 24-27, 1997. Program events are scheduled throughout the entire three-day conference, with a projected 1997 attendance of 2,500 participants from 150 countries. This premier conference provides an international platform focusing on developing and implementing Internet networks, technologies, applications, and policies worldwide for infrastructure development.

Jump right to the Sponsorship Form, or look over the INET 97 Sponsorship Opportunities and Benefits, as well as a listing of INET 96 sponsors.
