Help Wanted

Webmaster

The Internet Society’s International Secretariat, the international organization for global cooperation and coordination for the Internet and its internetworking technologies and applications, has an immediate requirement for a full-time on-site Webmaster for its Reston, Virginia, USA, headquarters office. We offer a friendly, small (but growing) office environment working for a prestigious Internet organization with a global, international focus.

We are looking for an individual with a proven track record in website development. Responsibilities will include:

  • Building and Installing WWW Daemons
  • Selecting and Developing Tools for HTML Authoring and Maintenance
  • Defining the Site's "Look and Feel"
  • Soliciting and Editing New Contributions
  • Creating and Maintaining Online Graphics and Documents
  • Interfacing to Databases using CGI
  • Establishing and Maintaining Relationships with Other Web Sites

The successful applicant will have experience with one or more of the following:

  • Content Creation Software (e.g., Adobe PageMill, Illustrator, Photoshop)
  • Site Maintenance Applications (e.g. SiteMill, MOMSpider)
  • Sun Solaris Development Environment (e.g. PERL, C, shell, dbm)

    Experience in developing international content, and interest or experience in advanced development tools (e.g. Macromedia Director, JAVA, Oracle, etc.) are a plus. All applicants should supply reference sites which they helped to create.

    The Internet Society offers a competitive salary and excellent benefits package in a friendly, non-smoking environment. Please, no phone calls, email, or faxes; forward your resume with cover letter including salary requirements to Webmaster, Internet Society, 12020 Sunrise Valley Drive, Reston, VA, 20191-3429.

    The Internet Society is an equal opportunity employer.
