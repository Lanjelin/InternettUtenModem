We've started a free mailing to let you know of late breaking news and features on our site and new products we've decided to carry. We call it The Armchair Brief. To get it you've got to request it. We won't sell, rent or exchange our email address list. We don't ask you to fill out a questionnaire; we only ask you for your email address; you can optionally tell us of any topics you'd like to see on our site or tell us of special products you'd like to see us carry. In the unlikely event that you don't find The Armchair Brief informative, simply email us and we'll instantly remove your name from our list. That's all there is to it!
