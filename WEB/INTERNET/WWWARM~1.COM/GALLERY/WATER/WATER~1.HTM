Art Deco Water Fountain

Armchair World's own indoor water fountain. Outdoor water fountains have been a delight since times of antiquity. Now you can have your own indoor personal water sculpture to be put by your bedside or on your desk or coffee table. Combining the movement of water with soothing sound, our sculpture brings the outside into your home or office while making a unique and quietly elegant statement that complements any decor. Our fountain has a translucent cobalt blue glass base with a polished copper water column surrounded by iridescent glass beads. Water gently flows from the column and side elements and courses over the beads in a subtly changing pattern. The fountain has a six-inch footprint, is eight inches high and is powered by a small and quiet submersible pump. It doesn't splash and is virtually maintenance free except for occasional addition of water lost by evaporation. Vary the amount of water in the glass base to change the intensity of the sculpture's sound. You are bound to find our water fountain meditative, relaxing and a beautiful addition to your environment.
