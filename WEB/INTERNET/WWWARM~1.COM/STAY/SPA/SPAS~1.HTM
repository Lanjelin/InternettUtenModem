The Ashram
P.O. Box 8009
Calabasas, CA 91372
818-222-6900

The Ashram is a place to know when you are tired of the world and need somewhere to go. A minimum of food and a maximum of exercise is the formula for this Ashram. Located 30 minutes west of Los Angeles, California and 10 minutes inland from Malibu beach the Ashram is in a secluded valley surrounded by mountains. The program is limited to 12 guests at a time.

Givenchy Hotel & Spa
4200 East Palm Canyon Drive,
Palm Springs, California 92264-5294
619-770-5000
619-324-7280 FAX

Located in the city noted for relaxed glamour, Givenchy in Palm Springs benefits from vistas of extraordinary desert beauty. The Givenchy Hotel and Spa, a lushly landscaped gated retreat, is designed for privacy, serenity and personalized service. Individualized couture programs for weekend or week-long stays include unique revitalizing and beautifying treatments.

The Oaks at Ojai
122 East Ojai Avenue
Ojai, California 93023
805-646-5573

Only an hour and half north of Los Angeles the Oaks attracts a fitness-savvy crowd that is serious about exercise. The owner of the Oaks at Ojai and the Palms at Palm Springs health resorts, Sheila Cluff, is a world renowned authority on physical fitness. A visit to the Oaks is more than just a spa vacation - it's a life affirming experience. Leave the designer sweats at home.

Skylonda Fitness Retreat
16350 Skyline Blvd.
Woodside, California 94062
415-851-4500

Nestled in a grove of redwood and madrone is Skylonda's unforgettable lodge. Built of ponderosa logs and river rocks, the secluded luxury retreat offers views from every direction of the woods and the forest beyond. Walk and hike amidst giant redwoods. Activities alternate between vigorous physical exercise, meditation and inner centering; between group activities and quiet reflective periods.

White Sulphur Springs Resort & Spa
3100 White Sulphur Springs Road
St. Helena, California 94574
707-963-8588

Surrounded by the beauty of the Napa Valley, White Sulphur Springs is California's first hot springs resort. Its historic natural beauty has been retained since the resort was founded in 1852. With the accent on simplicity and rustic beauty, White Sulphur Springs is a destination resort that can serve the needs of a group or individual travelers
