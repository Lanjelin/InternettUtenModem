Jane Cooper House
710 North Sixth Avenue
Tucson, Arizona 85705
602-791-9677

This house is an outstanding example of turn of the century Tucson architecture reflecting Anglo-American expansion to the region which began in the 1850's. With its fired red brick exterior, ground floor porch, second story balcony and Victorian detail, Jane Cooper House signals a move from the traditional Sonoran adobe home. The home has five bedrooms each furnished with country antiques.
