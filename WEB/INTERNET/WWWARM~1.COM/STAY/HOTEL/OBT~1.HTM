Hotels Off the Beaten Track

Bathhurst Inlet Lodge
P.O. Box 820
Yellowknife, North West Territories
X1A 2N6 Canada
403-873-2595

Although the Arctic is ecologically a desert, weather at Bathurst Inlet (30 miles north of the Arctic Circle) can be quite variable. Come share the snow, come for an Arctic spring in May. Cross-country ski, travel by snowmobile and komatik, or drive a dog team and experience the spring migration of the Bathurst caribou herd.

Convict Lake Resort
Route One, Box 204
Mammoth Lakes, Ca 93546
619-934-3800

What's in a name? The year was 1871. The most famous prison break to affect Mono County has just taken place. Twenty-one desperate prisoners have just escaped from the Nevada Territorial prison in Carson City. These convicts were finally apprehended at this very lake (thus the name) upon which Convict Lake Resort now stands.

Desert Cave

Coober Pedy

South Australia 5723
Australia

Coober Pedy is in the heart of one of Australia's richest opal mining area's. Coober Pedy is located in a semi-desert area of Outback South Australia. Many of the locals live in dugouts (underground homes) where the earth gives natural temperature control. The Desert Cave allows you to experience underground-style living (underground shops, bar and display areas all within sandstone surrounds.
