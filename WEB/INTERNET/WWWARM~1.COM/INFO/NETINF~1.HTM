The Well Informed Traveler

Being a Compendium of Travel Information and Advice

In general the information and a number of the hints and tips below are aimed rather directly at U.S. citizens traveling abroad. With notable exceptions which should be evident to the browser, the tips can be applied to persons from other countries. For instance, health and security tips are pertinent irrespective of your country of origin.

In the future we will address specific issues pertaining to those of you from other countries. So check back regularly as this section is expanding!
