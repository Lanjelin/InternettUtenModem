Armchair Cuisine is an exploration of culture, memories and experience through the medium of food and wine. Here we have brought our sense of exploration from the outside world into the cyber kitchen. Continue your explorations by experimenting with the recipes you find here. Experience a by-gone era through the senses of taste and smell. Share your experiences by submitting one of your own recipes. Reminisce by comparing your experiences and recipes with some of ours.

The Taste of Memory: recipes, time, place, memory

We all have associations of food and wine with time, place and memory. In this section we present short essays and recipes that might tap into a common consciousness.

About Food & Wine: ingredients, preparation, lore, history

This section contains essays, lore, facts and fiction about food and wine. We lead off with "Thoughts on Wine" a column by our new wine author, Louis Guida. We also present a brief essay on sourdough starters - a mainstay ingredient in the larders of 19th century settlers during their migration west. Starters are a "secret ingredient" of some of our finest chefs.
