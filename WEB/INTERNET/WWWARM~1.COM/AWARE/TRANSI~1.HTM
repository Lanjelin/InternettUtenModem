About the Author

Peter Melgaard Thompson, M.D., M.S. is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychiatry at the University of New Mexico. He is also the Director of the Mood Disorders Research Unit at UNM, and Staff Physician in the UNM Mental Health Center, Albuquerque, New Mexico.
