Short Description of Services

Features

NetEscapes (tm)
Great adventures, excursions and escapes. Expand your horizons, learn about other cultures, share adventures. Motivation for your own exploration of the world. Virtual or actual - that will be your final question.

Armchair Cuisine
Cuisine connected to time, place and memory: recipes, foods, ingredients and preparation - virtual synesthesia. Recreate a memory, create future memories with our recipes, share your memories by sending us your recipes with a story. We could add yours to ours.

Perspectives on Reality: The Time Warp
What is Armchair World's Time Warp? It's a glimpse into our past and a look into possible futures. Articles include "Los Angeles in the 1930's ", "Women Inventors" and more.

Perspectives on Science
Our perspective on the physical world. Find out how rainbows are formed (by Dr. Murty Mantravadi). Learn how Human Factors research has impacted industry, technology and our everyday life (by Dr. John Lyman).

The Well Informed Traveler
Almost everything you need to know about travel. Add this to your "A" list for future travel research. Topics include tourist bureaus, protecting yourself against terrorism, travel scams, customs regulations, information by country and much more.

Travel Medicine
Keep up with topics in travel medicine. This section leads off with a paper on Travel Medicine by travel and tropical medicine specialist Dr. Alan Spira. Be prepared before you go. A must for the well informed traveler.

Wellness
Investigate the world of psychiatry written with the layperson in mind. This section is anchored with a bi-monthly column on mental health and mental illness by psychiatrist Peter Melgaard Thompson. His column is titled "In Transition".
