The Travel Medicine Center provides all immunizations (including yellow fever) for travel; travel counseling for healthy, safe journeys; and treatment of travel-related illnesses. Utilizing the most current data on worldwide health conditions the Center can provide the most accurate and complete travel care available. Maintaining good health during your trip is more than just getting vaccinated; the Center teaches you how best to avoid illness and have a healthy, happy trip. Climactic and geographic considerations are all included in the individualized care, and customized health kits are sold providing a complete preparation for your journey.

The Travel Medicine Center also provides medical screening examinations for SCUBA diving courses, and education about health and safety while diving.
