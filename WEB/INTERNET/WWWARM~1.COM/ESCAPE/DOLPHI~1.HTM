Dance with the Dolphins

Swim and play with the dolphins during a week-long learning program based at Dolphin Discovery on the tiny island of Isla Mujeres, Mexico.

The Escape

There is a unique blend of fascination and interactive learning to be found in dolphin to human contact. Those on the cutting edge of the healing arts have suggested that close encounters with these complex and wondrous creatures can assist in the development of stress control techniques essential for a healthy life-styles.

During this tropical island getaway you will have the rare chance to swim, frolic and just be with the remarkable dolphins of Dolphin Discovery, Mexico's newest marine mammal facility.

Your accommodations are air conditioned and on the oceanfront. On day 1 you will meet the dolphins and enjoy a feast on the beach to set the mood for a week of elation and relaxation. Ample dock and water time is scheduled so that you will have numerous chances to experience the thrill of hands-on contact with gentle dolphins who are equally inquisitive and eager to play.

You will also have time to explore and snorkel the crystal clear waters around Island Mujeres. You may also visit the Mayan ruin of Ixchel (Goddess of the Moon.).

Quiet strolls along the beach and kicking around the quaint cobblestone streets of the fishing village combined with the lure of the dolphins make this an escape like no other!

Is This Tour for You?

Fitness level required: you should be in good physical health and physical condition to take advantage of the swimming and snorkeling activities.

Age Groups: children through adult

When: Nov 16-21, 1996. Jan 4-9, 1997. Feb 22-27, 1997. Mar 15-20, 1997. May 10-15, 1997.
