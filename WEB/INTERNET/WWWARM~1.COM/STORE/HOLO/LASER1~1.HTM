The Laser Lid (tm)

The Laser Lid was designed and patented by holographer and artist Robert Booth. What is a Laser Lid? It is both holographic art and a designer cap you can wear . Stitched on the top of the visor is a highly durable holographic diffraction grating that creates random "moire" patterns in a rainbow of colors which change with the ambient lighting. Let there be light!

The Laser Lid is uni-sex, is adjustable to fit all head sizes and comes in two colors - jet black and sophisticated white. Our cap-colors make a perfect foil for the rainbow hues of your Laser Lid.
