Our Philosophy

We provide unique items for you, the majority of which supplement the information on our site. We only sell what we would like to own or use. Our idea is to make things available that would be hard to find or get - especially if you don't live in a large metropolis.

Some of our products (such as the Art Deco Water Fountain) are of our own design and manufacture. We are adding great items regularly so check back to this page frequently. Many of our products are chosen based on our somewhat eclectic tastes. If there are items you would like us to carry, please email us with your requests. (That is how we came to carry sourdough cooking products.)

The Gourmet's Helper
Specialty products of interest to cooks and gourmets. This section features Old Fashioned Sourdough Starter and all the accoutrements.
For the Traveler
Travel related items to enhance the enjoyment and/or safety of your trip. Armchair World has found a Personal Air Purifier that is just perfect to help avoid a nasty cold or aggravated sinuses from your next plane flight. We also have fantastic Travel Medical Kits. Whether it's a Himalayan or South American trek, a deep sea dive, or a cross country skiing adventure - we have just the kit for you.
Science-Related Products
Our products are meant to inspire and educate and help bring out the hidden scientist in each of us. Perfect for presents. Several of our products can be used for a student's science project or a classroom or scouting project. We carry spectroscopes, 3-D anaglyph viewing aids and more. We have quantity prices for retail stores and educational institutions.
Holograms & Holographic Products

The Gallery
