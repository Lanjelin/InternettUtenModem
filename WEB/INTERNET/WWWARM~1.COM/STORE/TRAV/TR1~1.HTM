Personal Air Purifiers

The Air Supplytm - the first ever wearable air purifier
How many times have you taken a plane trip only to come down with a nasty cold? Take any group of 100-150 travelers, put them in a plane and probably at least one of them will have a contagious cold; if you are unlucky enough to be near the sufferer, you are at risk of catching their cold. Enter the Air Supplytm. You wear this compact beeper-sized state-of-the-art corona discharge air purifier around your neck or put it in your shirt pocket. The unit takes in contaminated air and emits clean air at a rate of 100 feet per minute towards your mouth and nose. The unit helps neutralize and destroy bacteria, smoke, viruses, dust, germs, toxic chemical fumes, mold and pet dander. A must for the modern traveler, the Air Supplytm is also of value to allergy sufferers and those with asthma and other respiratory problems. The Air Supplytm weighs only 4 oz and operates for up to 25 hours on a single 9 volt battery. It comes complete with a car plug, AC adapter and complete instructions.

First Aid Kits

The travel medical kits we carry are really well thought-out and executed. They are meant for the serious traveler especially when leaving the well-worn path where immediate medical attention might be problematic. The kits were designed by travel medicine specialist Dr. Alan Spira and are assembled by the Travel Medicine Center in Beverly Hills, California. The kits are an excellent value. An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.

'The Essential Traveler' - Travel Medical Kit
This marvelous first aid kit contains only quality components and comes in a unique three-pouch bag. It is stocked with the essentials. The kit is available with surgicel or without surgicel (surgicel is a sterile absorbable hemostat used to stop bleeding). The 'Essential Traveler' is ready for action! Be the well prepared traveler by carrying the 'Essential traveler' on your trip.
