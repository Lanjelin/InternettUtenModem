How to Order

We accept Visa® and MasterCard®, checks, cashiers checks and money orders. We currently do not take credit card orders directly over the internet. This is for your protection. We will be offering this service in the near future when internet security issues are resolved to our satisfaction. It's still easy to order from us by credit card - just fax your order to us or phone us toll-free. We also take orders mailed to us via snail mail or Pony Express, where still available. Fax and Mail Orders

  1. Get our Order Form and print it in your browser. Then fill it out. It might be helpful to take a look at our complete list of products with product codes. This should help you in filling out your order.

  2. For credit card orders, fax or mail your order to us. That's all there is to it! Your order will be given IMMEDIATE ATTENTION.

  3. If you would rather pay by check, cashier check or money order, mail your order to us. Make checks or money orders payable to Armchair World. Money orders or cashiers checks will be given IMMEDIATE ATTENTION. Please allow 2-3 weeks delivery with personal or company checks .

Telephone Orders
