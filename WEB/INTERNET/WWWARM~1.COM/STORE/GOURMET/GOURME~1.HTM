Sourdough Starter Packet
Old fashioned San Francisco style sourdough starter. Contains sourdough culture and unbleached, hard white flour. Doesn't contain bulk fillers, commercial yeast or preservatives. The culture contains "wild yeast." It makes great bread, biscuits and pancakes. It works in bread machines too! The packet comes with full instructions for making your starter, a recipe for quick sourdough biscuits and a recipe for quick sourdough bread.
