International Major Medical Insurances

Armchair World in conjunction with Insurance Services of America is now offering three major medical insurance plans for travelers WORLDWIDE. These are:

Diplomat America
15 days to 12 months of coverage for Foreign visitors coming to the USA.
Diplomat International
Up to 12 months of coverage for US citizens traveling outside the USA,
Diplomat LT (Long Term)
Up to 36 months of coverage for either Foriegn visitors coming to the USA or US Citizens traveling outside the country.

In addition to these plans, other medical plans available through Armchair World and Insurance Services of America are:

Frequent Traveler Coverage
Coverage for those who make several short trips overseas
Permanent Health Insurance
Comprehensive medical care for international citizens regardless of nationality, occupation, or current country of residence
Maternity Coverage
Long term coverage for those desiring maternity coverage -- up to $25,000 lifetime maximum
Group Coverage
For small or large groups travelling 15 days to 1 year or residing overseas permanently. Especially good for educational and religious groups as well as for missionaries.
