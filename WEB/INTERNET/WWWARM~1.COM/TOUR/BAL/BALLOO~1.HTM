Located in Napa Valley, California, the goal of this family owned company is to provide personalized ballooning experiences to a select number of adventurers. Flights launch daily just after sunrise and culminate in a champagne breakfast served in a lovely garden setting.

Bonaventura Balloon Company of Napa Valley
133 Wall Road
Napa, Ca 94558
707-944-2822
800-FLY-NAPA

Balloons are launched from selected small vineyards and wineries in the St. Helena/Rutherford area of Napa Valley. Flights (with licensed FAA commercial pilot) are made all year with small groups of four to six people per balloon. Celebratory local champagne and a sumptuous breakfast follow each unique ballooning experience.
