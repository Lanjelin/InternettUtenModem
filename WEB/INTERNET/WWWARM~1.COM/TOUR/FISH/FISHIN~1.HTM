Hakai Lodge
P.O. Box 70257
Seattle, Washington 98107-0257
206-784-4454
800-538-3551

Located north of Vancouver, B.C., and 100 miles from the nearest town, the only way to get into Hakai Lodge is by seaplane or private boat. The lodge is a rustic hideaway in the heart of the world's most famous salmon trophy fishing area. Fish for salmon, halibut, red snapper, sea bass and rock fish.

Salmon and steelhead fishing along the Rogue river. Rogue Wilderness has specialized in drift boat fishing since 1970. Experienced in a variety fishing techniques including fly fishing, spin casting, bait bouncing, and pulling plugs. Trips are 3-4 days and cover 50 miles.
