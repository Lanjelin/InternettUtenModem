Airfare Specials

Airfare Specials are airfare deals that are announced by the airlines. There are a few things to always remember:

  • The airlines could change their fares at any moment. So, just because the airlines say to ticket by a particular date, don't assume that the fare will not go up or down prior to that date.
  • Read all the fine print. In general these fares will be very, very restrictive. The ticket may be non-refundable and the inventory may be very limited.
  • When booking these flights through our direct interface and the price quoted does not match the special price in this section, the reason may be that the fare special is sold out or the conditions of the fare are not met by the data you have entered. In this case fill in the "Notes and Special.." section of the on-line form and a representative from Ultimate World will clarify your situation and, if possible, clear space for you on the flight.
  • Always protect yourself with travel bankruptcy insurance and pay for your airline ticket using a credit card. Check with your credit card company for their policy regarding whether they protect you in the case of an airline bankruptcy.
