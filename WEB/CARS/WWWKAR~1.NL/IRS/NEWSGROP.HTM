You want to know about everything concerning Formula One, a mailing list or a newsgroup is probably the solution. Both ways are intended for discussions, but you will receive more news in a newsgroup. There are a lot more postings in a newsgroup, so you will be more up-to-date. You don't have to subscribe to a newsgroup, but you need software. For more information about setting up a connection to a news server you have to ask your provider or refer to your software documentation. We ordered a list of newsgroups related to motor racing.
