Authentic Autographs
by Artur Marques and Carla Monteiro

Authentic Autographs is presented by Artur Marques and Carla Monteiro in co-operation with the International Racing Site. If you are a fan, but you don't have the driver's autograph, this is the place to be. Just go to the Autographs section by clicking on the menu option in the yellow part of the screen. Choose your favourit driver and print out the autograph.

In the Shots of signing section are pictures available of the moments of signing by the driver or team member.

Send us your contribution by mailing a picture in JPG format to arturmarques@mail.telepac.pt. We accept all autographs of people who have anything to do with the highest level of motorsport. We appreciate it very much if you include a photo taken at the moment of signing the autograph.

All pictures are copyright to Carla Monteiro.
