Source: BBC Ceefax
Date: March 6
Subject: Arrows seat failes to arrive

Damon Hill has suffered an embarrassing start to his racing career with TWR Arrows when the seat for his car failed to arrive in Melbourne. Team technicians hurriedly built him a new one just in case the original fails to arrive ahead of initial practice. "It's one of the most important parts," said technical director Frank Dernie. "We are not sure where it is. Damon cannot race without one, that is for sure. When you make a new one, you never get it right first time."
