Source: BBC Ceefax
Date: March 10
Subject: Hill vows to keep faith with Arrows

Damon Hill has vowed to keep faith with the Arrows team despite his nightmare weekend at the Australian Grand Prix. The reigning world champion, who is on a £4,5m deal with Arrows, failed to start the race after his car broke down on the parade lap in Melbourne. But Hill said: "knew the moment I did not have the opportunity of going with a top team, my life would be difficult. There will be a new champion this season. I know that. But I'm not one to walk away from a difficult situation."
