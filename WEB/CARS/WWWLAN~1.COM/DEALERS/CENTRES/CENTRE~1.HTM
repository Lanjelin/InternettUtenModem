A Land Rover Centre is a unique sort of sport utility dealership dedicated exclusively to the sale of four-wheel drive vehicles. Unlike some traditional dealerships, Centres are staffed by teams of sport utility experts dedicated to providing their customers with an adventurous, fun, personalized four-wheel drive vehicle buying experience.

Each showroom features a multi-media Adventure Centre and complete displays of Land Rover Vehicle Kit, vehicle accessories and optional equipment for recreational use such as roof racks, ski racks, auxiliary lights, and brush guards. Centres also offer a range of boots, clothing, and other adventure wear as part of a Land Rover Gear line of merchandise.

Despite the fact that fewer than ten percent of most other sport utilities ever leave the pavement, Centres encourage their customers to learn four-wheel drive skills and techniques and to explore local off-road areas. To this end, most Centres feature a small off-road test track and customers are invited to participate in regular off-road driving clinics.

From the Land Rover dealer locator, Land Rover Centres are identified by an asterisk next to the dealer's name.
