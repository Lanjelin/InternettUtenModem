LAND ROVER REPORTS BEST AUGUST SALES EVER


Discovery sales up 53% over August '95

Lanham, MD (9/4/96) - Land Rover North America, Inc. posted 2,140 sales in August for its line of premium British-built four-wheel drive vehicles. Discovery, which has a base price below $30,000, posted 1,577 sales for the month, a 53 percent increase over a year ago. Year-to-date total for all Land Rover models is 14,521, 21.4 percent ahead of the 11,957 tally for the same period in '95.

Charles R. Hughes, president, Land Rover North America, said, "A lease program offering free maintenance, no security deposit, and the first month's payment paid for the customer has proved to be very attractive to Discovery buyers. The program continues through September 30."
