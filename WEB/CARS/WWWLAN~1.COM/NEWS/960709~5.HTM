JOHN CUMMINGS, LRNA DEMONSTRATION COURSE ARCHITECT AND BUILDER


Art and engineering combine in unique design

Lanham, MD (7/9/96) -- John Cummings, 46, is the creator of the quarter-mile "Land Rover Demonstration Course at Lanham Creek." President of Houston-based Outside Associates, Inc., he has designed unique landscapes in this country and abroad on a variety of projects that display his talent for innovative design and impeccable construction. In addition to the Lanham, Maryland, facility, Mr. Cummings is to date responsible for six smaller-scale demonstration courses at Land Rover Centres, retailers that focus exclusively on four-wheel drive vehicles as well as related products and accessories. Five additional Cummings-designed courses are now in various stages of design or construction.

Cummings' credentials include nine major projects completed for EuroDisney Resorts in Marne la Vallée, France over a period of two years as well as three Walt Disney World facilities in Orlando. The range of his imagination is reflected in his designs for theme-based complexes ranging from the Newport Hotel, Sequoia Lodge, Hotel New York and Hotel Santa Fe in France to Disney's Caribbean Beach Resort in Florida. His work is also well known in Texas, where his talents are evident at Galveston's Moody Gardens and Houston's Chevron Tower, 1600 Smith Tower, Geosource Plaza at The Galleria, and the Lakes at 610 among other projects. He has also been involved in golf course and country club design and construction as well as the Hurricane Andrew relief effort.

A Houston native, Mr. Cummings attended Stephen F. Austin State University, where he majored in liberal arts. A resident of Houston, he is married and the father of two daughters.
