MANY WILDLIFE SPECIES INHABIT THE LAND ROVER DEMONSTRATION COURSE AT LANHAM CREEK (continued)


Although small, this wetland provides habitat to a wide range of plants and animals and offers a green oasis to migrating birds in an increasingly urban area. To date, the have been 13 mammals observed, 37 birds, nine reptiles and amphibians, nine butterflies, two dragonflies, and 94 varieties of plants. The plant diversity is large and offers a variety of berry, nut and forage plants especially conducive to wildlife as demonstrated by the following list.
