LAND ROVER REPORTS BEST NOVEMBER SALES EVER

Year-to-date sales up 21% for all models, eclipsing previous full year record


Lanham, MD (12/3/96) -- Land Rover North America, Inc. posted 2,172 sales during its best November, 3.1 percent ahead of the 2,107 posted in 1995, the previous best November. The company's line of premium four-wheel drive vehicles was led by Discovery, which posted 1,384 sales for the month, a 10.5 percent increase over a year ago. Year-to-date total for all Land Rover models is 20,772, 21 percent ahead of the 17,170 tally for the same period in '95, and surpassing the 1995 full year sales record of 20,026.

Charles R. Hughes, president, Land Rover North America, said, "We've set a new one-year sales record with our biggest sales month yet to go. Consumers are enthusiastically responding to the 1997 model Land Rovers, the best in the company's history, and our thirty-nine exclusive Land Rover Centres, which are a whole new automotive buying experience. All of our 100 dealers are well-stocked for December, which is traditionally our best sales month as buyers look for the safety and security of four-wheel drive during the winter snow season."

November also saw a new one-month sales record for the new Range Rover 4.0 SE and 4.6 HSE models, with 781 units sold.
