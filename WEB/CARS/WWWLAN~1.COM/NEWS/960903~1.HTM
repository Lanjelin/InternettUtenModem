LAND ROVER SPORTS PACKAGE OFFERS VALUE IN BLACK AND WHITE


Limited Number Available For Discovery Models

A new Sports Value Package for black or white 1996 Discovery sport utility vehicles is being offered by Land Rover North America, Inc. while supplies last.

The sporty trim adds esthetic and functional appeal and includes items not previously offered. With only 500 packages available nationwide, they promise distinction as well as a cost savings of $820 for a white Discovery and $1225 for the premium Beluga Black - as compared to separate list prices for the items in the package.

Included in the Sports Value Package are: an all-new "A" design-front brush bar, running boards, Land Rover driving lights with harness, a black cover for the Discovery's externally mounted spare tire and new hefty, black, spring-fastened wheel arch and side moldings. The brush bar is integrated with the supplemental restraint system and does not impede operation of the air bags.

The Sports Value Package is all black for the Alpine White Discovery and substitutes a stainless steel brush bar and stainless steel running boards for the Beluga Black Discovery.

For more information about the Sports Value Package or about Range Rovers as well as Discovery sport utility vehicles, call 1-800 FINE 4WD to locate the closest Land Rover dealer.
