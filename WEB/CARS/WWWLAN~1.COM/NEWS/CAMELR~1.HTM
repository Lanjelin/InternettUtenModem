CAMEL TROPHY '96 FINAL RESULTS

BORNEO (4/22/96) -- Jen O'Brien called from Borneo late Monday morning, April 22, with the excellent news that the '96 US Camel Trophy team of Fred Hoess and Ken Cameron has finished:


- 2nd overall for the Camel Trophy Award

- 3rd for the Team Spirit Award

- 7th for the Special Tasks Award

- 9th in the Land Rover Award standings.


We'll update you with details as soon as they're available.
