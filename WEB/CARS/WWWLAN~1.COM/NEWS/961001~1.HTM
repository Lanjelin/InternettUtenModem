LAND ROVER REPORTS SECOND BEST MONTH EVER


Discovery sales up 94.7% over September '95

Lanham, MD (10/01/96) - Land Rover North America, Inc. posted 2,202 sales in September for its line of premium British-built four-wheel-drive vehicles. Discovery, which has a base price below $30,000, posted 1,674 sales for the month, a 94.7 percent increase over a year ago. Year-to-date total for all Land Rover models is 16,723, 24.9 percent ahead of the 13,388 tally for the same period in '95. It was the second best sales month ever, exceeded only in December 1995.

Charles R. Hughes, president, Land Rover North America, said, "We are heading into our strongest selling period on the crest of record sales and with the best supply of Land Rover products we've ever had."
