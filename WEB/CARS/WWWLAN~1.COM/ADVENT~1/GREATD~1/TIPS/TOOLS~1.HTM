Tools & Spares


Toolbox:
Plastic toolboxes are best because they don't rattle and don't make marks on other gear. An alternative would be a soft, canvas bag. Either way, secure that tool carrier from moving around.

Jumper Cables:
An absolute necessity. Cables come in various lengths, but longer ones are more versatile.

Tire Gauge:
Dial type gauges are the most accurate.

Air Compressor:
A small, portable compressor that operates from the vehicles electrical system can be a valuable asset. Some models plug into the cigar lighter (remember the engine needs to be on for this socket to be powered), while others have leads that clip directly to the battery.

Sledge Hammer:
A five pound sledge may be the only tool that can "persuade" a bent piece of metal back into place.

Combination Wrenches:
A set of combination wrenches from 8 mm to 19 mm is a vital part of any tool kit.

Adjustable Wrench, 10 - 12 Inch:
Otherwise known as a "Crescent" wrench, this big boy is useful with large nuts and bolts and takes the place of several large combination wrenches.

Screwdrivers:
A mix of a few sizes of standard and Phillips screwdrivers. The Phillips should be of the "posi - drive" type. One large standard screwdriver, 12 - 15 inches long can be useful as a pry bar .

Pliers:
Standard pliers, a 6 inch vice grip, and a large pair of "channel lock" type pliers.

Socket Set:
8 - 19 mm, including a spark Plug socket (21 mm), 3, 6, and 12 inch extensions, and a swivel.

Miscellaneous:
Cold chisel, small punch, large drift, hacksaw (or hack saw blade with one end taped), electrical test light (if you know how to use it), one pound ball - peels hammer, and flashlight.

Welder:
Improvised welding can be accomplished by hooking two or three batteries series with jumper cables and using light welding rod.
Spares List
  • baling wire
  • fuses (including air suspension maxi fuse)
  • belts (used OK)
  • radiator stop leak
  • motor oil and automatic transmission fluid, 1 quart each
  • duct tape and electrical tape
  • ten feet of electrical wire
  • radiator hoses (used OK) and/or hose repair kit
  • a few misc. nuts and bolts appropriate to the vehicle
  • owners manual (service manual)
  • brake fluid, 1 pint Castrol LMA
  • radial tire repair kit or tire repair in a can
