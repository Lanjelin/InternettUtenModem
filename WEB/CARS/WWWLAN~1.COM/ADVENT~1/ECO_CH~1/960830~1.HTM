Day 6 - Team Land Rover on the Mountain Bike Course

Team Land Rover stops for a quick shot along the mountain bike course.


One of the many magnificent views of British Columbia along the race route.


Team Land Rover completes the first of two mountain biking legs of the Eco-Challenge course. After a twenty-one hour canoe trip, they packed their gear and biked 55 miles, first in blistering heat and then in rain, finishing twenty-four hours later.

Brandywine Falls in Garibaldi Provincial Park along the final mountain biking section of the race.
