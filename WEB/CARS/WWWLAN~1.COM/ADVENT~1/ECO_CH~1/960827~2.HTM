1 pm, Tuesday, August 27: Front-runner Team EcoInternet finishes a mountain biking section of the race, and is faced with a problem solving task. Before completing this leg, they must get themselves, bikes, and all gear across this rushing river. The team is given two canoes which are on the opposite shore. Assistants may deliver them and help the five team members move the gear. The team of four men and one woman had no problem with this exercise. After a short rest, they set off on another multi-day glacier trek.
