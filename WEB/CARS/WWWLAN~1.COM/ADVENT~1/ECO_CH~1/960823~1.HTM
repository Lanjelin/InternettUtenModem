8/23/96 - Three hundred seventy-five Eco-Challenge contestants from nine countries gather before the start of the world's greatest adventure race in British Columbia. Teams of five adventurers, men and women combined will race more than 300 miles non-stop canoeing, mountain biking, white water rafting, horseback riding and glacier trekking. Racers range in age from 18 to 73. Members of Team Land Rover from Aspen, Colorado include Jack Crawford, Amy Capron, Peter Vorhees, Darcey Angelo, and Charlie MacArthur.
