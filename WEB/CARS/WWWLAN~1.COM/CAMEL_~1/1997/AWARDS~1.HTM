Participants on the event compete for four trophies: The Camel Trophy itself, the Special Tasks Award, the Team Spirit Award and the Land Rover Driving Award. While securing the main trophy is undeniably every participant's goal, the greatest satisfaction often comes from winning the Team Spirit Award, as this captures the essence of Camel Trophy.

In competition for Special Task points, teams must complete a variety of scored tasks during grueling hours of continuous activity. Assessment for the Team Spirit Award is ongoing throughout the event and is based on the votes cast by the participants themselves. It is not enough to be a skillful driver and navigator on Camel Trophy; participants soon realize they must help others so that the common goal can be achieved, even to the detriment of their own individual performance.

The scores from the Special Task and the Team Spirit Awards are then combined to produce the overall winning team - the recipient of the coveted Camel Trophy - while the Land Rover Driving Award is presented to the team with the best scores in the vehicle-based Special Tasks.
