To prepare a vehicle for expeditions and extreme duty, Land Rover engineers have created specialized accessories they fondly refer to as "Vehicle Kit." Land Rover Vehicle Kit is designed and built to the same rigid standards as the vehicles themselves. Rigorously proven Vehicle Kit can be tailored to each owner's individual needs for off-road challenges and outdoor activities. Designed specifically for Discovery, each item has been tested and proven suitable for every extreme.
