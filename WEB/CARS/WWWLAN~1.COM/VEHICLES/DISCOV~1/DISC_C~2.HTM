"Sport" and "utility" are simply nowhere as well represented as they are in the 1997 Land Rover Discovery. You don't have to chose between a definitive family sport-utility vehicle and a bona-fide rocks-and-mud off-roader. Discovery is both.

Discovery blends the rugged authenticity of the Defender 90 and the refined comfort and convenience of the Range Rover. For many owners, it is the perfect balance of travel comfort, go-anywhere capability and safety engineering... at a price comparable to that of an ordinary sport-utility vehicle.

  an a vehicle so capable at exploring the four corners of the globe be equally well suited for every-day transportation? Resoundingly, yes! After all, practicality and utility are valued as much by families as by explorers. Unlike mere passenger cars, Discovery has the ability to take everything in its stride, whether running errands on a summer morning or getting home in the blizzard of '97. Is Discovery over-engineered? Perhaps, but just think of the confidence you'll have when the weather turns nasty or the road turns to gravel.
No other vehicle has the driving feel of a Discovery. It imparts a feeling of solidity and strength, a sense of confidence and supreme capability. And rightly so. Discovery is built to give you the perfect balance of practicality and adventure.

For a weekend shopping spree, Discovery gives you plenty of cargo room and the option of loading your new acquisitions on the built-in roof rack system. For hauling a trailer with your mountain bikes, snowmobiles or horses, few vehicles are as well equipped.

Pick up the kids at the soccer field? No problem with Discovery's available rear jump seats.
Find a secluded picnic spot way up in the mountains? Truly all in a day's work. Work or play, errands or recreation, it's hard to imagine an outdoor activity of any kind that cannot be enhanced with a Land Rover Discovery.

What sets Discovery apart in a world of pretenders is that it is genuine -- not a truck with rear seats added or a car raised on its suspension, but a purpose-built, authentic vehicle that combines sport and utility like no other.
