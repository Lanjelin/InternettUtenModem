Imagine a vehicle that can go virtually anywhere. One that can carry seven people. One that is powerful yet nimble, comfortable yet eager to travel along life's roads. Imagine how that vehicle would fit into your active lifestyle, the adventures it might invite.

 
 
hen taking the road less traveled becomes commonplace, perhaps the answer is no road at all. Imagine yourself there, and Discovery can accomplish the task. A distant mountain glen. At the base of the waterfall. Among the dunes or high on a ridge with a view out to the horizon. Whatever your idea of adventure is, Discovery gives you the freedom to get there. Deal with the mundane necessities of everyday life in comfort. But when adventure calls, Discovery lets you follow the path to wherever you choose.

The Land Rover Discovery was created by people with just such experiences in mind. It is capable, comfortable, reliable and spirited; a perfect conveyance for your family and a go-anywhere machine.

True to its heritage, Discovery is designed for the most difficult driving situations. Its combination of ample ground clearance, and short front and rear overhangs allows it to go places unknown to lesser vehicles. With its high-torque V-8 engine and permanently engaged four-wheel-drive system, tested and proven in the toughest conditions, this is a vehicle that openly asks for the road less traveled -- or no road at all.

Discovery's ability to travel to remote places, and its comfort and style make it perfect for any family getaway. Yet Discovery is so capable that you may find yourself seeking out more challenging destinations, or simply a different way to get to the ordinary.

As adept as the Discovery has proven itself to be at off-road challenges, it is an equally qualified partner in less arduous adventures.
The same features that have made it so successful in equatorial jungles or on the frozen tundra are qualities everyone can appreciate while going for a night out on the town, or driving to a deep-woods cabin.
