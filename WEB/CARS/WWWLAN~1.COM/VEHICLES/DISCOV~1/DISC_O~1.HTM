These reflect the Land Rover philosophy and lifestyle in even more detail, with displays of adventure artifacts and a natural ambience. Employees dress in "Land Rover Gear", and there is a casual atmosphere throughout. In addition, most Centres feature off-road test tracks on-site to demonstrate the amazing capabilities of Land Rover vehicles.

Land Rover ownership means more than the thrill of owning a highly capable vehicle. In many ways, your purchase or lease of a Discovery is your entree into an exclusive club. From specialty clothing to adventure vacations, Land Rover ownership both accents and expands your lifestyle.

For example, Land Rover Retailers frequently sponsor "Wheels" off-road events to initiate those new to off-road driving and hone the skills of the proficient. For owners who yearn for more exotic venues, Land Rover North America, Inc. offers four-wheel-drive "Adventures" in North America and abroad.

Land Rover Retailers also offer a full range of active wear and accessories called "Land Rover Gear". Like Land Rover vehicles, this line of men's and women's merchandise is fit-for-purpose and has been tested around the world. Whether you're fly-fishing, camping, attending a polo match or just relaxing, Land Rover Gear offers distinctive style and versatility.

 
The care Land Rover takes in manufacturing and delivering each vehicle reflects a continuing commitment that extends far beyond the showroom. Discovery owners have the peace of mind that comes with a comprehensive limited warranty that stretches for 3 years/42,000 miles (70,000 km), and a corrosion perforation limited warranty of 6 years/unlimited mileage.

In addition, every new Discovery comes with a 24-hour-per-day, 365 day-per-year Land Rover Road RecoverySM service to attend to the unforeseen. Should you run out of fuel, get a flat tire or in the unlikely event of a breakdown, a hotline call will dispatch a service vehicle to fix the problem on the spot or take you to the nearest authorized dealer or repair facility. Trip interruption benefits and computerized trip routing are also included.

To attend to the needs of your vehicle, Land Rover Retailers are equipped with special tools and diagnostic equipment. In addition, retailers stock an inventory of genuine, guaranteed Land Rover parts, and employ only high-caliber technical personnel, many of whom were trained at the factory in England.
