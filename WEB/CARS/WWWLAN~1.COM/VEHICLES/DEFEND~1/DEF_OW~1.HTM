Land Rover Ownership is more than the thrill of owning a highly capable sport-utility vehicle.

In many ways, your purchase of a Defender is your entrée into an exclusive club. From specialty clothing -- "Land Rover Gear" -- to adventure vacations, Land Rover ownership accents and expands your lifestyle.

For example, Land Rover Retailers frequently sponsor "Wheels" events to initiate those new to off-road driving or hone the skills of the proficient. For those who yearn for more exotic venues, Land Rover North America, Inc. offers four-wheel-drive "Adventures" in North America and abroad.

And Defender 90 owners have the backing of a comprehensive limited warranty and 24-hour Road RecoverySM service.


Defender's exceptional off-road abilities bring clear and definite environmental responsibilities.

Land Rover strongly urges drivers to keep four-wheeling a positive recreational pursuit. To that end, Land Rover
is a founding member of and fully supports the U.S. Forest Service's Tread Lightly! program. This program is dedicated to protecting public and private lands for future generations. Being environmentally responsible is not difficult, but it does take a conscious effort. Please join our efforts to protect the land for everyone's future enjoyment.
