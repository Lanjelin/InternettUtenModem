4.6 HSE : 4.6 HSE with Kensington Interior : 4.6 HSE Vitesse

For those who require Range Rover's extraordinary permanent four-wheel-drive system but desire the performance and handling feel of a European sport sedan, Land Rover has created the limited-production Range Rover 4.6 HSE. With a longer-stroke 4.6-liter V-8 engine, the 4.6 HSE offers a very capable 225 horsepower and 280 pound-feet of torque.

Handling improvements perfectly match the new engine's capabilities. The Range Rover 4.6 HSE
is fitted with special 18-inch alloy wheels and 55-series tires - the only sport-utility vehicle to feature such low-profile tires. These dual-purpose tires offer excellent on- and off-road traction while increasing handling response and subjective feel.

With subtle custom details that set it apart, the Range Rover 4.6 HSE is offered with exclusive Ash Black or Lightstone leather trim, including a leather-clad shift handle. A choice of six high-gloss exterior finishes for 1997 includes sporty Monza Red and dramatic AA Yellow (both limited in availability), plus, appropriately, traditional British Racing Green.

4.6 HSE : 4.6 HSE with Kensington Interior : 4.6 HSE Vitesse

The idea behind the Kensington is seemingly quite simple. Start with the most exclusive model of the most exceptional four-wheel-drive vehicle available - the Range Rover 4.6 HSE. Then make it even more sumptuous, even more exclusive and add an even more impressive audio system. The results are sublime. The Range Rover 4.6 HSE with The Kensington Interior is truly the crème de la crème.

The standard Lightstone Leather interior includes contrasting Ash Black piping on the seats. Leather trim is used lavishly throughout the interior, including on the center console and cubby box lid. Rich burl walnut trim adds warmth and luster to a larger area of the center console, including the fascia surrounding the dual-zone climate control and the audio system. Even the cupholders are accented in walnut.

The audio system bears further elaboration. This exclusive harman/kardon premium sound system with 10 power amplifiers has been tuned specifically to the Range Rover's interior. With an AM/FM stereo receiver, cassette player and six-disc CD auto-changer, this 300-watt, 12-speaker system is an auidiophile's delight.

The optional, fully integrated, hands-free cellular telephone works with the audio system, muting audio programs when the phone is in use.

But don't forget that all of this is in addition to the extraordinary features and attributes of the Range Rover 4.6 HSE. With permanent four-wheel drive, a 225 horsepower V8 engine and 18-inch alloy wheels, the 4.6 HSE offers the performance of a European sports sedan with the legendary off-road capabilities of a Land Rover.

The limited-edition Kensington Interior is simply the icing on the cake.

The Kensington Interior Special Edition Features

  • Exclusive color and upholstery - Beluga Black or British Racing Green exterior with Lightstone Leather interior featuring Ash Black piping.
  • Hand-stitched, leather-trimmed center console with padded leather cubby box lid and trim.
  • Genuine burl walnut trim on center console surfaces and climate-control surround, audio surround, window switch plate, rear ashtray lid, cupholders and vent surround.
  • Premium harman/kardon AM/FM/cassette/CD 300-watt audio system with full parametric equalization to enhance sound quality and 12 speakers, including an active dual-driver subwoofer.
  • An integrated, hands free cellular telephone with roof-mounted antenna and automatic audio-system mute is available through your Land Rover Retailer.
  • Limited edition - only 750 vehicles will be produced.

4.6 HSE : 4.6 HSE with Kensington Interior : 4.6 HSE Vitesse

Sometimes subtlety is not the recommended modus operandi. If you'd like your Range Rover 4.6 HSE to make even more of a statement, then the limited-edition Vitesse is for you.

Offered in "high impact" exterior pallettes of AA Yellow or Monza Red, the Vitesse includes body-color mirrors and lower grille valance. A touch of brightwork is added to the interior with the chrome door handles.

Also making a statement - at your desired volume - is a premium harman/kardon audio system, featuring 10 power amplifiers tuned specifically to the Range Rover's interior. This 300-watt, 12 speaker system, with AM/FM stereo receiver, cassette player and six-disc CD auto-changer, is an audiophile's delight.

With its powerful 4.6-liter V8 engine and massive 18-inch tires, the Range Rover 4.6 HSE Vitesse is certain to make an impact wherever you go.
