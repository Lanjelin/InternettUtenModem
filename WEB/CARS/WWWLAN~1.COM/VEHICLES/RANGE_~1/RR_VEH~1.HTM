No other manufacturer has a range of vehicles that are as distinctive and capable as Land Rover's. For almost 50 years, Land Rover has produced rugged, purpose-built vehicles that have become the standard for comfortable and reliable off-road travel. To prepare a vehicle for expeditions (or to customize a Land Rover to better match your lifestyle), Land Rover designers and engineers have created a series of specialized accessories referred to as "Vehicle Kit."

Over the years, Land Rover vehicles have been equipped for global expeditions, military service, country estates and active lifestyles. Land Rover Vehicle Kit is designed and built to the same rigid quality and durability standards as the legendary vehicles themselves.

For Range Rover, intelligent and rigorously proven Vehicle Kit can tailor your vehicle to a variety of specific needs. Each item of Land Rover Vehicle Kit, designed specifically for the Range Rover 4.0 SE and the limited-edition Range Rover 4.6 HSE, has been tested, both on- and off-road, to be suitable for use in every extreme.

Land Rover Vehicle Kit for Range Rover can be used to prepare a vehicle for adventures as exciting as an African safari or as peaceful as the solitude of your favorite trout stream.
