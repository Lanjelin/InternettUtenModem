Few vehicles are as adept at finding adventure as Range Rover. It has been designed and engineered for so much more than simple highway travel. Its powerful V-8 engine, permanent four-wheel drive and electronically adjustable air suspension system adapt the vehicle perfectly to every terrain. Its comfortable yet versatile interior provides a soothing environment for hours of interstate travel, or the perfect place to stow your gear while fishing in a remote trout stream. It is truly a vehicle for people who wish to enjoy all that life offers.

  
ange Rover's incredible capabilities give you extraordinary freedom to explore the world. In extreme conditions, the abilities of its sophisticated permanent four-wheel-drive system become even more apparent. With three differentials, a viscous coupling unit and electronic traction control, the highly refined Range Rover can leave conventional vehicles back in civilization.
  Range Rover's unmatched off-road prowess includes the ability to wade through 20 inches of water. Ground clearance under the front differential is 8.4 inches, while under the frame, 11 inches of clearance help clear obstacles that would stop lesser vehicles.

On-road towing capacity is 7,700 pounds (in Low Range), making Range Rover the perfect companion for a variety of outdoor activities. And factory and real-world testing have confirmed an amazing ability to operate at ascending and descending angles as steep as 45 degrees while leaning at angles as dramatic as 35 degrees.
