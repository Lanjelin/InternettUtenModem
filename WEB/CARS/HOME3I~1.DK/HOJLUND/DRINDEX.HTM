Denmark offers a range of high-quality rallies of international standard.

Tarmac events, inernational rules, Danish cosiness and competitors of an international level is the brief description of our offer for you.

All event are national events. This means you only need to hold a national EC-license to join.

To make your choice please find the necessary information about all six Danish Championship events and four great rallysprints below. Every organizer has special offers for alle foreign competitors.

Very welcome to rallying in Denmark !

  • Date: 11.-12. April 97
  • Place: Hobro
  • Location: Northern Jutland
  • Total km / stages: Total 400 km incl. 135 km SS with 85% asfalt. 5 different stages all run twice

Description:

Scrutineering Friday evening. Rally Saturday at 10-18 H and prizegiving at 22 H. Rally-HQ at Hobro Sports Centre.

Totally new stages for this year incl. one fine forest-stage in Rold Skov.

Papers and recce from 22/3 - 11/4.

Entry fee:

For foreign competitors an entry fee of DKR 500,- incl. overnight stay from Friday - Sunday.
