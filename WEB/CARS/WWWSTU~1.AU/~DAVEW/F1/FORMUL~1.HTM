Retrospective 1989-1996

A recent post to rec.autos.sport.f1 led me to produce this. It was the list of winners by driver, team and engine for the last 5 years, with the figures aggregated eg. Hill 21 wins .... Prost (nee Ligier) 1 win. I thought this was a good idea, but I wanted it broken down more, which led to these tables of wins, poles and fastest laps for drivers and constructors season by season. It allows trends in a team or driver's performance to be seen eg. Hill's rise in races won, the fall of McLaren as a force in F1. Hope you find them useful too.
