TV Interviews

These are transcripts of interview with Michael that I have seen. 3 of these have only been seen on Australian television (as far as I know), so they will be new to you. I have deliberately not corrected any errors made during the interviews, so don't say "Michael won 2 races for Mercedes" because I know that, but I decided to leave them as they were presented for accuracy (to what was broadcast). Enjoy!
