Sounds

Below are a couple of sound bites from Michael.

These are from Mild Seven Benetton Renault, at the start of 1995. In the English file, Michael says:

"Hello. I'm Michael Schumacher, the first driver of the MILD SEVEN BENETTON RENAULT Team. And my first target is to win the 1995 World Championship. Thank you."

Download English .au file, (85 K)
In the German file, Michael says:
"Hello, Ich bin Michael Schumacher von MILD SEVEN BENETTON RENAULT Team und mein erstes Ziel wird sein, in diesem Jahre 1995 die Weltmeistershaft zu gewinnen. Vielen Dank."

which apparently translates to:

"Hello, I am Michael Schumacher of MILD SEVEN BENETTON RENAULT Team and my first aim is to be the World Champion in this year. Thank you."
