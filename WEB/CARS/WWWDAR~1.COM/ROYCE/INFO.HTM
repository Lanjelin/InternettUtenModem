Information Service

We may be able to help you find the the full history of your Rolls-Royce or Bentley motor car.

Copies of original build sheets can be obtained showing among other things, who the car was built for, what specifications were asked for, original colour scheme, type of tyres and battery, all the tests that were done on the car, etc, etc.

If the vehicle was registered in the United Kingdom, we may be able to supply a copy of the original "Logbook" showing all the previous owners up until the time when the logbook was replaced with the new "V5" in the 1970's

If you are interested in these items or any other information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
