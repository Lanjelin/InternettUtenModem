Formula One results, Free Practise, Qualifying, Warm-up and Race, it's all here. The time when a session start is almost everytime the same, with a major exception of Monaco, when Free Practise is on Thursday before the Grand Prix on Sunday. A normal Grand Prix has two Free Practice sessions on Friday (11h - 12h and 13h - 14h local time), two Free Practise sessions on Saturday (11h - 11h45 and 12h15 - 13h local time). One Qualifying session on Saturday (13h - 14h local time) and a Warmup on Sunday (09h30 - 10h local time). The Race is on Sunday (14h00 - 16h00 local time).
