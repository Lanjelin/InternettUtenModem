Source: RTL Television
Date: March 1
Subject: 49% believes in Schumacher
Translated from German

According to an inquiry of the Public Opinion institute Forsa, agrees about half of the German that Michael Schumacher becomes world champion this season. The inquiry instructed by RTL has as result that 49% of the people chooses Michael Schumacher has world champion 1997. After Schumacher is Canadian Jacques Villeneuve (15%) and Heinz-Harald Frentzen (8%).
