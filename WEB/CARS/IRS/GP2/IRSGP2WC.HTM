IRS Grand prix 2 World Championship 1997: How does it work?

They came, they saw... and they were conquered. This championship is a competition were a lot of people were waiting for... Well, here it is! The championship has 12 races over 1997, from March to October. Check out the Championship rules to get a better view on the Championship. You can earn points to finish within the first 50 competitors! System is described in the Championship rules section.

Send your 'Practice on any Circuit' Hot Lap on the Wednesday till Saturday (before a Grand Prix on Sunday). Just pack the file (e.g. with WinZip) and send it to duggyx@worldaccess.nl. The rankings will be published on Sunday.

There are two different rankings. Rankings for digital and analog control method users. If you drive with a Thrustmaster T2 you are using an analog control method. If you are using a Thrustmaster T1 or Keyboard you are using a digital control method. Points are given to all competitors based on the general rankings. For example:
