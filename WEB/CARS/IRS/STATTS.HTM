Some major statistics of the International Racing Site are scheduled below. For the latest statistics, counted by NedStat, click here. Statistics below are counted in our first month online (at the new address (http://www.kart.nl/irs), and are all reliable (it can never be less). When you visit the International Racing Site your browser will load an image, which is on top of the menu list at the left side of your screen, and count for one visitor one hit. It is possible that the image is in cache memory of your browser, so your visit will not be counted. That means that most of the time there are more visitors as shown below.
