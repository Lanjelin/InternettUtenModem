All changes made on the International Racing Site are shown on this page. The online version of the International Racing Site is version 4.0. There were lay-out changes in version 2.0 and 4.0. Version 3.0 was an improvement on 2.0 because of the low-space we had available. Currently, with help of our sponsors, we have more space available, and with this space we had the possibility to improve the lay-out again. We started the International Racing Site on 20 March 1996 without any announcement in April. So the page was available for public on 28 April 1996. Let's see what the latest changes are:
Hot Laps and News articles will be updated every day. These are not worth to be added on this page, otherwise it will be a very long list.
9 March 1997 - Warm-up and Race results added, standings 97 updated, results page updated.
8 March 1997 - Hard work these days, with a huge amount of visitors (1167 on Friday March 7). No questions or mailings, but we had to keep the site up-to-date, as we do always. We also updated the session results, but this is regular, so we won't add these changes to this list.
