Airports in Oslo

Fornebu (appr. 20 min. by car/bus) from the town centre. Norway's main airport for international and domestic flights.
GA-Terminalen this terminal facility uses the same runways as Fornebu Airport, and is used by private aircraft and airline companies with smaller planes. The terminal is very close by the main terminal of Fornebu.
