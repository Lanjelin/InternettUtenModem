The Norwegian Hotel in the centre of Oslo

Welcome to a hotel with distinctive Norwegian features right in the centre of Oslo. We are just around the corner from the main street: Karl Johans gate, an very close to everything worth seing in Oslo.

When you travel you shall enjoy yourself

We who work here are proud of our history, nearly 100 years with norwegian culture and tradition. Best Western Hotell Bondeheimen is an unusual bigcity-hotel, where we want you to feel welcome. A guest is a friend! You will notice this if you stay with us.

Norwegian design is at ist best here at Hotell Bondeheimen, with warm colors and beautiful pinewood furniture. We offer 76 well equipped rooms, all modern and comfortable. More than half of them are non-smoker. Our sauna and sun-bed is situated at the fifth floor. If you need a conference-room, we have the facilities for 25 persons.

We have been loooking after Norwegian culinary traditions for nearly 100 years. At the Kaffistova you will be served home-made, traditional Norwegian food. We bake all our cakes and bread ourselves, and have a versatile, varied menu. Welcome to a good meal in the Kaffistova!

Use Best Western Hoteles bonuscard; "Gold Crown Card". We will give you bonus from the first night you stay, and on all your buys with us!

Welcome to Oslo! We hope you wil have a pleasant stay at the Best Wetsern Hotell Bondeheimen.
