Conferences

The conference facilities are furnished and equipped to allow for smooth and effective meetings and conferences, both big and small.

Our conference personelle is always on hand to take care of your individual needs from arrival to departure. Our ambitions extend beyond following up on our guests and providing for their comfort. We like to give you a surprise or two as well; one day you may get fresh waffles, some other day it may be Danish pastry. For your health fresh fruit is always available at the conference facilities, as is ice-cold drinking water and coffee & tea.

In the surrounding area you will find a rich and varied mixture of sights and sports facilities, for your rest and recreation, or as a surprise for your guests.

An 18-hole international golf course is only 10 minutes away.

Getting to and from the hotel is easy. Whether you travel by bus, train, or your own car it is only a 10 minute journey from downtown Oslo. There are 3000 parking spaces by the hotel, 1500 of which are indoors.
