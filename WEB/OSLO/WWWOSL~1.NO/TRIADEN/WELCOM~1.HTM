Welcome!

Our most important task is to make sure our guests have an enjoyable and exciting stay in a colourful environment. "Flexibility and hospitality" is our motto.

We welcome you to a bright, airy and friendly atmosphere, with some very special touches to the interior decoration and lots of live green plants and trees.
The glass-covered atrium-lobby of the hotel is a virtual oasis, surrounded by Gildestuen Restaurant, Sydvesten Piano Bar, Vasshjulet Dancing, and Uthuset Pub with its attractive summer terrace.

 
Facilities

The hotel offers 155 varied rooms and suites. Bathroom facilities include floor-heating, hair drier, and bath-tubs with shower. The hotel is equipped with modern telephone- and information-systems which eases your day, whether you are here for business or pleasure.

 
When you want to hold that special party, Triaden Hotel can cater to your every need. We arrange weddings, confirmations, banquets, and receptions. Or perhaps you would like an exotic theme-party for that special occasion? We make it just the way you want it.

Via the hotel lobby you have direct access to Triaden shopping mall, which has 75 stores, a movie theatre, and a library. Triaden Sports center is a great place for working out. It also has squash courts, solariums, and saunas. You can enter directly from the hotel lobby.

 
 
For your safety the hotel is equipped with the most modern fire-warning systems, with a direct connection to the fire brigade.
