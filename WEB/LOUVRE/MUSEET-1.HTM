Le temps d'un musée

Le temps d'un musée, is history in a nutshell: 200 years in 70 minutes.
A rich narrative of the changes and developments of the Louvre museum. The development of its conception and in its different vocations.
Le temps d'un musée, is an invitation to travel through the time of the artists and in the space of the works. But it is also a living history, peopled with men and women, some of whom are well known (Tardi is one of them), others anonymous, who recount what enraptures them and what they regret.
Le temps d'un musée, is a visit which can be relived and through which it is possible to acquire a wealth of collective and personal impressions.
