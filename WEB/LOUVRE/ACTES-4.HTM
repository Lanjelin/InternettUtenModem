Conference papers and lecture notes


The conferences and certain lecture series that have taken place in the Louvre's Auditorium are jointly edited and published in the collection Louvre: conférences et colloques, on sale in the bookshop.

All the events are also recorded on audio cassette and are available for hire from the multimedia library (on the first floor of the Accueil des groupes area, tel. (33-1) 40 20 52 80).
