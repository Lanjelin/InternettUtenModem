Where to find the Publications

You can purchase printed and audiovisual publications, as well as gifts, casts, jewellery etc. at the Museum Bookshop in the hall Napoléon, under the Pyramid. The shop is open from 9.30 a.m. to 9.45 p.m. every day except Tuesdays.
