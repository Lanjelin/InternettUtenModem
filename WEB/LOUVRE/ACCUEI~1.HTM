Reception Area under the Pyramid


Information Desk

Open every day except Tuesdays, from 9 a.m. to 9.45 p.m. Tel.: (33-1) 40 20 53 17.


Information Screens

A Public Information System, made up of 14 video screens located in the hall Napoléon, provides information about the location of collections within the museum, the daily opening and closing times of the galleries and the museum's cultural activities programme.


Vestiaires et bagageries gratuits

All belongings deposited in the cloakrooms must be collected the same day.
Money, cheque books, credit cards, valuable objects, furs, food and drinks are not accepted.
An area for changing babies is at your disposal; pushchairs can also be made available upon request at the Information Desk.
Lost property: (33-1) 40 20 53 17.


Bookshop, Boutiques and Chalcography

From 9.30 a.m. to 9.45 p.m., guides, art books, reproductions of works, engravings, postcards, films on the Louvre, gift items, jewellery and casts. Tel.: (33-1) 40 20 52 06.
