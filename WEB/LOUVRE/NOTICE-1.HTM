The Louvre Server

With effect from 14th July 1995, it has been possible to connect to the Louvre museum server on the Internet at the address http://www.louvre.fr/.

Designed as an introduction to a visit to the museum, this server provides the following information:

  • The chapter "Magazine" aims to periodically highlight a work taken from the museum's collection via an article written by one of the curators of the Louvre. You will also find information on the new features of the museum under this heading (only available in the French version).
  • "Practical Information" provides timetables, admission charges, access and visit information for the museum.
  • "Cultural Activities Programme" describes the current programme of temporary exhibitions (in English) and series of lectures, symposia, films and concerts (only available in French).
  • "Publications and Databases" provides a bibliography of printed publications (some of which are in English), audiovisual and interactive productions (in French) produced by the Louvre museum, as well as information about the museum's databases.
  • "The Collections" presents a selection of the works of art from each of the seven departments of the museum.
  • Two titles "The Louvre Palace and Museum" and "The Grand Louvre" trace the history of the Louvre from the fortress to the pyramid.
  • "Partners and Mailboxes" permits a dialogue between the visitors of the site and the Louvre museum, and also gives a list of partners and other Internet sites which are accessible via the Louvre server.
  • The "Survey" will help us to better know the visitors of our site.
