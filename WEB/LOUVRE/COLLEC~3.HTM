  • L'Amateur de dessins, un portrait de Louis-Antoine Prat
    Interview with Louis-Antoine Prat on the occasion of the exhibition of his donation to the Louvre museum.
    Director: Nick Quinn (1995)
  • Dietrich von Bothmer - Fragments
    The specialist in Attic goblets with red figures and the generous donor of the Campana gallery talks to us about his life and his passion.
    Director: Jean-Christophe Ballot (1994)
  • Les Trois Reines du professeur Ledoux-Lebard
    Interview with a collector with a passion for three Parisian Queens: the Empress Eugenie, Juliette Récamier, Mademoiselle Mars.
    Director: Catherine Beuve-Mery (1993)
  • Jean-Pierre Changeux, le plaisir et la raison
    Interview with one of the greatest French neurobiologists on the reasons behind the pleasure and passion inspired by works of art.
    Director: Matthieu Poirot-Delpech (1993)
  • Le Don de l'apôtre
    D. David-Weill, collector and donor.
    Apôtre, probablement saint Philippe, provenant de la châsse de saint Romain (1260-1280).
    Director: Bernard Monsigny (1988)
