Albums and monographs



These relate to a theme, centred on a series of homogenous works (Suse, 6,000 ans d'histoire, Les Chasses de Maximilien, François Ier et ses artistes etc.); they make up the Monographs collection of the Réunion des Musées Nationaux. Details concerning the titles and all information appear in the catalogue of publications of the RMN, 49 rue Etienne Marcel, 75039 Paris Cedex 01, tel. (33-1) 40 13 48 00, fax (33-1) 40 13 48 61. They are on sale in the bookshop.
