A day trek to Panauti, an ancient riverside town via Namo Buddha Stupa or to Palanchowk Bhagawati is very popular among our guests. A trip to the Tibetan border or to the hot Sulphur Spring at Tatopani (80 km) by car is also rewanding. Tafting along the Bhotekoshi river (down the valley) that originates from Tibet is quite adventurous. A full day excursion to Jiri (170 km) - the starting point of the Everest trek or up to Charikot (120 km) only on the Jiri road is most spectacular for its beautiful landscape and Himalayan Ranges. It is quite interesting to take these excursions. All these services can be acquired from the resort. Please contact at the reception for information.

Dhulikhel Resort also provides an excellent opportunity to watch the beautiful sunrise over the majestic Himalayas. Strolling along the terraced pathways within the resort, you may catch a glimpse of natural beauty of hills at sunset.

This unique Resort offers different restaurants to sample various types of food.
