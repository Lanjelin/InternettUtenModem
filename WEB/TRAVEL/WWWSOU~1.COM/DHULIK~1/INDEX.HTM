DHULIKHEL LODGE (RESORT)
managed by Dhulikhelikhel Lodge (P.) Ltd.

A Breath of Fresh Air ... 30 Km. East of Kathmandu

Dhulikhel Resort is situated 30 km east of Kathmandu in a small town famous for its scenic beauty and old traditions. As you drive along the Arniko Highway heading you are rewarded with a sight so spectacular that even and artist would struggle to convey its real beauty. Once you are in your room, you can relax and enjoy the full extent of the scenery.

The Resort is located at the top of a valley some 1525 m (5000 ft) above sea level. You can admire the ingenuity of the rice terraces lining the valley with a vast range of greens and browns. Beyond, lies the majestic range of white Himalayan Peaks. At day break the horizon is transformed into a magnificent panorama bathed in exquisite colours from Langtang in the northwest to Mt. Karyolung in the far east.

We take a pride of providing a destination which is guaranteed to enchant our guests. The surrounding forest is a home to many wild birds ranging from eagles to phesants. The lush and livily forest contains many indigenous trees, plants and mature Rhododendron bushes.

Dhulikhel is an ideal place for those planning to trek, stretch legs, and get bodies acclimatised to the rigours of trekking.
