PERFECT LOCATION :

"The Everest Hotel rises where the three medieval cities meet in the heart of Kathmandu Valley. Conveniently located in the upcoming business and commercial District, it is just a walking distance from the International Convention Centre, minutes away from the Royal Palace, Embassies and 5 minutes drive from Tribhuwan International Airport.

A deluxe 5-Star hotel which reflects the traditional culture of Nepal. The rooms are spacious and offer a panoramic view of the Himalayas and the Valley. The hotel has 7 Restaurants and Bars serving Continental, Chinese and South Asian cuisine."
