BUGLES & TIGERS

A posh rooftop Bar has all the inimitable, elegant ambiance of a Gurkha Officers Mess. A place of memories that celebrates the gallant Gurkha. Drink and appetizers are served from 12 noon to 11 p.m.

THE CAFE

Bright and airy, a place for an all day dining located at the lobby level. The Cafe offers a sumptuous Breakfast buffet tempting menu selection with a variety of international cuisine plus a wide range of Asian favourites.
Also features a Nepalese Buffet and Cultural Programme in a traditional Nepalese ambiance.

REPUTATIONS

Opening Hours : 9.00 p.m. to 1 a.m. except Mondays
