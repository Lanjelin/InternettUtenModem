The Artist:

German by birth, Rolf A. Kluenter received his higher education at the State Academy of Fine Arts in Dusseldorf. A grant for a field study on religious art brought him to Nepal in 1980. He has remained ever since. What's more he has been exhibiting his work consistently both in Europe (Switzerland, Germany, Belgium, Holland) and in Nepal. Kluenter also has lectured for several years at the Tribhuvan University in Kathmandu and started the first Animation Film Production project in Nepal beside spearheading other painting projects in Belgium as well as here. Current projects include also a limited serie of Klueters personally autographed designs on silk ties and scarfs.

Puzzle:

Rolf A. Kluenter has been using puzzle shapes as a motif in his paintings since 1995. The shapes themselves seem to be freely floating through space, some receding and others advancing into the foreground. Thre is no hint what the completed puzzle may look like.
A longside the paintings titled puzzle on handmade paper, the artist has also done a series of three-dimensional painted objects on clay. These comprise groups of puzzle shapes that, again, do not fit together. It is only the space between them that defines the constellation as a unit.
