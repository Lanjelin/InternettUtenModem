Poor schools need help

Every year the nation marks the Education Day with different programmes on the 12th of Falgun throughout the country. We have been celebrating the day for many years now. Schools and colleges remain closed to celebrate the day. Some of the rich schools distribute prizes to their students who have done well in their final examinations while poor schools distribute sweets to their students.

All teachers want to encourage their students in their studies by giving them prizes but they cannot do so because they lack money. The government hardly provides the schools money except for paying the teachers.

Therefore I request the authorities concerned to provide certain amount of money to the poor schools to buy the goods to be distributed as prizes on the Education Day so as to encourage all the students to excel in their studies.

Om Rayamajhi

Kirtipur,

Kathmandu
