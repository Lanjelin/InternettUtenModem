MEMBERSHIP

NGCCI offers three types of memberships.

Ordinary: Any company or organization registered with His Majesty's Government of Nepal which has industrial, commercial, and economic ties with Germany may apply for ordinary membership. Registration fee is Rs. 10,000 and annual fee is Rs. 3,000.

Co-member: Any industrial and commercial agency of German nationality or any agency representing industrial and commercial firms of Germany can apply for co- membership. Note: Co-members are not allowed to vote or contest any post or get elected or nominated to the executive board of the chamber. Registration fee is Rs. 20,000 and annual fee is Rs. 5,000.

Honorary: Any Nepali or German national who has contributed or is contributing to the industrial, commercial, tourism, and economic sectors between Nepal and Germany is considered for honorary membership by the executive committee. Chiefs of Nepalese commercial organizations who have helped fulfill the chamber's aims and objectives, may be selected for honorary membership by the executive committee. Any firm, company, or organization shall fill a prescribed form prepared by the chamber and the executive committee executes the right to grant or reject membership to any applicant. A Membership Recommendation Committee has been formed to scrutinize applications and recommend suitable candidates.
