INTRODUCTION

Nepal German Chamber of Commerce and Industry was established in October 1990 in Kathmandu to strengthen and expand economic and trade ties with Germany. The organization is the first of its kind in the Himalayan kingdom. Its main objective is the development of trade, investment, tourism, and industry with the Federal Republic of Germany. During the past five years, it has extended all possible support to promote these specific areas and has maintained a keen interest in their progress. NGCCI as a bilateral chamber represents the interests of both Nepalese and German business communities in the two countries. Also, NGCCI maintains close professional relations with German chambers of commerce and trade related services are exchanged. In fact, the services are not only given to members but also to other non-member capable companies. Overall, the chamber is constantly on the look out for ways and means with which to help nurture business relations between Nepal and Germany.

THE NEED

In the past, Nepalese traders experienced a need to develop a business forum to promote further business relations with Germany. Opportunities to expand business ties between both countries existed but without a proper medium. Because of this most business persons felt it would be difficult and insecure to start new enterprises related to these two countries. Exporters and importers who had already benefited from the business between Nepal and Germany were keenly interested in promoting and expanding it further. Thus, it was felt that a sound body was required to develop better interaction between both parties as well as to establish cordial business ties. It was important that this body keep the business communities of both countries abreast of new changes in trade policies, rules, and opportunities so that business persons could have a clear understanding of the trade situation and make appropriate business decisions. In addition, Nepal was also interested in bringing German technology and investment into the country in order to enhance the Kingdom's industrial sector. Thus, the creation of NGCCI was considered essential as it would fulfill both the objectives of the nation and its business persons.
