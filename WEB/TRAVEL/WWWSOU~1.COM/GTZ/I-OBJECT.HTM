OBJECTIVES

  • To create an environment conducive to small business enterprises.
  • To increase the number of entrepreneurs in urban and semi-urban areas.
  • To provide consultancy and training programs to small-scale industries.
  • To increase employment opportunities in Nepal.
  • To share skills, experience, and expertise with small entrepreneurs.
  • To identify investment and marketing opportunities for small entrepreneurs.
  • To provide technical knowledge to small business persons.
  • To assist organizations that promote micro-businesses in Nepal.
