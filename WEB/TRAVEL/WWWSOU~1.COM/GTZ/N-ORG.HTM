ORGANIZATION

The chamber's highest policy making body is the General Assembly and it is represented by all members. Elections are held every three years for an Executive Board. The present Executive Board was elected in May 1994 and its 16 members will represent Nepal's trade, tourism, and industry sector from 1994 to 1997. A team of professionals will implement all plans. The secretariat is equipped with modern equipment and amenities and has an Information and Documentation Cell which has the latest news and information relating to business. The chamber also hires consultants to help complete research work and collect data.

NGCCI supports itself with membership fees, donations, grants, financial assistance received from benefactors, and fees charged for services rendered. It takes loans or accepts monies from other sources. Prominent Nepalese business houses and industries, who have trade and economic relations with Germany, have joined NGCCI and so have German companies doing business in Nepal. The chamber has 137 members.
