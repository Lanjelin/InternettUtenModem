DUTIES

  • To develop as an autonomous, professional and result-oriented institute.
  • To conduct programs on entrepreneurship, management, and technical skills development for trainers and trainees.
  • To make provisions for small business evaluation.
  • To organize, by itself or in collaboration with other organizations various types of research, trainings, studies, and surveys related to industry.
  • To promote exhibitions, workshops, and seminars on entrepreneurial development.
  • To prepare necessary curriculum and manuals for industrial enterprise development.
  • To conduct constructive follow-up programs.
  • To develop effective services for micro-enterprises and women entrepreneurship.
  • To rehabilitate sick industries.
  • To develop mechanisms for delivering small business promotional services through intermediary organizations.
  • To revise and disseminate existing training packages.
