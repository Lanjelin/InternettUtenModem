OBJECTIVES

  • To try to develop trade relations and cooperation between industrialists, business persons as well as other professionals in Nepal and Germany.
  • To represent the chamber in government organizations and other agencies
  • To strengthen economic cooperation between Nepal and Germany.
  • To promote the transfer of technology and investment between the two countries.
  • To promote joint economic ventures and technology development in industry and commerce and arrange conferences and seminars.
  • To strengthen relations in the best interests of commercial and industrial communities of Nepal and Germany.
  • To act as an arbitrator in case of dispute in the field of industry and commerce between Nepal and Germany and undertake necessary action to solve the problem.
  • To organize visits of professionals or business communities and develop relations in the fields of industry, commerce, and tourism.
  • To collect various statistics and information regarding business, economic cooperation, tourism and set up an Information and Documentation Center.
  • To do research in the field of industry, commerce, and economic relations and collect data and circulate and exchange information between the two countries.
  • To publish bulletins, research papers, and other publications and exchange necessary information relating to industry, commerce, and economy.
  • To organize and participate in industry and trade exhibitions.
