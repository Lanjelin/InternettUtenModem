ACHIEVEMENTS

Many programs in the field of entrepreneurship and small business development have been created and implemented in Nepal within the last decade by SBPP. Due to its effectiveness, New Business Creation package is well known abroad and has been implemented in 70 countries.

  • The ability to transfer entrepreneurship and enterprise development and training skills, as per the needs of target groups and partner institutions, has been developed.
  • Action research has been conducted to develop sustainable entrepreneurship and management development packages, especially in rural areas.
  • As a result of small business training and consultancy services, the country has progressed in business and more employment opportunities have been opened up.
  • Able and committed professionals have been developed to assure technical and management improvement.
  • The trust of the small business community has been won through successful programs developed and implemented by professionals.
  • As per the objectives of HMG and donor agencies, the target group has been directly involved in various phases of program planning and implementation.
