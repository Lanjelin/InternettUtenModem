January-February:
    Too cold to undertake long treks and high altitude treks. Short treks near Kathmandu or Pokhara can be done in mid-January to February with warm clothes. Possibility of unexpected rain in low land and snowfall in high mountain (above 1800 meters) in Janauary to mid-February.
March-May:
    Good / best season for all kinds of treks including trekking peak climbings, and major expeditions. Ideal time for photography and view flora and fauna.
June-July:
    Monsoon. Off season for treks. Possible to undertake research or educational treks for students near Kathmandu or Pokhara.
August:
    Treks can be done. Possibility of unexpected rain for short time.
September-October:
    Good / best season for all kinds of treks including trekking peak climbings and major expeditions.
November-December:
    Off season. Short treks near Kathmandu or Pokhara can be done in November with warm clothes. Possibility of rain in low land and snowfall in mountains in November. Expected rain in low land and heavy snowfall in mountains during mid-November to December.
