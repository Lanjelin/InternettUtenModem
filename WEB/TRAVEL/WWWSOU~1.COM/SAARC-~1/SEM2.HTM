Report of the Second Seminar of SCCI Tourism Council held in Colombo, Sri Lanka on 2nd and 3rd of September 1996

The SCCI Tourism Council in collaboration with the Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry of Sri Lanka (FCCISL) oranised its 2nd seminar on Common Policy for Progress in Colombo, Sri Lanka on 2-3 September, 1996. Tourist Hotels Association of Sri Lanka (THASL), Travel Agents Association of Sri Lanka (TAASL) and Association of Group Tour Agencies (AGTA) were the co-hosters of the seminar. In the course of the seminars the delegates made a courtesy call on Rt Hon'ble Mr Shirimavo Bhandaranayake, Prime Minister of Srilanka. The seminar was inaugurated on 2nd of September by Hon'ble Mr Dharma Siri Senanayake, Minister of Media, Tourism and Aviation. The working sessions were held on 3rd September 1996.
