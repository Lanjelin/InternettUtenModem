BUSINESS PLAN

Basically, this business plan seeks to address the issue of taking the council activities to the national, regional and international arenas through tactical activities as per the need of the tourism business of the region. The plan will be refined as we go along to chart strategic initiatives that have long term consideration.
This plan proposes that the council seek to undertake the activities that are in tune with the constitution of SCCI and the Tourism Council Declaration. These activities can only succeed with the full support and co-operation of the council members, apex chambers, governments and public at large of SAARC countries. The plan will focus on the following areas :

  • Establishing Contacts / Networking
  • Marketing / Communication
  • Research / Exchange of Information
  • Human Resource Development
  • Finances
  • Causing Community Participation
