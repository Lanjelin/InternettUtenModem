Of Treks, Climbs and Tours

In Pakistan

Walji's Adventure Pakistan, with more than 30 years of experience in travel and trekking in Pakistan offers a full program of fixed departures of treks, climbs and tours throughout Pakistan. Through the internet, we can handle your reservations and e-mail you the necessary information you need to travel to Concordia, the K-2 Base Camp, to watch the world's highest polo match, trek to Fairy Meadows at the foot of Nanga Parbat, or travel by boat along the Indus River.

You can e-mail Walji's in Pakistan for additional information, you can download a reservation form for faxing to us, or fill in the online reservation form. We will reply quickly and send you equipment lists, visa information and other details necessary to make your adventure trip to Pakistan a success.
