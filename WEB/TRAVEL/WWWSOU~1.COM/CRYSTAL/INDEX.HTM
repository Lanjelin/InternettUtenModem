VALLEY OF
JOY - POKHARA

Himalayas, history & culture beckon travellers to the Kingdom of Nepal.
Your visit to this land is not complete until and unless you have experienced Pokhara.

A  visit to Pokhara is a complete experience in itself. Lying 200 kms west of Kathmandu, Pokhara is easily accessible by road and daily flight. The valley is wide and flatan enormous sunlit play ground covered by hill, lakes, forests, rivers, waterfalls, farms and fields.

Friendly people inhabit the valley, revelling in nature's lap. In Pokhara, nature and culture blend beautifully to present a panorama unparalled anywhere else.

Pokhara is interspersed with numerous lakes known as 'tal'. Thelargest among them, the Phewa Lake reflects the glorious peaks of majestic western himalayas - the Annapurna Range. On a clear day, you will be captivated by the dancing shadows of Fish Tail Peak (machhapuchhare) in the Phewa Lake.

For trekking enthusiasts, Pokhara is gateway to the popular trekking routes of Annapurna Region passing through rolling terrain and scenic countryside. You can go boating in

Pokhara or fishing, if you feel like. One can browse through old bazars and watch the mule trains passing from northern highlands. Listen to the escapades of rescapades of retired Gurkha soldiers in a tea shoo. Or just relax and let the atmosphere soak in.

Located midway between the lake and the town, New Hotel Crystal is fully equipped to cater to all your needs. All rooms are individually airconditioned with private bath and shower with IDD telephone.
Business and recreation facilities complement the aesthetic property. Our trained staff enjoy serving. Every evening, you can sit down to watch Nepalese cultural programme reflecting the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Enrich your Pokhara experience by being at New Hotel Crystal.
Later, when you take a trek, go hking, visit Lumbini - birth place of Lord Buddha - or air dash to another destination, the memories of valley of joy will linger.
