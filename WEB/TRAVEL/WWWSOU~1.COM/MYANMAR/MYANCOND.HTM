Tour Programs in Myanmar

Tour Conditions

Tour Price

All tour prices are quoted net in US Dollars. Tour price (first class and standard tour) includes all accommodation in twin share bed basis, full breakfast at confirmed hotels and table d'hote lunch and dinner at first class local restaurants, all internal transportation and transfers, all city sightseeing and excursions, all entrance fees, porterage for two pieces per person, accompanied guide fees, and Mandalay Marionette Show for a group of five and above.

Please note that first-class accommodation is not always available.

Economy Tour price includes accommodation at economy hotel/motel/guest house with breakfast, all internal transportation and transfers, all city sightseeing and excursions, all entrance fees, porterage for two pieces per person and station guide fees.

Tour price does not include: airport departure tax, meals not listed, optional tours and personal spending money.

CANCELLATION

Cancellation charges are calculated on the day written notification is received. Cancellation fees are as follows:

45 days prior to arrival: Nil
44-21 days prior to arrival: 10% of Tour Price
Less than 7 days prior to arrival: 30% of Tour Price
