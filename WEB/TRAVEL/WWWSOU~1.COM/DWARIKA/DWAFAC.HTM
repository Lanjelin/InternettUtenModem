THE FACILITIES

  1. 30 rooms completely unique with nepali upholstery and artifacts are complemented by modern bathrooms with all the amenities of a luxury hotel.
  2. "Breakfast room" where one can watch the trees and the garden.
  3. "Toran", a cosy room where you can have home style Nepali, Chinese, Indian and Continental food.
  4. "Krishnarpan" a speciality Nepali restaurant where food is served in years old plates and glasses by charming hostesses in ethnic costumes. Eating here is not just a meal but an experience itself of nepalese hospitality and style. our menus st art from 6 course upward to 16.
  5. "Astamangal bar" , where you can enjoy your before and after dinner drinks of your choice.
  6. The lounge/bar (Lumbini), a room with two fireplaces for winter, old Italian marble floor, painted glass doors from the times of victorian England, carved windows and doorways with exceptional paintings and artifacts for you to relax at the en d of the day.
  7. Shop "House of Treasure", a souvinier shop with exceptional "Thanka" paintings, wooden goods, stone and metal figures silver jewellery, hand woven nepali "dhaka" shawls and many more other things from nepal. If you cannot find what you want, we are here to help you.
