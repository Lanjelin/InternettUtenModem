Speakers Corner

This is your opportunity to get it off your chest! Is there a burning issue that you feel needs to be discussed, and doesn’t appear on the conference programme? Or perhaps you’ve got a brilliant idea that you want to share? Or an experience that you feel is relevant? Whatever it is, please share it with us.

At the conference we will have a speakers corner. This will be a 5 minute opportunity for people to stand up, say what they think, then sit down and wait for the reaction! Unfortunately you can’t stand up in front of us and have your say. However, if you send us your thoughts by e-mail, we’ll make sure that someone reads them out on your behalf, and we’ll even keep a record of the response!

And we’ll post them on this page for everyone else to read!!

So get thinking - be creative, be innovative, be radical. Then click on the button below - type in your ideas, and send them on to us. Couldn’t be easier!
