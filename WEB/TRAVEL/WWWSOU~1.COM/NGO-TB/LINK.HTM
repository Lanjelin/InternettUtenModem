tbNETLinks

Wouldn't it be great if all the people in the world who had any interest in TB were able to get together? We can't manage that - but we can at least try to link everyone up. And to start the process, let's try to build a list of all the organisations in the world who have a significant interest in TB control.

We'll start the ball rolling by making links to other relevant home pages from this site. To help us add more links, please send details of any home pages you think should be added to this list. Home pages from the developing world particularly invited - but others accepted too!

Click HERE to send us a suggested link.
