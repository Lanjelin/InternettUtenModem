ICIMOD's Work Programme (1995-1998)

On 1st January 1995, ICIMOD embarked on a dynamic, ambitious four-year programme that was designed after intensive consultations with partner institutions, the Board of Governors, ICIMOD Support Group, and many others. A detailed description of this programme is presented in the document "Regional Collaborative Programme for the Sustainable Development of the Hindu Kush-Himalayas", which can be obtained from ICIMOD. The document

  • reflects the main mountain development concerns and potentials of the countries of the HKH;
  • addresses major issues in sustainable mountain development from a regional perspective;
  • provides opportunities for the HKH countries to collaborate with ICIMOD in a wide range of key areas in integrated mountain development on a continuing basis;
  • presents a comprehensive operational programme for ICIMOD, with distinct activities & outputs; and
  • translates the recommendations of Chapter 13, Agenda 21, "Fragile Ecosystems, Sustainable Mountain Development" into action at regional and national level.
