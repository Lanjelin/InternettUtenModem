Goals and Objectives

Long-term Goal

To contribute to attaining environmental stability and poverty eradication in the Hindu Kush-Himalayas.

Objectives

Within this overall long-term perspective, the present four-year programme has the following general objectives.

  • To promote sustainability of mountain farming systems
  • To promote sustainable management of mountain natural resources
  • To promote diversification of mountain economies
  • To build national capacities for preparing and implementing strategies for sustainable mountain development
  • To establish a strong information base and network on sustainable development of the HKH
