Opportunities/Advantages

Diversity - The region is unique because of its great variety of environmental conditions, which is reflected in its

  • ecological diversity,
  • biological diversity,
  • cultural diversity, and
  • institutional diversity.

Opportunities-The mountains provide a "niche" or opportunity for development in which they have comparative advantages. Examples of such opportunities are:

  • hydropower
  • tourism
  • vegetable seeds and off-season vegetables
  • horticulture
  • medicinal plants and other high-value products

Human Resources - The people of the region are its strongest asset. Despite cultural, ethnic, and institutional diversity they have coped with the harsh environment by developing mountain-specific adaption mechanisms for their technologies, community-based natural resources' management systems, and traditional practices of ethno-engineering and indigenous knowledge systems.
