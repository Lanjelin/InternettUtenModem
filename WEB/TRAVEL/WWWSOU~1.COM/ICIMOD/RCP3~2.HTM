ICIMOD's Human Resources

During this four-year period ICIMOD is expected to have the following 26 internationally-recruited professional staff, most of whom will occupy key positions to act as focal points and as intellectual leaders for specific subjects and sectors. National officers and administrative support staff will complement these international staff with locally available skills.
