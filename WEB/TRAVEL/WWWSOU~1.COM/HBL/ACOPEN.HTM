Deposits:

The Bank offers following types of deposit schemes to the customers:

  1. Checking / Current Account
  2. Savings Account
  3. Short Term Deposit Account (7 days to 90 days)
  4. Fixed Term Deposits. (above 90 days)

The Bank accepts and maintains these types of deposit accounts in the following currencies:

  1. Nepali Rupees
  2. U. S. Dollar
  3. Pound Sterling
  4. Duetsch Mark
  5. Japanese Yen
  6. Swiss Franc

An account can be opened by any Customer complying with the General Rules for Account Opening and fullfilling the document requrements..
