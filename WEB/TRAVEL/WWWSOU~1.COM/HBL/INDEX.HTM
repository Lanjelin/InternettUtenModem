Himalayan Bank Limited ( HBL ) is a joint venture commercial bank, incorporated with Habib Bank Limited of Pakistan.

The Bank has two fully equipped commercial branches operating in Kathmandu, one rural Branch in Nagarkot and an extension counter in Teaching Hospital. The Bank is in the process of opening three more Branches in the near future.

Himalayan Bank's policy is to extend quality service to its customers. The guiding principles of HBL has been sensitive tailoring of facilities to our customers, prompt service, prudent investments and introduction of innovative products in the Nepalese market. Himalayan Bank will tailor the facilities to its customers, taking into account the customer's needs and requirements anywhere in the world.
