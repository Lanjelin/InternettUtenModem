COMPUTER ASSOCIATION OF NEPAL

  • Back Ground
    Computer Association of Nepal (CAN) was established in December 1992 as an autonomous, non-political, non-partisan, non-profitable and service oriented organization. The computer association has been formed with the involvement of professionals, specialists, vendors, manufacturers and information technology experts within the country.
  • Objectives
    As mentioned in the constitution of CAN, the main objectives of CAN are as follows :
      1. To assist in the utilization, enhancement and promotion of computers and information technology in the country and to help develop strategies to meet the necessary requirements for the development of literacy and skill regarding computer science.
      2. To provide and protect the necessary rights and privileges benefits to individuals, institutions, companies and organizations affiliated to activities of CAN.
      3. To provide support and facilities to all general, corporate and honorary members of CAN.
  • Activities
    To fulfill the above objectives, the following activities are envisaged :
      1. To assist in the institutional development of computer technology.
      2. To assist and develop the synchronization and standardization in the field of computer training and education.
      3. To develop coordination among computer professionals with their services.
      4. To organize and conduct seminars, workshops, talk and training programs and to provide consultancy and R & D services.
      5. To exchange knowledge, skill and technology in the field of computer and information technology with similar types of organizations within and outside the country.
      6. To assist in the field of technology transfer, export, import and formulation of national policies.
