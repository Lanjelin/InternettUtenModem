WE ORGANISE TWO TYPES OF TREKKING

1) The Tea House Trek : This style trek is offered in the Everest, Annapurna, Langtang and other major areas where guest houses are available on the trails.

2) The Camping Trek : Camping allows you to steer away from the most trekked routes and beaten tracks, even on low altitude treks. This is made possible by travelling as a self contained unit, carry all food, camping and kitchen equipment.

We use quiet village trails that lead through terraced rice paddies, magnificent forests and glacial valleys rarely travelled by other trekkers. We provide all support staff who ensure your trek is a holiday.

COST INCLUSIVES ON THE CAMPING TREK:

Trekking permits, transportation (by land and/or air) to and from the trekking route, three meals a day on the trek, cooking utensils, sleeping bags, jackets, tents, mattresses, water bottles, rain ponchos, ice axes, guides, cooks, kitchen boys, required porters, a first aid kit, insurance for Nepalese crews, and two nights hotel accommodation in Kathmandu at a tourist standard hotel or guest house.

COST INCLUSIVES ON THE TEA HOUSE TREK:

Trekking permits, transportation (by land and/or air) to and from the trekking route, three meals a day on the trek choosing from local menus, accommodation (in tea houses), guides, required porters, a first aid kit, insurance for all Nepalese crews, and two nights hotel accommodation in Kathmandu at a tourist standard hotel or guest house.

COST EXCLUSIVES ON BOTH TYPES OF TREKS:

National park fees, emergency rescue, personal expenses, drinks from local markets, travellers' insurance, day packs, and royalties for government specified regions.
