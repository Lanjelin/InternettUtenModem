BHUTAN TREK/TOUR:

Bhutan is a unique, medieval, tiny Kingdom. It's the only Himalayan country not yet touched by modern civilization. The lofty silvery mountain peaks, evergreen hills, old customs, art and architecture make Bhutan a virgin Shangri-la country. To trek and travel in this isolated Kingdom of Bhutan is a voyage into nature, culture and adventure. We are offering:

  • 4 Day tour (Glimpse of the Kingdom-1)
  • 8 Day tour (Discover Druk-yul Kingdom-2)
  • 12 Day trek/tour (The Sacred Valley-3)
  • Group size: Minimum 4
