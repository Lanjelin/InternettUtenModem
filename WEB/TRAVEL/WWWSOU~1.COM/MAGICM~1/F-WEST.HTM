TREKS IN THE FAR WEST

Dolpo

Set in Nepals' wild west the Dolpo region has a lot to offer the trekker who wants to see something different to the better known areas in the east. Experience culture and religion that is unique to this part of the Himalaya. The landscape too is totally different to that seen elsewhere in Nepal. Rugged mountain ranges give way to barren rolling hills; tranquil lakes reflect snow capped peaks; isolated monasteries and villages populated by people of Tibetan descent eking out an existence in a harsh, inhospitable climate and deep, virgin forests sheltering birds and animals found only in this region.

Duration: 33 days in Nepal
Grade: 5
