OTHER DESTINATIONS

Magic Mountain can organise trekking holidays anywhere throughout the Himalaya. Our experienced associate companies will ensure that your trip is all that you expect. The most practical destinations are Tibet and India although we can quote for itineraries in Bhutan and Pakistan. Due to political unrest we are not currently organising trips into Kashmir or Zanskar.

In Tibet we are offering the following trips:

Lhasa Overland
10 days ex Kathmandu. Largely vehicle based with a flight into Lhasa. Three days in Lhasa then overland back to Kathmandu via Everest Base Camp
North Face of Everest
26 days ex Kathmandu. Probably the worlds' highest trek. Climb high above Everest Base Camp for views of the mountain that you can get from nowhere else.
Mt Kailash to Lhasa
29 days ex Kathmandu. Trek Nepals' far west then cross the border to visit Mt Kailash and Lake Manasarovar before an overland trip to Lhasa.

In Sikkim the choices are:

Kanchenjunga trek
15 days ex Kathmandu/Calcutta. See Kanchenjunga from the Sikkim side trekking through the unspoiled foothills above Darjeeling. A peak climb may be included in this trip by adding six days to the itinerary.
Dzongri Trek
10 days ex Kathmandu/Calcutta. A shorter trek into the Indian Himalayan foothills below Kanchenjunga.
Sikkim Darjeeling Cultural Tour
10 days ex Kathmandu/Calcutta. Fully escorted cultural tour taking in the best of Sikkim and the Darjeeling regions. Hotel accommodation and vehicle transport.
