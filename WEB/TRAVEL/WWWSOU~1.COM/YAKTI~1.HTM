An Ideal Location

Settled in the very heart of Kathmandu - only 15 minutes from the airport - is the legendary Yak and Yeti. A spectacular 'new' hotel that blends with an equally splendid 18th century palace, creating an unrivalled 20th century residence of outstanding luxury.

Comfort and Convenience

Each of the Yak and Yeti's 270 rooms and suites have been carefully appointed in the highest degree of comfort and convenience.
All rooms have ensuite bathrooms, and International Direct Dial (IDD) telephone, multi-channel colour television and fully stocked minibar refrigerator. Room Service of course is available 24 hours a day, with same day laundry and valet service.

The Pleasure Of Doing Business

Conducting business at the Yak and Yeti is designed to be as efficient as much as pleasurable.
The business center is fully staffed during office hours offering Executive Secretarial Services, Telex and Facsmile transmission, Word Processing and a reference library of Directories and Periodicals.
The Yak and Yeti has well equipped meeting rooms and facilities for the most prestigious receptions, business dinners or corporate presentations.
This state- of- the- art business center has been recently equipped with Microsoft Windows'95 software.
