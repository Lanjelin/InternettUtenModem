Hospitals and Drug Stores: There are standard hospitals with all sorts of modern facilities in the city of Kathmandu. Outside the valley of Kathmandu, there are hospitals in every zone. The drug houses, dispensaries and pharmaceutical shops of Kathmandu sell all kinds of medicines, including those imported from overseas.
