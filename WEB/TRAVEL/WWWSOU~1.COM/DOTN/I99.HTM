Newspapers and Magazine

The Rising Nepal, in English and the Gorkhapatra in Nepali Devanagari Script are the two major newspapers published by the government owned corporation. A number of daily news papers such as The Kathmandu Post (in English), Kantipur (in Nepali), Himal Timess (in Nepali), Samachar (in Nepali) etc and other weekly newspapers and magazines, local and international are also available in the book stalls.
