Domestic Airlines

The Royal Nepal Airlines corporation operates an extensive network of air services in the interior of Nepal. It has scheduled flight by Twin Otter and Avro from Kathmandu to Taplejung, Bhadrapur, Rajbiraj, Bhojpur, Phaplu, Lukla, Lamidanda, Tumlingtar, Rumjatar and Biratnagar in the Estern Development Region; to Simra, Janakpur, Ramechhap, Jiri, Bharatpur and Meghauli in the Central Development Region; to Pokhara, Jomsom, Manang, Baglung and Bhairahawa in the Western Development Region; and to Nepalgunj, Chaurjhahari, Surkhet, Dang, Rolpa and Jumla in the Mid-western Development Region and to Bajura, Baitadi, Darchula, Mahendranagar, Dhangadi, Silgari (Doti), Tikapur and Sanphebagar in the Far Western Development Region.

Mountain Flight: One of the highlights of a visit to nepal is the mountain flight, organized by the Royal Nepal Airlines. Avro or Boing takes 45 minutes flight along the great Himalayan range to Everest or on a similar flight west to Dhaulagiri past such giant as the Annapurna massif. There is also a "Complete Himalayan panorama" flight which takes one on an enchanted review of the entire Nepal Himalayan range.
