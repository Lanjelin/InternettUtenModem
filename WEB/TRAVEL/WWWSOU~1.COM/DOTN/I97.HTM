Tourism Information Centers:

Department of Tourism:

His Majesty's Government of Nepal, Department of Tourism runs and information counter at its office in New Baneshwar to provide factual information and guidance to the tourists. It also distributes booklets, folder, posters and stickers to the tourists free of cost. There are also Tourist Information Centers run by Department of Tourism at the following places.
