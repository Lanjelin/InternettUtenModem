Wondrous Vistas!

Wonderous vistas, huge rooms, soaring cavern walls reflecting every color of the rainbow, towering formations and columns. delicate crystals that look like flowers, winding passageways, waterfalls, streams, pools and lakes, speleothem draperies, massive onyx formations, slender shafts - standing alone or crowded together in unbelievable profusion...All of this, and more, can be seen in the show caves listed in this CAVES AND CAVERNS Directory.

Visitors feel a thrill of excitement as underground pathways are followed through a cave or cavern. Every cave is a new, exciting experience and different from all the others. Well-informed guides entertain tours, telling about the history, lore and geologic development of the cave. Many walkways are almost level, others include stairs and ramps. Some caves are entered by walking, others by boat or elevator, and at one cave the visitors ride through in a tram. Because trails follow the natural contours of the cave, visitors with disabilities might want to inquire about the accessibility of the caves they plan to visit. Comfortable walking shoes are suggested and visitors often like to have a light jacket or sweatshirt with them during their tour through a cave or cavern.
