Nynäshamn - A paradise for nature-lovers

Waves lap gently against the smooth grey rocky shoreline, ducks wing their way across the glittering bay and the sun's warm rays gently caress the jetty. It is summer in one of the world's most beautiful archipelagos.

Holidays in Nynäshamn mean having thousands of islands to explore. There are deep forests rich in wildlife, flower covered meadows, small villages and numerous beautiful trails. A peaceful oasis for everybody. A place for both recreation and inner peace. If you choose to leave the peace and quite for a while and seek "the bright lights" you can wander about in the small harbours that abound on this coastline. Visit shopping center, go to a dance, watch a sailing regatta, take in a few rounds of golf - or try some something exciting such cliff climbing, canoeing, riding or as diving for treasure. Feel history come alive in our age old fishing villages. A thousand years of live and tradition as it was.
