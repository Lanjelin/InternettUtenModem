And you thought you had management problems: Scotland's territory includes 787 islands, an astouding number for a country 274 miles long. From the University of Edinburgh's geography department comes this straight-forward guide to things Scottish. Lots of facts and figures here: a cool historical database with weather and crop stats from 1550-1780 and the complete list of Scottish and British monarchs. But there is some life here, from Scottish soccer teams' pages to drawings of more than 280 tartans (the plaid patterns particular to the various clans). Add a quick history and links to many other pages round out a spartan but useful page. Certainly nothing out of kilt here.
