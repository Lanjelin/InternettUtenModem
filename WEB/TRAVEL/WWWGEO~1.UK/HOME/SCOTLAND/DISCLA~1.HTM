Aims & Disclaimer

Aim:

The "Gateway to Scotland" aims to be the definitive source of validated information on the geography, history, culture, tourism and traditions of Scotland. We hope to achieve this through locally provided information, together with links to carefully selected pages and organisations which are known to provide reliable information.

The "Gateway to Scotland" confines itself to facts, and explicitly does not present opinions.
