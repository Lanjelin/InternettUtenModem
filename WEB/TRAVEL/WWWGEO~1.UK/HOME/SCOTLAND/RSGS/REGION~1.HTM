RSGS Regional Centres

The lecture series are held at RSGS Regional Centres in:

  • Aberdeen
  • Airdrie
  • Ayr
  • Dundee
  • Dunfermline
  • East Kilbride
  • Edinburgh
  • Galashiels (Borders)
  • Glasgow
  • Kirkcaldy
  • Livingston
  • Perth
  • Stirling

Other Centres are planned for future seasons.
Admission to Lectures for Non-Members is normally £5.00 inc. VAT

Note: Admission to lectures is limited by the capacity of the auditorium and cannot always be guaranteed. Members are required to book tickets for some lectures.
