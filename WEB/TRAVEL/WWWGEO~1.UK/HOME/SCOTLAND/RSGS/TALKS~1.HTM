Royal Scottish Geographical Society

Illustrated Talks

Each year from September to March the Society provides a programme of over one hundred illustrated talks hosted at RSGS Regional Centres throughout Scotland and, occasionally, at larger venues when the popularity of the guest speaker demands.
By bringing the world's foremost geographical and environmental experts, travellers and explorers to address the Society, RSGS aims to widen the understanding of the issues facing the world's peoples, cultures, environments, cities and wildernesses.

Guest Speakers

Speakers for the 1996/7 season included:

  • Explorer, Sir Ranulph Fiennes
  • Astronaut, Helen Sharman
  • Environmentalist, Jonathon Porrit
  • Dr Richard Leakey
  • Dr Jane Goodall
  • Stills photographer of the "Life in the Freezer" TV series, Ben Osborne
  • Thesiger biographer, Michael Asher
  • Mountaineer, Simon Jenkins.
