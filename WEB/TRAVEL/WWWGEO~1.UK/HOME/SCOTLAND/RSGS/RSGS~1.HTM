Welcome to the RSGS Home Page

The Royal Scottish Geographical Society has a distinguished history and an exciting and innovative future.
RSGS aims to be the foremost independent body in Scotland promoting the understanding of the inter-relationships between people, places and the environment through geographical research, education, debate, travel and exploration.
