Royal Scottish Geographical Society

Joining the RSGS

Membership of RSGS is open to all.

Members are entitled to free attendance at Royal Scottish Geographical Society Illustrated Talks which are held at RSGS Regional Centres or larger venues when the popularity of the guest speaker demands.

Members receive Scottish Geographical Magazine and Geogscot the Society's newsletter free of charge, and are entitled to use the Society's Library Collections to borrow books.

Other benefits include Field Outings, travel offers and competitions.
