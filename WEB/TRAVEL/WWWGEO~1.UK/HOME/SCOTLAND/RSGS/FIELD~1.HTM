Royal Scottish Geographical Society

Field Outings

Field outings to a wide range of destinations are organised by members of the various regional centres and RSGS headquarters.
Members of the Perth Centre in association with Perth Hill Walking club made a summer visit to The Faroe Islands.
Stirling Centre members recently visited the new Glenfinglas Reservoir and Loch Katrine, where they enjoyed a comprehensive tour of the water works.

Opportunities for special trips abroad are advertised in the Society's newsletter Geogscot. Leaders are chosen for their experience or specialist knowledge.

Members also have the chance to contribute to geographical field work and research. Recently members had the opportunity to take part in the 1996 British Land Use Survey organised by the Geographical Association. The results of the survey are due for publication in April 1997.
