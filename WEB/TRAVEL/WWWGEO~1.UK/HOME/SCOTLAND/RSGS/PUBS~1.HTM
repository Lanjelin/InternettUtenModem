Royal Scottish Geographical Society

Publications

The RSGS publishes The Scottish Geographical Magazine and Geogscot, the newsletter for members. In addition the RSGS produces books and pamphlets on geographical and environmental issues.

Scottish Geographical Magazine

Established over a century ago, the Scottish Geographical Magazine, the highly regarded journal of the Royal Scottish Geographical Society, publishes research papers and articles on all aspects of geography, particularly relating to Scotland.
Subscription to the magazine is free of charge to Members.

The November 1996 issue included papers on:
Talus rock glaciers in Scotland; The Poll tax and the Scottish regional elections; Household migration behaviour in Scotland; Scotland's linguistic heritage; Glasgow's drumlins; Derelict and vacant land in Scotland.

Geogscot

Geogscot is the newsletter of the Society. Subscription is free of charge to Members.

Edited by the Director of the Society, Geogscot contains news and issues of particular interest to Society Members: expedition reports, Awards, competitions, book reviews, research news and conferences, member's travel tales, excerpts from the Society Library Collections and archives, Field Outings and travel offers, projects, links with other societies and news from the RSGS Regional Centres.
