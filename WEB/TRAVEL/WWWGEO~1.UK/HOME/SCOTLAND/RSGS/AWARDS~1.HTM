Royal Scottish Geographical Society

Awards

The Society's medals and fellowships are amongst the world's most prestigious awards for outstanding contributions to geography and exploration.

Awards are presented at the Society's annual awards ceremony and dinner to which Members, Sponsors and their guests are invited.

Past award winners include:

  • H.M. Stanley
  • Scott
  • Shackleton
  • Amundsen
  • Hunt and Hillary
  • Neil Armstrong
  • Fiennes
  • Attenborough
  • Bonnington
  • Leakey
  • HRH The Princess Royal.
