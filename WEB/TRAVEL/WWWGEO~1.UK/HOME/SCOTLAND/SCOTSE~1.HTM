Commercial Servers


There are now a large number of commercial servers in or about Scotland and our list had become very incomplete, resulting in only a random selection. It is our firm belief that the information content of "Gateway to Scotland" is critical. and that this must always greatly exceed any promotional content. Thus we have desupported our list of commercial servers. We will still include links to promotional material where we regard that it complements what we provide, but hope that this stance retains our editorial independence.

If you want to view our old list of commercial servers it is, for the moment, available here. To find other commercial servers, look at the links to comprehensive lists at the bottom of this document.

Thus we include pointers to these various excellent indexes (see below), and only add particularly interesting sites our list (above).
