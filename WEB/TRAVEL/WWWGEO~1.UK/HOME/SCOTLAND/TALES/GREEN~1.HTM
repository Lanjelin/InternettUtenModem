Linda Murray Green

Linda Murray Green is an American journalist who has spent 22 years as a writer and editor for daily newspapers. She is currently managing editor of Bank Rate Monitor Publications in North Palm Beach, Florida. Linda and her husband, Mike, spent more than a year living in and exploring Scotland. She took many photographs of the people and countryside and has written extensively about both.
