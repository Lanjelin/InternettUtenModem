CAMPING IN OSLOMARKA

Cars and caravans are not permitted to drive into Oslomarka or BÊrumsmarka. Parking areas have been set aside in many places; these can be used as a starting point for walks or bicycle rides in Marka.

UNCIVILIZED BEHAVIOUR

Many camper-vans spend the night in Oslo's car parks and other places: this is not permitted. Unfortunately there are also many people who empty their effluent and other waste in places where this, likewise, is not permitted. Campers are requested to use the excellent facilities at our camping sites.

EMPTYING OF EFFLUENT

Both Bogstad Camping and Ekeberg Camping have facilities for the emptying of effluent and waste. Here, there are also facilities for washing cars and caravans.
