Public transports in Oslo

Trafikanten on Jernbanetorget by Oslo Central Station gives information about public transport in Oslo and Greater Oslo: local trains, tramways, buses, ferries and underground in Oslo, Greater Oslo area and in the Central Eastern Region. This Information office is run jointly by several transportation companies (buses, trains, boats, ferries, and underground) which may be municipal, regional, governmental and private.

Trafikanten
Jernbanetorvet 1 v/Oslo Sentralstasjon Tel 22 17 70 30. Telephonic information service. Open every day 7-23 (8-23) (The information office is also open for the public).

Ferries

There are ferry connections to Hovedøya and some islands in the inner part of the Oslofjord. They sail from Vippetangen all the year round. In June and August they also sail to the beaches at Langøyene. From Rådhusbrygge (City Hall Quay) pier 3, there is a ferry connection to Bygdøy from April until September. Sightseeing boats also leave from here in the summer.From Aker Brygge there is a ferry connection to Nesodden all the year round, and to Håøya via Drøbak on summer weekends.

Underground (T)

There are 8 underground lines which converge on Stortinget Station. Stations are sign-posted: T.

Tram

There are 8 tram-lines that run through the city from east to west.

Bus

There are 50 bus routes within the city boundaries. These buses have a route number consisting of 2 figures. The converging point for all the bus routes is Oslo M (Oslo Bus Terminal) of Vaterland - by Oslo Central Station.

Transport from Oslo to Akershus

The Oslo Card in the SL-area
The Oslo Card can be used on NSB's local trains leaving Oslo. Oslo City and Akershus Fylke have close ties with each other, and public transport is coordinated under the designation of "Stor-Oslo Lokaltrafikk" (Greater Oslo Local Services), often referred to as the abbreviation, "SL", on tickets, notices and timetables. The Oslo Card can be used for free transportation throughout "SL" area on NSB, buses, trams, underground (T) and numerous boats and ferries.

Express boat "Prinsessen"
Information Tel 22 17 70 30.
