Wedding Planning in the Virgin Islands

NUPTIALS WITH FLAIR performs weddings on St. Croix, St. John & St. Thomas.

Designed for the discerning bride and groom, Nuptials with Flair creates customized weddings on all three US Virgin Islands.

   Priced competitively with stateside catering facilities in predictable and ordinary surroundings, getting married in the Caribbean is an irresistable option.

  Based on tastes and budget, we provide the wedding party with a selection of options consisting of intimate ceremonies on a secluded island beach, using a fleet of sailboats landing on a private island, sunset ceremony at a historic Great House, 90’ under the sea in a submarine, underwater in scuba gear, in a seaplane to a lovely traditional wedding at a private home or a resort hotel. If your imagination can conjure it, we'll make it happen.
We coordinate guests’ air travel, accommodations, catering, clergy, music and entertainment, florals and honeymoon travel. Location Virgin Islands makes planning your wedding exciting and efficient. Cost- conscious couples can take advantage of off-season accommodation rates from April 15-December 15. Prices are lower and there are a greater range of venues easily obtainable.

Honeymoons

The only thing better than being in love, is being in love in the Caribbean. Silky warm beaches by day, icy cold drinks at sunset and fresh seafood at night. Nature hikes with gourmet picnics, daysails and scuba and snorkeling adventures all within lovely ecological historical surroundings provide the ultimate honeymoon.

Location Virgin Islands works within your budget to provide the most appropriate accommodations, activities and services. From simple back-to-nature camping to deluxe accommodations with ultimate pampering.

Cost-conscious couples should take advantage of off- season accommodations rates available from April 15-December 15. Prices are considerably lower and a greater range of venues are obtainable.
