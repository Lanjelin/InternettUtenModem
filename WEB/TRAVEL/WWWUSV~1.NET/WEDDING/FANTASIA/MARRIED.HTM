Set in tropical splendor of the Caribbean Islands, your wedding ceremony and honeymoon will be the memory of your romantic journey.

Remember - the American Virgin Islands offer much more than beauty and romance. - it's easy to get to, there is no language barrier and you can feel right at home with the assurance that your marriage is protected under U.S. laws.

Our personalized attention to every detail, is geared to island "know how", so you can be assured that your needs are well taken care of to ensure a memorable occasion.

We use only the finest of our island's professionals to provide you with a "hassle-free" wedding day.

By acting as your personal liaison with all court procedures by mail prior to your arrival, all you need to do is arrive! Fantasia will have a personal escort provided to greet you at your ships gangway or hotel lobby to take you to the court to pick up your marriage license then on to the wedding site. (Please bring your photo I.D.s for your appearance before the Clerk of the Court.) After your ceremony is complete, Fantasia will provide a complimentary copy of your Certified Marriage License mailed directly to your home approximately 45-60 days after your wedding date.
