I am extremely pleased that you are considering our beautiful Island Paradise for your Wedding, and I want you to rest assured that my staff and I will do everything we possibly can to assist you in making all of your Wedding Day dreams come true.

Our Weddings are simple yet tasteful events which take place at scenic locations on lovely St. Thomas, St. John, or St. Croix. A phone call is all that is needed to arrange the basics, and those special little extras. Extras like a beautiful Wedding cake and tropical bouquet, champagne and a limo, and the quality photo and video services you need to preserve the memory of your romantic Tropical Wedding day.

Weddings in the Virgin Islands are a fairly simple matter, with no physical exam or blood test required. All that is needed is a completed license application, and an appearance before the Territorial Court. We can provide you with the necessary forms, and make the arrangements for your court appearance as well.
