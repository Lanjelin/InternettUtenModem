Mrs. Estelle Roth of Tropicana Perfume Shoppes, Inc. in St. Thomas, USVI strongly believes that an educated consumer is always the best customer. To that end she encourages customers to understand how to buy perfume:

"PERFUME" - A bottle bearing the designation, "Perfume" contains the highest concentration of fragrant essence (that is, the oil extracted from blossoms and spices) and the lowest concentration of alcohol. That means that the purchaser receives between 15 an 30 percent fragrance extract.

  • "eau de Perfume" contains less essence and more alcohol, with an essence percentage of between 8 to 15 percent.
  • "Eau de Toilette" contains between 4 - 8% essence
  • "Eau de Cologne" - 3-5% essence
  • "Splash Cologne" - contains 3 percent or less fragrance essence
