||Estelle Roth Interview ||Treasure Trove of Classic Fragrances||
||Choosing a Fragrance ||Fragrance Quality ||

When asked what she likes about her work Estelle Roth doesn't hesitate even for a minute. "I like beautiful things," she muses. "I like to be surrounded by beautiful bottles and I love helping people find just what they like."

Tantalized? If so, stop by Tropicana Perfume Shoppes, Inc. and surround yourself with all things -- all scents -- beautiful. You'll find Tropicana on Main St. in St. Thomas, the United States Virgin Islands. Meet Mrs. Roth and her trained staff. Browse and sniff. No one will hurry you. Get to know and love a fragrance that's special for your special person or special just for you!
