The Twenty-third Annual Rolex Cup Regatta

For three days every year the St. Thomas Yacht Club is host to some of the world's most enthusiastic yachtsmen. Their ambition - to carry home the prestigious Rolex Cup.

Ever since its inauguration in 1973, the reputation of the International Rolex Cup Regatta has traveled to all corners of the world. Last year the St. Thomas Yacht Club welcomed yachts from many nations to the beautiful shores of the US Virgin Islands. Participants from Argentina, Venezuela, Norway, Great Britain, Canada, North America, Mexico and Puerto Rico gathered to compete in this keenly contested competition.

Of course, many local competitors from the Caribbean also converge on St. Thomas and over a hundred entries are attracted to the event every year. A variety of classes, combined with exciting and testing courses, ensures competition suited to all.

Together with the stunning locality of the event, it is the unique the unique partnership of Rolex and the St. Thomas Yacht Club that has provided the basis of this regatta. Like those before it, the 1996 event is sure to match the highest levels of excellence that have always been associated with the International Rolex Cup Regatta.
