U.S. Virgin Island's Favorite Camping Grounds on St. John

Camping in America's Paradise

The white sand beaches of the Virgin Islands is not only a beautiful place to visit. You could also camp near the sea and enjoy all the sites and sounds of nature. On St. John, you have a choice to camp at Cinnamon Bay or Maho Bay.

Cinnamon Bay

Camping is restricted on park land to Cinnamon Bay Campground. Accommodations include bare tent sites, sites with tent covered platforms already set up, and cottages. Prepared sites and cottages are equipped with cooking supplies and linens. There is also a camp store with food, other camp supplies, and a cafeteria. Because this site is so popular, reserve well in advance. (Maximum stay is two weeks)

Maho Bay

There is another Campground on St. John -- a private enterprise, at Maho Bay. Maho Bay Campgrounds is privately run camp with "tent cabins" containing cooking supplies. Facilities includes a camp store and central dining area where meals are served.
