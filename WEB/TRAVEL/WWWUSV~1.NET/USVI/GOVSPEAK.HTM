Welcome to the United States Virgin Islands.


    On behalf of the people of St. Croix, St. John and St. Thomas, I want to extend our heartfelt hospitality to you in what is a most exciting time for us.

    As you visit our three islands, you will see evidence of great progress - expansions to our airport on St. Croix, new shops and restaurants and tourist friendly police on many street corners. We are eager to share our excitement about these numerous improvements and additions to our beautiful islands.

    But some things will never change. The natural beauty of our sugar-sand beaches and crystal-blue waters, the many activities that can be experienced on land and in the water, and the unique culture found in our food, music and welcoming people will always be here for your enjoyment.

      Sincerely,
      Roy L. Schneider, M.D.
      Governor, United States Virgin Islands
