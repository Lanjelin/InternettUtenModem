Floral Tour of St. John

Good Day! Welcome the smallest unspoiled hideaway of the U.S. Virgin Islands, St. John. Many say it is the loveliest of the three islands. It measures 28 square miles of velvety mountains and primeval shoreline. In fact about two thirds of the island is National Park watched over by rangers who extend their guardianship to adjacent waters.

Along the way you may see a few of the wild donkeys & mongoose which inhabit the island.
