Amenities


The entry foyer opens onto picture windows contrasting the sapphire blue waters of Magen's Bay to Sand Dollar's cypress ceilings and almond tile floors. The 10 to 12 foot ceiling heights add to the spacious feeling and allow the cool breeze off the bay to circulate throughout the house.

Off the foyer are two large bedroom suites. The master, includes a separate sitting room with chaise lounge for relaxed reading, king size bed, triple dresser, desk, marble bath with whirlpool tub, indoor shower and an outdoor shower on the sunning deck. The second bedroom, has twin beds, double dresser, vanity/desk, loveseat, marble bath, shower, and sunning deck. The third bedroom with private bath is on the main level and has queen size bed, double dresser, and sitting area on the gallery overlooking the pool. Each bedroom is equipped with ceiling fans and individual air conditioner for added comfort.

The living/dining room area is furnished with sectional sofa, loveseat, 25" cable TV with remote control, Sony stereo with cassette and compact disk player, a wet bar, ceiling fans, glass top dining table and four arm chairs. The kitchen is fully equipped with refrigerator, dishwasher, gas range, microwave/convection oven an garbage disposal. A half bath and laundry room are located off the kitchen.

The main level living area expands onto a wide gallery with arches framing the picturesque coastline of the bay. This outdoor space provides lounging, dining and barbeque areas, and overlooks the pool and the deck with several chaises for sunning but also a gazebo for shade.

Wooden steps traverse down the hillside through lush gardens to the expansive beach where the white sand continues on a gentle grade into sparkling clear waters. You can find sand dollars, shells, small reef fish, an occasional sea turtle, and always pelicans and terns diving for minnows. A true bit of tropical paradise in a private villa setting.
