Location


"Sand Dollar", is located on the north shore of St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands in exclusive Estate Peterborg, the peninsula that forms the outer boundary of Magen's Bay with one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. It is only a few minutes drive to Charlotte Amalie with its many shops laden with world treasures. Golf and tennis are two miles away at Mahagony Run. While our constant tradewinds provide unexcelled sailing in deep waters, the islands are also known for world records in marlin fishing. Numerous beaches and reefs are easily accessible to snorkelers and divers but Sand Dollar's crowning jewel is the pristine white sand beach of its own.
