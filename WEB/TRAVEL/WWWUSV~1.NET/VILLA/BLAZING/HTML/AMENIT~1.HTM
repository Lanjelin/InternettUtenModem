ST. THOMAS
 U.S. Virgin Islands

Guests at BLAZING VILLAS are invited to use the facilites and participate in the activites of the Renaissance Grand Beach Resort -- including room service.

Conscientiously decorated with Caribbean flair, each Blazing Villa unit has four spacious bedrooms that can be rented separately or together.

Choose a GARDEN BEDROOM for a great, inexpensive sojourn, or reserve a STUDIO SUITE for maximum Caribbean comfort.  We can make up any accommodation combinations for your family or traveling companions.  We'll even help you plan special events such as weddings or reunions for groups of up to 40 people!

Garden Bedrooms all have private patios, coffee maker, refrigerator and microwave, some have kitchenettes. Your hosts, Marc  Marianne Blazar, have created a comfortable, laid back atmosphere that caters to personal choices and requirements.  We're happy to accommodate our Jewish guests with Kosher housekeeping facilities.  Spacious paths on the property and the ground level setting and/or access ramps of each Blazing Villa unit make this a perfect vacation choice for physically challenged individuals.  There's even a hotel doctor on call at all times.

 We pride ourselves in being gay-friendly and we truly enjoy making an extra effort to accommodate visitors from around the world.
