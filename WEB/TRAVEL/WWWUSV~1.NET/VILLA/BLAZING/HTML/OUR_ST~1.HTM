ST. THOMAS
 U.S. Virgin Islands

Meet your hosts, Marc and Marianne Blazer.  They've lived on St.Thomas for over 12 years where Marc is the owner and operator of Blazing Photos, a one-hour photo processing company, with three locations on St.Thomas

He's also a professional photographer.  Over the last few years, he and Marianne have been able to travel around the world in Marc's capacity as guest photographer with various cultural organizations.  He also conducts photo tours for Princess Cruises.  If you're a photographer, Marc will be happy to share some of his favourite spots or even set up a photo tour for you.

Blazing Villas is particularly proud of their staff.

If you call direct, introduce yourself to Phillip, Blazing Villa's mamager.  He does a great job making sure everything runs smoothly. 

The housekeeping staff epitomizes the warmth and friendliness of Caribbean people.  Venola, Tina, Theresa, Veronica, Carolyn and Bridgit will fill you in on Virgin Island's lore and customs, and provide excellent local-knowledge tips to make your vacations more interesting and enjoyable.

If you're planning a trip to St.Thomas, give Blazing Villas a call or send an E mail.  They're looking forward to hearing from you, and you'll be very happy to have found a wonderful get-away, and an excellent vacation value.
