Sailing the Virgins

Ever since the first tourist, Christopher Columbus, arrived in 1493 the warm clear waters of the Virgin Islands have welcomed cruising sailors.
To truly experience the beauty of the islands, many visitors elect to explore the hundred or so islands islets and cays comprising the Virgin Islands by boat. Be it a an afternoon to evening daysail, or a all inclusive week on a charter yacht, St. Thomas, St. John and St. Croix offer the right combination for every nautical excursion. The countless coves and bays invite modern vessels to explore and enjoy just as they have for the last 500 years.
