Celebrating the New Year with the Boston Pops

New Year's Eve with Boston Pops Traveling Ensemble featuring Rhythm and Brass
Tuesday, December 31, 1996 - $75, $30, $20
Ring in the New Year with a Champagne Buffet and the Boston Pops!
Composed of 14 members of the legendary Boston Pops Orchestra, this Traveling Ensemble teams up with Rhythm & Brass, six virtuosi performing on trumpets, horn, piano, trombone, tuba and percussion. The format of the program is based on that of a traditional Boston Pops concert with the first part being devoted to European classical music and the latter part being devoted to lighter, more "popular" music and jazz.
