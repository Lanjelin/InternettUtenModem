Care Of Pearls

  • Remember that your cultured pearls are precious jewels so treat them with care.
  • Your pearls can become scratched on the hard edges of a jewelry box or on the surfaces of other metal jewelry, so always store them in a cloth bag or wrap them in tissue.
  • Apply your make up, perfume and hair spray BEFORE you put on your pearls. These chemicals are not pearl-friendly and can cause a dull film to form on your pearls.
  • Don't clean your pearls with chemicals or abrasives. In fact it is a part of pearl folk lore that they should be washed in warm water. Don't do it! Water can seep into the hole in the pearl and damage it.
  • Feel free to wipe your peals with a chamois cloth to restore luster.
  • Instead of at-home treatments, bring your pearls to a jeweler once a year. She can clean them if necessary and restring them, preferably by adding a knot between each pearl to prevent loss if the string should break.
