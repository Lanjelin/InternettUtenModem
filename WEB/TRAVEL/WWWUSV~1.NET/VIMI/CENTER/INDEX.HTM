Welcome Aboard

The Charter Boat Center has everything you need to arrange the perfect yachting vacation in the beautiful Virgin Islands. Choose from a variety of Sailing, Poweryachting, and Sportfishing charter options. Located dockside in the heart of the Virgin Islands chartering area since 1975, our staff are most familiar with the local yachts & crew. we have the experience to make your vacation dreams come true!

It's easy and costs no more to book your charter through The Charter Boat Center. Let us take care of all the details so you can spend your time preparing to enjoy our breathtaking scenery and beaches, charming islands, terrific food, world class snorkeling, peaceful starry nights, and warm heavenly days.

So on to those details. Before you know it, you'll be packing a bag with your passport and half as many clothes as you think you'll need while your yacht awaits you at the dock.
