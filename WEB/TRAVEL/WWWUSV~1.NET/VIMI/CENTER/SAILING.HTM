Sailing Charters

There's nothing quite like sailing in the Virgin Islands. The calm inshore cruising area and steady tradewinds make it one of the best sailing destinations in the world. No worries, just you and your friends sliding through the crystal blue waters enjoying the tranquil scenery go by. Choose between Fully Crewed charters, Captain Only charters, or for the experienced sailor, a Bareboat to sail yourself. You can choose from yachts ranging in size from 32 feet to over 85 feet that can accommodate up to 30 guests, packed with amenities and all the comforts to make your sailing vacation a pleasant one.
