THE INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL OF SAILING


For the Experienced Sailor looking to "bareboat" the Virgins, our St.Thomas location is the best jumping off place to visit ALL of the Virgin Islands. We offer new boats at affordable prices.

For the Not So Experienced, But adventurous, we can provide experienced, Coast Guard licensed skippers to lead the way.

For Folks Looking for Full Service, and the sit back and relax sailing experience, we'll find you your own private yacht with captain, chef and the whole nine yards!

Our International School of Sailing offers hands-on live aboard experiences to sailors of all kinds and provides U.S. Sailing Keelboat Certifications.

Our Yacht Ownership Program offers a unique way to own your own boat in paradise, sail free for 4 weeks every year, and build equity.

Our Friendly, Knowledgeable 'On-Island' Staff is here to help you and provide highly personalized service from your first contact to your last. The breezy tradewinds, aquamarine seas, bright happy sunshine, and dancing palm trees await you!!!
