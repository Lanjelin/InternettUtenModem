WTJX TV-12: Virgin Islands Public Television
 We love our local PBS affiliate. WTJX is very active in our community, and has served a major role to bring Internet and a Freenet to our islands. Their latest project is a documentary about one of our most famous native sons, French Impressionist Camille Pissarro.
The University of the Virgin Islands
 Founded in 1962, UVI offers a variety of degrees in the Arts and Sciences on its St. Thomas and St. Croix campuses. See what's happening at UVI - from time to time you can catch an issue of UVIsion, the student newspaper.
