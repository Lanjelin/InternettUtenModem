Come fish the blue waters of the Caribbean aboard the Marlin Prince, the USVI’s own 45’ Viking docked at American Yacht Harbor, Red Hook in St. Thomas. Conveniently situated to the North Drop, the Atlantic Oceans’ deepest point, Marlin Prince is proud to fish the blue Marlin capital of the world. Long recognized by the Billffish Foundation for a release of over a 1000 pound blue marlin, Marlin Price is equipped to handle any sportfishing trip and provides tackle, equipment, bait, refreshments, fresh water and a first class mate for an enjoyable day on the water! Whether you’re fishing for the catch of your dreams or out for a breathtaking island tour—our excursions are limited only by the imagination! So, join the Marlin Prince for a day or half day...whatever your choice we want you to relax…and enjoy…be ready catch The big one….And have a day you’ll never forget!!
