Location

"Many visitors recall St. Thomas by the bustling, intriguing port of Charlotte Amalie ...the Free Port Town...a shame, for there's far more to this dazzling, lovely island..."

Eastwind Condominiums are located on the east end of St. Thomas, on the peaceful side of Red Hook, adjacent to  Vessup Bay beach, and the National Park Service landing.  Between the two, is Latitude 18, a small marina  and bar. Here you'll find local sailors swapping sea stories,  Happy Hours leading to reasonably priced dinners that often include live entertainment.

A five minute walk south brings you to Bluebeards Beach. This undeveloped beach which neighbors the Ritz Carlton St. Thomas, is a favorite for locals especially windsurfers,  and is a popular spot for weekend beach Bar-B-Qs.

A few minutes drive from Eastwind -  is Red Hook, where ferries cast off hourly for the 15 minute Ferry ride to the island of St. John. Charter sailing, sport fishing, snorkel and scuba excursions depart daily from Red Hook's American Yacht Harbor marina. Small power boat rentals are also available. Conveniently, Red Hook has a shopping center with 2 good supermarkets, a drug store, laundromat, bank, post office and several excellent restaurants.
