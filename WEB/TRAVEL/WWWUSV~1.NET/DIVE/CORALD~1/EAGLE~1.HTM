Eagle Shoal, our only 15 minute away off shore dive, is for the experienced open water diver. It is the most amazing dive site on St. John. Depths range from 10-50 ft. At Eagle Shoal, also known as The Cathedral, one can swim through underwater caverns, viewing colorful corals, sponges and tropical fish as well as sleeping nurse sharks. We often see large Eagle Rays at this site..possibly the reason this dive is named Eagle Shoals

It is very much alive , a must see.! We consider this an experienced dive site primarily because the rough sea conditions promote sea-sickness... however if the conditions are calm or if you are an old seahand then you...............

..........................better not even think about missing this spot.
