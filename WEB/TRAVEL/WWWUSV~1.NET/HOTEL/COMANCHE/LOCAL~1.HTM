Local Attractions

The Club Comanche is located in the heart of Christiansted which is itself centrally located along the north coast of St. Croix.

It is a short, scenic stroll from the hotel to many of the finest restaurants on the island. Ferries, beaches, the visitor's center, the Old Custom's House, the market, the Steeple Building, Government House, and Fort Christiansvaern (1749) are all within easy walking distance.

Judith's Fancy, Frederiksted, the Estate Mount Washington Plantation, The Whim Plantation Great House, Victoria House, and the Botanical Gardens are all within a short drive from the Hotel. Golf? Horseback riding? Duty-free shopping? Art galleries? Museums? Crafts? Historical, cultural, and ecological tours? "No problem, mon!"

No other hotel on the island of St. Croix combines such convenience and accessibility with such peace and quiet as the Club Comanche.
