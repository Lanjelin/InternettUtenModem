The Amenities

The Club Comanche is a 42 room hotel located in the heart of historic Christiansted.

It has a large saltwater pool, and all the modern conveniences (air conditioning, Cable TV, bath, etc). Its restaurant and bar are popular with hotel guests, visitors, 'continental' residents, and locals alike.

Its honeymoon suite, located in the romantic 'Old Mill' building overlooking Christiansted Harbor, is world-renowned among newly- weds. Other buildings include the Old Main House, the Cottage, the Bridge House, the Stag House, the Strand House, and the Gate House.
