Club Comanche Centrally Located

The Club Comanche is centrally located in the town of Christiansted which is itself centrally located on the island of St. Croix.

It is a short walk from the hotel to 12 of the finest restaurants on the island. It is only moments away from a ferry which will bring you to one of the finest beaches on the island or you can bypass the ferry and swim to the beach if you prefer. The famous 'Buck Island' excursion sailboats practically leave from the hotel's dock.

Golf? Horseback riding? Duty-free shopping? Historical, cultural, and ecological tours? "No problem, mon!"

The visitor's center, Old Custom's House, the Market Place, the Steeple Building, Government House, and Fort Christiansvaern (1749) are all within a few moments leisurely stroll of the hotel.

Club Comanche: a perfect combination of convenience and accessibility with peace and quiet.
