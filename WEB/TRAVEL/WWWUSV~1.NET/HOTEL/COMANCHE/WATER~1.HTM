Watersports

There are few places on this watery planet which offer a richer diversity of watersports than St. Croix.

Diving, snorkeling, swimming, beachcombing, windsurfing, kayaking, jet-skiing and yacht racing top the list.

The St. Croix Yacht Club is located in Teague Bay and sponsors the world-class Mumm's Regatta in October. A wide variety of watercraft is available for rental locally.
