Diving from The Comanche

The diving around St. Croix is superb.

In fact, its unique wall dives are world famous among experienced divers. Salt River Bay is particularly spectacular with its canyons, walls, and pinnacles. Cane Bay is another dramatic wall dive which features some fantastic coral formations. Of course, the reef at Buck Island is wonderfully pristine because it is protected by the U.S. National Park Service.

The Club Comanche is associated with Dive Experience, one of the best dive operations in the islands. It is a Five Star PADI facility, and located on premises.
