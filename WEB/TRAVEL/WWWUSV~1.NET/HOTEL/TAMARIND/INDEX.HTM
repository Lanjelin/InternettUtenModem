Time is different here . . . in an idyllic oceanfront setting.

With only 46 rooms you are assured of personalized service in an intimate, relaxed atmosphere, the Caribbean the way it should be. Tamarind Reef is St. Croix's newest hotel and, as such, offers all the latest amenities one would expect in a contemporary resort. Each room features either a garden patio, steps from the shelling beach. Or a private balcony overlooking the sparkling Caribbean. Nineteen suites have fully equipped kitchenettes for four people and all accommodations include the following: air conditioning, ceiling fan, telephone, satellite television, clock radio, in-room safe which is actuated by your personal credit card, refrigerator, mini-bar, coffee maker, hair dryer, vanity mirror, bathrobes, irons & ironing boards.

Each morning, enjoy a complimentary light Continental breakfast poolside while deciding on activities for the day. Close to home, one can explore the sea with complimentary watersports equipment.
