Sapphire Beach Resort & Marina announces spring/summer rates

*Book Six Nights and Stay the Seventh on Us*

ST. THOMAS, U.S. Virgin Islands, February 1996 -- Sapphire Beach Resort & Marina, one of St. Thomas' most luxurious beachfront properties, has announced spring/summer rates for 1996, effective April 12 through December 20, 1996. During the months of May, June, September and October book six nights and receive the seventh night free -- that's another whole day for snorkeling, sailing and sunbathing on Sapphire's renowned beach.

Suite:

  • Beachfront: $240 per night
  • Yacht Harbor View $195 pernight

Villa:

  • Beachfront: $280 per night
  • Yacht Harbor View $250 per night

Rates are based on double occupancy, EP plan. Each luxury suite (sleeps four) or villa (sleeps six) features fully equipped kitchens, living rooms private terraces. Add $35 pernight for each additional person. An Map plan (breakfast and dinner) is available at $70 per adult, per night.

Children 12 and under stay, play and eat free when accompanying their parents and can enjoy a day-long complimentary supervised activities program.

For additional information and reservations call 1 800 524 2090.
