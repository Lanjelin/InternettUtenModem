Sapphire Beach Resort and Marina
has a number of 'pure romance' packages.

Honeymoon packages include round trip airport transfers, champagne, Hors d'oeuvres, rum, cut flowers, keepsake, photo album and film, T-shirts, $50 gift certificate, and more. (Of course, this is in addition to all the other complimentary activities and services the resort provides its guests).

Wedding packages range from the simplest of 'cruiseship passenger port weddings' to the most elaborate of specially-designed ceremonies during which the resort's wedding coordinator takes care of everything from the clergy to the cake!
