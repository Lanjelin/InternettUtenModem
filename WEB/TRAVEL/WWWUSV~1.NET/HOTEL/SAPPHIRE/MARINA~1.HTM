Your gateway to the Caribbean

The Sapphire Beach Resort and Marina offers one of the finest marine facilities in the Caribbean.
Located on the western rim of lovely Pillsbury Sound, the marina is convenient to both St. John and the British Virgin Islands. Many of the finest sportfishing boats in world headquarter out of here. Or use the Non-Netscaped
Version of this page.
This modern, protected harbor is virtually hurricane-proof, and features 67 slips with all the conveniences. (Electricity, water, gas, diesel, cable TV, telephone, car rental, taxis, etc). In addition, marina guests have use of the resort's fresh water pool, tennis courts, restaurants, bars, etc. The marina's convenient location to Red Hook Harbor and the St. Thomas Yacht Club makes this an ideal place to reprovision for your down-island cruise.
The marina caters equally to large power yachts and globe-trotting sailing vessels---while maintaining a faithful local customer base because of the its pristine central location and diverse resort amenities.
