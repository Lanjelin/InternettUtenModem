The beach is undeniably the main attraction of the resort. It's the star of the show. It is over a half mile long. Palm-fronded. Floral-scented. Wave-splashed. Warmly wind-swept. Absolutely dazzling.

The 171 unit resort itself is crisp, clean, and clearly professional. The pride of the staff is everywhere; revealed in a multitude of small details and subtle personal services.

The entire layout of Sapphire is laid-back, yet convenient. Comfortable and cozy. It only takes a few moments for the discriminating vacationer to realize that this is one of the best managed resorts in the Caribbean. It is an ideal place to rediscover and reaffirm your 'love of life' again. It is also a perfect spot for romance---and yet it still caters to kids.

Everything seems to conspire to relax you here at Sapphire, the glorious beach, the 1/4 acre fresh water pool and those warm tropical trade winds...

Volleyball? Tennis? Windsurfing? Snorkeling? Sunbathing? Beach combing? Shelling? Swimming? Sportfishing? Sailing? It's all here.

The Sapphire Beach Resort and Marina was designed as the ultimate vacation experience. Each of the suites and villas have stunning ocean views from private balconies, as well as full kitchens and living/dining rooms.

And while it is true that we may never find true perfection in this world, there are certain places... certain tropical moments... certain coconut-scented vacations... that come wonderfully close. Sapphire Beach Resort and Marina is just such a place, and a vacation at Sapphire is as close to paradise as you'll find this side of heaven.

That's why so many of our previous customers rebook, and why so many regular visitors to St. Thomas eventually join us.

Make your next vacation as perfect as possible---make it Sapphire!
