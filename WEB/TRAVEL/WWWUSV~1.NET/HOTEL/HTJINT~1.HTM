A diverse variety of overnight accommodations are offered in St. Thomas and St. John.

In St. Thomas, Lodgings range from the Large Hotels & Resorts offering world class facilities, to Small Hotel & Guest Houses often designed from gracious historical buildings of an era gone by. Modern and convenient Condominiums & Vacation Homes are also available in a wide range of locations and styles.

St. John offers an attractive mix of lodgings ranging from deluxe resorts to campgrounds in and around the National Park.
