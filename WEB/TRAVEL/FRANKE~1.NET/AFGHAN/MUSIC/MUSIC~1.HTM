The Afghan Music CD/Cassette

Listing and Reviews

  • Guldasta-e-Kharabat
    A gem as far as Afghan musical and cultural heritage is concerned. A compilation ranging from Ustad Bereshna, Ustad Shaida, Ustad Qasim, Ustad Rahim Bakhsh, Ustad Yaqub Qasimi, Ustad Ghulam Hossein to Ustad Mohammad Hossein Sarahang. The names of these giants speak for themselves.
  • Ustad Awal Mir
    Ustad Awal Mir's "Da Zemozh Zeba Watan" has become the unofficial national anthem of the Afghans.It is bound to bring tears in the eyes of every patriot.
  • Nashenas
    Nashenas delves deep into "Bekhudee" and with his beautiful voice brings Hafez and Bedal to life. He is brilliant and unique as usual. His music rings TRUE for the current hard times.
  • Haydar Saleem
    Truly the best of Haydar Saleem. A compilation of new songs and some of the timeless selections from the past. Features "Dar Fasal-e-Bahar", "Dukhtar-e-Afghan" and similar GEMS. Brilliant.
  • Nasrat Sharqi
    Nasrat, our ailing musician, song writer and instrumentalist is one of those rare gems that commands instant admiration. A wonderful human being and a professional musician Nasrat's music has a SOUL and timeless beauty that has captivated any audience who had the good fortune of listening to his music.

    For the past three years Nasrat has been confined to bed due to a stroke. Buying Nasrat's music is not only buying a unique testimony to the beauty of Afghan music but also a great help for our brother Nasrat who lies sick and marooned from home in alien corn.
  • Wahed Wafa
    This is a CD which is truly NEW and FRESH. Wahed Wafa does justice to Tarana Saz's music. It is extremely well done and is geared for happy occasions expecially a wedding. Features "Haseena", "Marghola, Marghola" and "Anar, Anar".
  • Ahmad Zaher
    Ahmad Zaher, the King of Afghan popular music, needs no introduction. This is a great compilation of the masterpieces of Ahmad Zaher. "Zendagee-Aakher Sarayad, Bandagee dar kar-o-naist" is prophetic and is sure to send chills down your spine. God Bless our beloved Ahamd Zaher.
