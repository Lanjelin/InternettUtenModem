  • Afghanistan News Service
    The Afghanistan News Service is a home page dedicated to Afghan news. This is your best source for exclusively Afghanistan related news.

  • Dawn Karachi Weekly Digest
    Dawn is one of the oldest and most prestigious paper in Pakistan. Afghanistan is often a topic in this magazine and the editorials and news about Afghanistan are often in depth and thorough. A great resource.

  • Indo Link, Indian News Service
    A major source of news from Indian news services. Its international section and regional section covers Afghan related news. A great resource.

  • Amnesty International News Release
    Amnesty International News release. This page is updated daily. News of Human rights issues and violations in Afhganistan abound in this page.

  • World News
    News from the Clarinet -- the Internet source of world news.

  • Iran's Official News Agency
    Iranian government news agency. News must be taken with a pinch of salt.

  • Cable News Network, CNN
    This is the CNN web page. A great source of the latest news just as you see it on the cable. Do a search for Afghanistan and it will return you a large hit list.

  • South Asian News
    This is a USENET newsgroup totally dedicated to South Asian News which includes mostly Pakistan, India and Afghanistan.

  • Electronic News Stand
    Truly the electronic news-stand for the Internet community. Though not directly related to Afghanistan you may find magazines which have Afgahn related news/essays/analysis in them.

  • U.N News Service
    News and reports published directly from United Nations.
