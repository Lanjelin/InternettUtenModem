Accreditation

 

Forms

The Accreditation forms has been sent to all Federations affiliated to FINA
and shall be returned to the LOC no later than March 15th 1997.

 

Procedure

Accreditation Centres will be set up at the Scandinavium, 5 stations, and
the Scandic Crown, 1 station. The Centres will be open as shows below:

Scandinavium
8th - 11th of April18.00 - 22.00
12 th - 16th of April08.00 - 22.00
17th - 20th of April08.00 - 20.00

This Centre will accreditate:
Press, Teams, LOC and Volunteers,
approx. 3.200 persons.

Scandic Crown
12th - 20th of April09.00 - 21.00

This Centre will aacreditate:
VVIP´s, VIP´s and Sponsors,
approx. 800 persons.

Both Centres can provide Daily Passes.

Since none of the hotels are divided into areas and it is possible
to gain access without a badge, lost badges will be reported
in the morning at the Accreditation Centres.

 

Areas

The Scandinavium has been divided for this Championships in
nine different areas, they will be in force by the 17th of April 08.00
and until the end of the finals. Each day the public will be let in
approx. 08.30, so the areas will be force at 08.00 on each competition day.
