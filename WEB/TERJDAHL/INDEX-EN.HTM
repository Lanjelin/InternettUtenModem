THE NORWEGIAN ADVENTURER AND CLIMATE-REFUGEE TERJE "TERRY" DAHL


SOUTH PACIFIC!

I sailed single-handed from Norway to the Pacific in a 22-feet yacht.
Coco Loco was damaged beyond repair in the Solomon Islands after four years of incredible adventures. I returned to the Pacific after a few freezing months in Norway, by plane this time, and married a chiefs daughter in the Polynesian country of Tuvalu. We built our home on an uninhabited coral-island and lived a happy Robinson Crusoe-life until climate-change with hurricanes forced us away six years later....
