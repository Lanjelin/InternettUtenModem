The Spanky Fractal Database has been around for quite a while now and I thought it was time I presented some of my own images in a more reasonable fashion. I've created a series of linked pages with thumbnails of my favourite fractals. Well okay, my thumbs aren't that big. Lets call them pawprints. They are all hyperlinked to larger 1024x768x256 gif versions. There are a few recent efforts but most of these images were created sometime ago and have bounced around the net in one format or another, either as headers to my fractal pages, or hidden in my directories here at SPANKY or at the Fractal Gallery at CNAM moderated by Frank Roussell. Many thanks to him for his support, both to me and to the fractal community in general.

I've broken these images down into a series of 9 pages with 9 images/page. Each page is linked to the next in sequence or you can get to them directly from here.
