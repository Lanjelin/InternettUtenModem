Specific Fractal Programs at Other Sites

  • An IFS Demo program By Kryachko Y.A. kya@cv.jinr.dubna.su
  • A distributed fractal generator designed for use with the mentat system.
  • The FFFF hicolor fractal generator programmed by Daniele Paccaloni paccalon@ghost.dsi.unimi.it
  • Joris Van Ranst pub00762@innet.be Has sent in a very interesting realtime IFS dynamic display program. IFSIM1 is available from the archive at Spanky. Source included. Or you could try a second version IFSIM2 which has colour and several other new features.
  • Laurens Lapre ljlapre@xs4all.nl has a great 3D L-system package for DOS called LPARSER V.4 available from his webpage dedicated to Lsystems.
  • Darryl House has ported the Fractint code into a nice x/motif version called Xmfract and it is configurable for a number of unix platforms.
  • Nicolas Guerin's xfractal explorer version 1.0 for xwindow systems.
  • The xmntns fractal terrain generator for unix xwindow systems.
  • Reinoud Lamberts has created PostMan. A mandelbrot program designed to create large B/W tiled mandelbrot sets for postscript printers.
  • Generate lyapunov exponents with lyap for unix xwindow systems.
  • A 2D IFS editor that works with Linear and Nonlinear Iterated Function Systems from The Institute of Computer Graphics at the Technical University of Vienna.
  • Ken Shirriff's xwindow port of Fractint version 18.2. xfractint version2.03 from his directory at Berkeley.
  • J.C. Sprott gives us a few free fractal programs.
  • A program for generating Fractal Trees from Phil Drinkwater at Loughborough University of Technology.
  • A Unix L-systems interface called lsys available at U.N.C.
  • Stephen Booth at the University of Edinburgh created xmountains. A nice fractal terrain generator.
  • A Mandelbrot generator and a Lyapunov generator for NEXT systems.
  • Peter Johansson's Fractal Studio is a distributed network fractal program under development.
  • A fractal compression program called limbo available from Carsten Frigaard: cfri91@vision.auc.dk in Denmark.
  • A PhotoShop add-on for the Mac called Expression from Jim Bumgardner.
  • A program called 3DFract for IBMPC's from Brent Hugh.
  • Michael Peters and Randy Scott have made available their HOP "Fractals in Motion" program for IBMPC's.

Note:

I have many Fractal codes and executable programs available at Spanky, but not all. I can't keep up with all of the new releases, updates etc. so I will try to keep valid pointers to alternate sites. More of these links will become available as I verify them. Also note that most of these links are through FTP or GOPHER and your web client may not be configured to navigate these spaces directly. Email me if you know of any specific locations for good fractal programs or large repositories of fractal software.
