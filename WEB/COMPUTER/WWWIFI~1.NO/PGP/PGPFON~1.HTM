PGPfone - Pretty Good Privacy Phone

What is it?

PGPfone (Pretty Good Privacy Phone) is a software package that turns your desktop or notebook computer into a secure telephone. It uses speech compression and strong cryptography protocols to give you the ability to have a real-time secure telephone conversation.

PGPfone is currently in beta test version. It is available for both Macintosh (1.0b7) and Windows 95/NT (1.0b2).
