We will make printable versions of the Internet Entertainment Charts, available for download. You can then print the charts yourself, in a nice lay-out.  
You will be able to see the number of voters for each of our charts over a long period of time. It's interesting to see how the charts are developing, and which of our lists are doing best.  
There will be a small list of the best titles ever made. The titles in this list will be judged by their performance on the charts.  
Lists of the newest and best performing titles from the charts. With a small list of 10 or 20 entries it is easier to recognize the titles that are the best new hits at this moment, without all the older titles getting in the way.

A completely new set of algorithms for even better weekly charts. It will be next to impossible for people to sabotage our lists and stuff the ballot. All the things we do manually now will then be automated.

Anonymous voting, without the need for an email address (with restrictions).  
Lists and charts of most frequent voters.

Introduction of the seventh weekly chart.
