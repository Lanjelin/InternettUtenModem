We are interested in any kind of advertising or sponsoring. The Internet Entertainment Charts are a free and very popular service for the Internet community. To keep our service alive, and introduce more features and possibilities, we need funding or other financial support.   
Website    
  Our website currently receives 30.000 - 35.000 visitors per month. This includes the mirrors on the other continents. Since all our charts are updated every week, our visitors have a good reason to keep coming back. Our Internet Entertainment Charts website has been awarded several times for originality, news-value and lay-out.
Possibilities:    
  We can carry your logo and a link to your website on the left side (frame) of our website. It will then always remain visible.
  We can include a banner and a link to your website at the top of our Top 100 charts, which are the pages most visited by our readers.
  We can include a line at the bottom of all the pages, carrying the text "sponsored by ...", possibly with a logo.
Each of these possibilities has its own price, which is a fairly small one, since we are just volunteers trying to keep this free service running. As long as we can pay for our expenses, we're happy to make any reasonably interesting deal.  
Newsgroups/Mailing lists    
  Text versions of our games, movies and music charts are cross-posted to many dozens of relevant newsgroups all over the world. Since the information in our postings is interesting and free for everyone, our readers do not mind some third-party announcements being included in these postings.
  Voters for our charts receive a copy of the text version in their mailbox. There are 4000 - 5000 people on our mailing list.
  There are 80 - 100 paper magazines, electronic magazines, websites, bulletin boards, newspapers and mailing lists distributing our charts. Most of them copy the entire text version of our charts, including any third-party messages.
Possibilities:    
  We can carry your announcement (in ascii) in the text version of our charts. It will then be distributed to many dozens of newsgroups, to our mailing list and it will be copied by our distributors for their own readers. Your announcement will reach tens of thousands of readers all over the world.
  We can include a line at the top of the text version, carrying the text "sponsored by ...".
Each of these possibilities has its own price, which is a fairly small one, since we are just volunteers trying to keep this free service running. As long as we can pay for our expenses, we're happy to make any reasonably interesting deal.
