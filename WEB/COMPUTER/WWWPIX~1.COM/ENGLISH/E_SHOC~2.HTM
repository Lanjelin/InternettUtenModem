Free shockwave files from Pixelwings -- we supply you with more fun!

Here you will find freeware Shockwave files, that you can download and add to your own pages.
These files are thought of as decorations in the first place, that is, they are not always especially intelligent -- but they are especially small (on the average between 5 and 12 k).
Simply click on the respective button in order to look at the files and to download them.
On the pages you will find the appropriate instructions about how to add the files to your own pages.

If you want to use our files, please send us an e-mail with the URL of your pages. Of course we are delighted about every link to our site (http://www.pixelwings.com) -- resp. a link to our site is already integrated into these files.
