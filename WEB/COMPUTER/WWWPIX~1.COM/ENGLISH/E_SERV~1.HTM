Welcome to the data jungle! To the amazing world of bits and bytes, the Mecca of abbreviations and pc-jargon, ampersands and insecurity -- in short -- Welcome to the Internet!

YOU'VE GOT THE LOOK
Many questions have to be answered, if you don't want your entry to the Web to be the act of a tightrope walker without a net. Is it too early, or just the right time, or is it at all necessary for your company to go on the Web? How much does it cost? What are the possibilities and what can you do to at least win by a nose electronically against the competition? We can answer these questions -- knowing full well that in realizing an idea, the design has the highest priority. Just as in other fields, there is something special about the Internet: the first impression is the most important, and there is never a second chance. If someone looks at your homepage and is bored, escape is possible with the push of a button and that person never looks again.

A few more details in passing: If you wish, we can arrange the service provider for you, and take care of the formalities. All the sites that we produce are automatically registered at all the most important search engines at home and abroad. We offer reasonably priced update packages to keep your site up-to-date.

In developing solutions for the Internet, first the concept, then the design, and then the technical realization should be considered and carried out -- and not in the reverse order, as is so often the case. But it would be best if you look at a few examples yourself.
