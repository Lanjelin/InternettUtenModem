Holobar, the Atrilian Campaign

Gaming InformationDescription
Game Master: Gianni Vacca
Gaming System: AD&D with home rules
Players: 20 needed, 16 present
Number of Turns: 1 turn a month
Campaign Description: Diplomacy, every player plays the role of a king, duke, or sultan and struggles to conquer his neighbors. Alternatively, one may play a guild master, high priest, carvan master, etc.
Other Information: The setting is heavily Arabian Nights/African oriented, with some Planescape incursions.
