                                 Grandel

       The city of Grandel lies on the bay of Hernason. Busy merchants line it's streets going about their buisness. The city's main enconmy revolves around the trade the the port enables. Despite the being the melting pot the city is, the natives tend to be bitter and depressed about the war. The city has also been stricken with a terrible crime wave, and flocks of theives take refuge inside it's walls. They've established a guild in the corner of the city, much to the despair of the mayor.
