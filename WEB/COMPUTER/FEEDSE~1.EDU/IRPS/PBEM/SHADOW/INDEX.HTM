The land of Hendar has been taken over by an evil force. Evil that the people living there have never before known. Death and destruction has taken over the land. Societies have crumbled, and chaos has taken over. A dark lord rules over the land. Many people would rather die than live under his tryanical rules, and some do. But a ray of hope has sprouted from amongst the shadows. A small band of adventurers have decided to do change things back to the way they had been.
