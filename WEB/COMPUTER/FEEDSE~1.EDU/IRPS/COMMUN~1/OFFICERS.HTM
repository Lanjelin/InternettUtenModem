IRPS currently has a chain of command in which work is passed up and down. This is done for efficiency. Below are a list of current IRPS officers and with their current titles along with their main staff members should you need to contact any of them. Titles which do not have names next to them are currently open to members who can demonstrate that they are capable of holding such a position. Also, IRPS has not accounted for every possible position in the world, so if there's one which you think IRPS could use, feel free to contact the President at any time with your suggestion.
