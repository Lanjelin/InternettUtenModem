Club Information

Welcome to the Internet Role-Playing Society's Homepage. We are a club, dedicated to the advancement of role-playing over the Internet. We seek to offer services and information to our club members, free of charge. The only thing we ask is that our members actively participate in club activities, all which can be accomplished with a touch of the button.

Club Benefits

The Internet Role-Playing Society has several benefits to offer its members, all of which come from it's members themselves and businesses and companies which IRPS is associated with. The benefits range from a user database with geographical information concerning all members to a monthly newsletter written by IRPS members to be published.

Free Membership

If you are interested in becoming a member of the Internet Role-Playing Society fill out the following form on the link above. The information on the form is strictly confidential except your general location (state, province, nation, etc) unless you otherwise specify. Membership is free but we do ask all of our members to participate in the club.
