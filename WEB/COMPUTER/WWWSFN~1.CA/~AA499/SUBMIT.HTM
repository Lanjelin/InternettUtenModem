How to Submit to The Gallery Of Magical Blades

There are several catagories in The Gallery Of Magical Blades, and if you could, I would appreciate it if your submission fits into one of these catagories (special exceptions are made for blades that need a catagory of themselves). The catagories are:

Bastard Swords, Broad Swords, Daggers, Katanas, Knives, Kopeshes, Long Swords, Rapiers, Sabers, Scimitars, Short Swords, Two-Handed Swords

Please, if you are to submit a blade that requires tables, it helps if it's typed up in Microsoft's Word for Windows (version 2.0 or less) as it saves me a great amount of time in formatting.
