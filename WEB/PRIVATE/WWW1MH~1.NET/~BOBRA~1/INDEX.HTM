Who is Doctor Bob?

Bob Rankin, known as "Doctor Bob" in the online world, is a writer, computer programmer and consultant who enjoys exploring the Internet and sharing the fruit of his experience with others.

Doctor Bob is a columnist for Yahoo! Internet Life, Boardwatch Magazine, and has also been published in ComputerWorld, NetGuide and other computer magazines. One of Bob's current projects is The Internet TOURBUS. Come along and join 80,000 others on this ongoing tour of Internet hot spots...
