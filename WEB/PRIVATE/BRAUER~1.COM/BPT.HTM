Ballpark Tours, Inc. was founded by bleacher-dwelling Twins fans in 1982 as Save the Met, to avoid the hermetically sealed, placticized menance Twins fans now lived with for 14 years (welcome to the fight, Carl Pohlad!).

Of course, we were squashed flatter than home plate...Anyway, since local fans couldn't see outdoor major league baseball, we decided to take fans to outdoor major league baseball -- and Ballpark Tours, Inc. was born.

Since the early '80s, we have taken fans to the best outdoor stadiums in America -- Wrigley Field, Tiger Stadium, County Stadium, Camden Yards, and Old Comiskey Park (R.I.P., dear friend...). Each year, we renew the love affair that baseball owners do their best to violate -- real fans, going to real ballparks, with real grass, paying realistic prices! That is our mission! These are our trips!
