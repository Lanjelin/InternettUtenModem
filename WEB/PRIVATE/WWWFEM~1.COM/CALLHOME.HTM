Beginner's Guide to HTML - A complete introduction to the HTML language with all the features you need. If you want to learn how to make your own homepage this is a good place to start.


Angelfire - Get your own free website at Angelfire.com. Unfortunately, you are not allowed to upload pictures here, and you have to do all of your editing online.


Geocities - The best place to get a free website out of those currently available. Geocities will let your upload pictures, too but the URL can be a bit hard to remember.


HoTMaiL - Get a free e-mail account. Hotmail is web-based which means that you can access it from all over the world using any web-browser.


Starmail - Are you tired of some silly e-mail that people can't remember? If you have one already, you can get the coolest name you want.. choose between a bunch of celebrities and other fun addresses.


Bigfoot - Get an e-mail address that called yourname@bigfoot.com.. not much of a choice but this way, you won't have to change your e-mail address again. But I love the graphics on this page!
