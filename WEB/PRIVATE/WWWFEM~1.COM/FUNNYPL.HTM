Lego's Website - I used to love playing with Lego when I was a kid and I think their website is really cute. A great place for kids to go.


Team Holm - A list with links to Danish women on the net. You are very welcome to vote for me if you would like! You need my e-mail address to vote for me; it's sofie@femalenerd.com.


Internet Bookshop - Shop for books online and order them home, 912.000 titles to choose from. Great for Christmas shopping if you need to find something fast.


Disney's Website - With links to all your favourite Disney movies and a bunch of other stuff. If you're a Disney fan don't forget to bookmark this site.


Virtual Flowers - This is a wonderful service if you want to send a virtual bouquet to someone you know would appreciate it and you can also shop for real flowers with your credit card. (That's up to you and your wallet to decide!)


Greenpeace - I think they're doing a wonderful job in trying to save the environment and I'm also a member of Greenpeace myself. Their website is really nice, too.


Hollywood Online - Read about the latest movies, awards, download soundtracks and explore backstage. Great for movie freaks. (But unfortunately, I'm not one of them!)


Dilbert - One of my favourite, nerdy cartoon characters! Get your daily Dilbert cartoon here. Many people find the situations described in Dilbert cartoons to be frightingly realistic.


Garfield - I like Garfield even better than Dilbert.. he's my absolute favourite and I have every cartoon album issued. And his website is just adorable.. Big, fat hairy cats rule!


Sony Online - Sony's website with information about music, television, movies, video games and electronics. Okay layout but too heavy on graphics and not enough content!


Virtual Presents - Oops, Christmas is coming up, you're broke and your love says she wants a diamond ring.. so what do you do? Get her a virtual one! Here you can get anything from a cup of coffee to a trip to Hawaii and it's all free.


UNF - Youth Association of Science. For all science-interested people in Denmark! Foreigners can check out the English version. I'm a member of the web group that designs their homepage and we've got big plans...


Giggles - Tons of jokes, smileys and everything else you need to get in a good mood on a rainy day.. Visit this girl's website if you want to find yourself rolling on the floor laughing.
