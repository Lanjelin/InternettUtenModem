Check out a few of my favorite watering holes in Pittsburgh.
The Attic -- Forbes Avenue/Oakland -- 682-2050
Wednesday night is dollar night, all drinks are one dollar.

Chiodo's Tavern -- West 8th Avenue/Homestead -- 461-3113
Get a taste of the Pittsburgh steel industry at Chiodo's Tavern.

Fat Head's South Shore Saloon -- Carson Street/Southside -- 431-7433
Fat Head's has over twenty different beers on tap as well as a large selection of bottled imports and microbrew beers. Fat Head's is also home to the Volcano Wings, some of the hottest wings you'll find in the Burgh. You have to sign a disclaimer before eating them!

Mario's South Side Saloon -- Carson Street/Southside -- 381-5610

Silky's Saloon -- Murray Avenue/Squirrel Hill -- 421-9222
For those hungry people among us, Silky's makes an excellent Cajun chicken sandwich.

Panther Hollow Inn -- Forbes Avenue/Oakland -- 682-9999
Panther Hollow Inn, better known to some as PHI, is a Carnegie Mellon tradition.
