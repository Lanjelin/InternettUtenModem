There are plenty of things to do around Pittsburgh, below are just a few.
Andy Warhol Museum -- Sandusky Street/Southside -- 237-8300

The Carnegie Museum of Art -- Forbes Avenue/Oakland -- 622-3131

Carnegie Science Center -- Alleghany Avenue/Northside -- 237-3335
Check out the laser light shows held there every Friday and Saturday nights. Previous shows have included music by Pink Floyd, Zeppelin, U2, Nine Inch Nails and many others.

Heinz Historical Center -- Smallman Street/Strip District -- 454-6000

Kennywood -- Kennywood Boulevard/West Mifflin -- 461-0500
Ride the historic merry-go-round; enjoy the thrill on one of the four rollercoasts, three wooden and one steel; get drenched on the Pittsburgh Plunge or the Raging Rapids; and eat the fries from the Potato Patch. At Kennywood, how can you not have fun!

Three Rivers Stadium -- Northside -- 323-1200
Three Rivers Stadium is the place for you sports buffs. During the football season watch the Steelers on their way once again to the Superbowl. Then baseball season brings out the Pirates, GO BUCS!.
