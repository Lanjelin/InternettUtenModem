Despite the almost unbearable heat, Pietro Casiglioni's annual gathering at the Reading Pennsylvania Country Club brought out some unusual sights. Everything from a historic 1953 375 MM Spyder to the technologically advanced F50 were present. As always, the Reading Ferrari Concours d'Elegance featured a different model, this year being the 246 Dino. Following the annual dinner was the awards ceremy where wood mounted yellow Dino models were presented as trophies. All owners in attendance who brought their Ferrari were also given a beautiful yellow 246 Dino print to remember the event.

Three white tents surrounding the concours offered various Ferrari memorabilia, models, books, posters and prints to enthusiasts attending the event. Directly in front of the three tents sat Leonard M. Rusiewicz's beautiful 1953 375MM Pininfarina Spyder, s/n 0370AM, which he had just recently restored.

Following the concours at 3:00pm, the customary dinner and awards ceremony was held inside the country club, which we were unfortunately unable to attend since we had to head back to Pittsburgh.
