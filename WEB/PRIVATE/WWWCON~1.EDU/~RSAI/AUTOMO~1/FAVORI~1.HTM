For those who are interested in automobiles, I have composed a list of several of my favorite automotive related sites below. I also have included a short description of each site to make surfing for what you want easier.

Ferraris.Com: The Ferraristi's Resource
http://www.ferraris.com/
The best online resource for the enthusiast of Ferrari creations from the 80's. Find expert advice on buying a used Ferrari for first time owners, information on maintaining your machine from Maranello and an extensive list of sources for all things Ferrari.

European Ferrari Magazine
http://www.europeanferrari.com/
Particularily suited for the Ferrari enthusiasts on the other side of the pond, this magazine explores events about Ferrari.

Forza Ferrari! Canada
http://www.specialcar.com/ferrari/
Check out Forza Ferrari for detailed information on many of the road going and race cars producd by Ferrari. You can also find Ferrari racing news, schedules and information as well as the Northern Prancer, the FCA East & Central Canadian region quarterly newsletter.
1974 Ferrari Daytona Spider
Prova
http://www.prova.com/
Published by Guy Mangiamele of Cavallino magazine, Prova is the latest Ferrari site on the web. More like a magazine than a website, it features great graphics, regular articles, and classified advertisements.

The Official Ferrari Website
http://www.ferraris.it/
The official Ferrari Factory Web site contains a broad spectrum of information for Ferrari enthusiasts including the latest press releases, model information, official club listings and the newest official merchandise. Also online is full race coverage of Formula 1, Manufacturer's World Championship, IMSA and Challenge series.
Car & Driver Magazine
http://www.caranddriver.com/
The popular newstand magazine now has its own homepage. Find the latest car news and reviews, event information and read selected articles from the current issue. Interested in buying a new car? Check out their complete guide to new cars and trucks.

 Autoweek Online
http://www.autoweek.com/
For those who can't wait a full month for the latest automotive news the place to check is Autoweek. The weekly sourc efor road tests, inside information, and motorsports coverage is now available electronically 24 hours a day.

Ferrari Market Letter Online
http://www.ferrarimarketletter.com/
The invaluable resource for the Ferrari enthusiast and purchaser is now available online.
