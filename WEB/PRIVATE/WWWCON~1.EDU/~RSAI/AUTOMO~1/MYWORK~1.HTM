1996 Reading Ferrari Concours
A hot May Afternoon at the Reading Country Club provided a small-scale look at some of Maranello's best. The featured model for this year was the 246 GTB/GTS Dino.

1996 Pittsburgh Vintage Grand Prix
A collection of photographs from the week long events and two days of racing held in Pittsburgh, PA during the summer. COMING SOON!

1996 Monterey Historic Races
Photographs from Friday and Saturday, the first two days of the three day long event in mid-August. Everything could be seen from four of the BMW art cars to Formula 1 racing and hot laps of the McLaren F1. COMING SOON!

1996 Concours Italiano
Nothing but Italian cars at this event, Bertone was the featured Carrozzeria for 1996. This year was the first annual Italian style runway fashion show sponsored by Saks Fifth Avenue. COMING SOON!

1996 Pebble Beach Concours
The creme de la creme of automobile events, located on the 18th hole of the world famous Pebble Beach golf course. The 46th annual Pebble Beach Concour d'Elegance featured 100 spectacular automobiles with the featured marques being Lincoln and Carrozzeria Zagato. COMING SOON!
