My main interests and hobbies...

Besides caring for the sweetest leopard gecko in existance (aka Jasper, see GeckoCam) I enjoy a multitude of other activities, such as:

  • Horseback riding
    I began riding regularly about eight years ago when I started attending the riding camp at Camp Hantesa. I fell in love with the sport and began taking private lessons when I was about 13. I ride english, mostly hunter-jumper, but I've also had some training in dressage. I don't ride now as much as I'd like, but I still love the sport and consider it one of my favorites.
  • Cross country running
    This is really a rather masochistic sport, and I'm not even sure myself why I enjoy putting myself through this sort of pain. But I really do love having the endurance to run six miles straight and being able to say I actually I have some solid muscle in my legs. What I love even more are the friends I make on the cross country team and the endless fun we always manage to have.
  • Music
    I've been playing the french horn since fifth grade, and I truly believe it is one of the most beautiful instruments in the world. In September of 1995, I purchased my first horn: a gorgeous silver-nickel Holton H179 double horn. I named her Angeline and she is quite simply one of the most wonderful things in my life. I also play a little piano and love listening to a wide variety of music.
  • Surfing the Internet
    Since you're visiting this page, I have a feeling you understand this little addiction of mine. I don't surf around as much as I used to or as much as I'd like to, but I love how I always come across the neatest things.
  • Writing letters for Amnesty International
    If I'm not spending a large portion of my time helping those who need help, I feel useless and pathetic. I write letters for Amnesty International because I admire their organization, and I realize there are many violations of human rights everywhere in the world; I can't let myself just watch it happen if there's something I can do about it.
  • Skiing
    This includes downhill and cross country skiing, but downhill is my greatest passion. Utah ranks among my favorite when it comes to top ski resorts! I've also enjoyed hitting the slopes at Breckenridge, Keystone, Copper Mountain, Arapahoe Basin, and Lake Tahoe. Unfortunately, Iowa doesn't have much in the way of mountains, and the midwest in general is just stuck with big hills. When I can't get out to the mountains, I usually hit the slopes in Minnesota or settle for some nice, relaxed cross country skiing in the park behind my house.
  • Belly dancing!!
    Belly dancing has always fascinated me, but it wasn't until September '95 that I finally acted upon this interest. I took an 8-week course at our local center for the arts, learned the basics of belly dancing moves, and absolutely fell in love with the art! It's a great way to relieve stress, keep in shape, and show off your stomach! In the fall, my school newspaper printed an article about people taking this belly dancing class. There's a great quote by me in there!
