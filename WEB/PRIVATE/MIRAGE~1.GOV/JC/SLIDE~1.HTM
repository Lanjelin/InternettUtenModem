My
European Vacation
of June 1995

On 9 June, 1995, I left with a group of five from Ames, Iowa to see the countries of England, France, and Italy. Our tour included stops in London, Paris, Florence, Assisi, Rome, Pompeii, and Capri. This is my wonderful slide show!

While viewing, you do not need to do anything to change the slides. It's a slide show, kick back and relax! The slides change automatically about every 10 seconds. (If the time doesn't seem reasonable to you, let me know what amount of time you would prefer.)
