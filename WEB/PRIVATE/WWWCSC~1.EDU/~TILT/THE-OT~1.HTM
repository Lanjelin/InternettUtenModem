As with any space, there is that which is within, and that which is without. Here, formally, I present that which is other than my space.

Of course, the nature of hyperspace is such that the interface between the self and the other is a hopeless mush, as secondary links lead from almost every page within this corpus into the dangerous outside world. Still, here is a launching pad presented for your use; a place to build up escape velocity from my artificial orbit and to bounce back into that strange web of tangled lies and imagemaps we've weaved.
