Euphonium People

This page lists various people who either play or are otherwise involved with the euphonium, and have asked me to list their URL here.

Andrew Lamb
Andrew lives in Tasmania, and plays euphonium for the City of Launceston Brass Band. He has also played with the University of Tasmania Wind Ensemble and the Australian National Youth Band.
Karen Key Speck
Euphonium palyer with the Orcas Island Community Band, Orcas Island, Washington.
Luke Spiros
Luke Spiros is the principal euphonium and featured soloist with the Spires Brass Band.
David Werden
David Werden was the euphonium soloist with the US Coast Guard Band for more than 20 years.
Alastair Wheeler
Alastair Wheeler plays piano, trombone and euphonium, and is a student at the New College at Oxford. (Of course, "new" means it was founded in 1379 CE, don't ya know. :) He's second euphonium of the National Youth Wind Orchestra of Great Britain.
Douglas Yeo
Douglas Yeo is the bass trombonist for the Boston Symphony Orchestra.
