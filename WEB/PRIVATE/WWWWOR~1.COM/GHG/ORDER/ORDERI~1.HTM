R-406A in the US is EPA SNAP acceptable for most uses (retrofit) in "fixed" refrigeration and cargo transport refrigeration. It is also approved for some categories of "new manufacture". Contact us if you have a new manufacturing use for R-406A.

R-406A is CURRENTLY EPA ACCEPTABLE for mobile air conditioning in the United States (cars, tractors, aircraft, etc). (effective 11/15/96, see Federal Register Oct 16, 1996).

GHG-X4 has also gone through the EPA SNAP approval process, it is now LEGAL to sell and use for both stationary and automotive uses (special fittings required for automotive only)

All "end users" (i.e. retail sales) must have proper EPA technician certification on file (fax in copy of card) in order to purchase R-406A or GHG-X4. Part 608 "stationary" certifications (Type I, Type II or "Universal") or mobile MVAC (part 609) certifications are allowed to purchase R-406A (after 11/15/96 for Mobile).
