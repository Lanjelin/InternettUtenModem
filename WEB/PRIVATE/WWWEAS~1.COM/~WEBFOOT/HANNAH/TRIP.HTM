20,000 Miles Traveled


Portland to Shanghai

Shanghai to Hangzhou

Friday afternoon, took the train to Hangzhou where we were to meet Hannah. 3 hour train ride.

Hello, Hannah!

Hangzhou to Guangzhou

Guangzhou to Portland

One week later, began the 36-hour trip home. Arrived the afternoon of the day we left (crossed the international date line again)
