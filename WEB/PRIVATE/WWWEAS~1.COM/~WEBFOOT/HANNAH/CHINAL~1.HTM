Chinese Cultural and Language Resources

In an attempt to maintain awareness of Hannah's cultural heritage, we have found a number of resources on the Internet to assist us. Here are some of them. If you know of others that might be useful, please let us know.

Families with Children from China This is a national page with abundant resources and information on adopting a child from China.
Portland, Oregon Families with Children from China This is a local page which lists activities and resources in Portland and SouthWest Washington by families with adopted Chinese children.
Chinese Language-Related Information Page If you are interested in knowing more about Chinese language, just curious or want to learn some, this is a good place to start..
Chinese Software Digest is a listing of Chinese related software packages available from the Web.
