The Arts - Your Online Gallery to the World
Provider: The Arts Company

This self-described "gallery to the world" is nothing if not ambitious. While it may never do justice to the vast range of arts and entertainment categories its author is planning, it does have dozens of interesting pointers under many headings, including some that you might not stumble onto otherwise. Current link lists include visual and performing arts, photography, talent agencies, book publishers, and music labels, with myriad other categories such as comedy, computer art, authors, models, and stamps slated to appear soon. This is absolutely worth a browse, and probably a bookmark.
