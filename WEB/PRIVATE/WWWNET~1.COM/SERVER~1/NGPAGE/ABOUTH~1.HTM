How to advertise on our site
 		How to advertise on our site

Netguide Live offers flexible advertising options designed to expose your ad banner to a large, targeted audience. Net rates are based on banner positioning and monthly impressions. All ads on NetGuide Live are hot links to your site or a co-branded promotion page.

Contact information

Run of NetGuide Live
Banners are rotated across pages throughout the entire NetGuide Live site, within the directory, on the search page and search results page, on the Live Events and on editorial pages. Your ad will be rotated to get the exact number of impressions you want, in the time frame you require.

Fixed pages
Banners may be placed on specific pages in NetGuide Live for highly targeted advertising.

Search words
Banners may be presented with the results of searches for specific words to directly target special interests.

Other Banner locations:

Browsing Categories

Top-level category
Ads appearing when people click on top level of category topics in Browse pages.

Second-level category
Ads appearing on secondary category pages when people click on subtopics beneath the main Browse topics.

Banner guidelines

Banner dimensions and resolution
Banners are 476 pixels wide by 54 pixels high and are to be at 72dpi resolution. The maximum file size is 12 kb.

Graphics requirements
Banners will be hyper linked to an Internet address (URL) of your choice. Please send your ready-to-post banner image in Graphics Interchange Format (using the naming convention "yourfilename.gif") format to NetGuide Live by attaching it to an e-mail message to ads@netguide.com or FTP to: edit.netguide.com (ask your salesperson for the password). In the subject field type your company name. In the body include the title of the ad, the URL, and a 50 Character tag line or ALT text that will accompany the ad.

Submission guidelines
Send to: ads@netguide.com. or FTP to: edit.netguide.com (ask your salesperson for password).

Prices, terms and conditions subject to change without notice.

Advertising inquiries
