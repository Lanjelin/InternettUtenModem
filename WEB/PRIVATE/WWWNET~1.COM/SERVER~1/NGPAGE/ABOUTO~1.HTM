What's On Now
Here you'll find Live Events, NetGuide Live's daily, searchable calendar of hundreds of live Web happenings, including sports, concerts, talk shows, news programs, and more. To make your choices easier, our editors offer daily viewing and listening recommendations. What's On Now also features the Tools You'll Need section, in which you can learn about and download the audio and video applications that'll make your browser fly.

Explore
NetGuide Live's most happening corner is filled with Net ambience. Every day, the Spotlight articles focus on news and phenomena through the lens of new Web sites. The List Guy describes several sites on a related subject, be it his favorite vice or his religion (are they one in the same?). And our New Sites column offers direct access to the Web's best new locations.

Search and Browse
Web surfers can search by topic for detailed, insightful profiles of more than 50,000 sites in our "Best of the Web" catalog. Or, they can search for information across the entire Internet using our index of tens of millions of destinations.

Net Know-how
This cyber manual helps both Net aces and newbies get the most out of their time online. Our Internet Glossary helps users learn Net lingo. Our Snap Guides take visitors through quick lessons on using the Internet for practical pursuits: shopping, investing, finding a job, etc. NetCoach answers your questions, from basics, like logging onto e-mail, to more complex conundrums, like creating and maintaining a Web page.

News/Reference
Here you can cross the bridge to the real world. Constantly updated CNN news, business, weather, sports, and entertainment reports, links to CMP Media's TechWeb site, and easy access to the Big Book yellow pages directory await.
