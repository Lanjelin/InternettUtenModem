 		NetGuide Live team

Robert Seidman
Project Director
Robert, editor at large and columnist for our sister print publication, NetGuide magazine, is leading our editorial, technical and marketing teams to the goal of establishing NetGuide Live as the Internet's premier guide resource. A pioneer electronic journalist, Robert has developed one of the online world's most authoritative and popular newletters, Seidman's Online Insider. The Insider grew out of Robert's work on news-filtering technology for MCI. Before joining CMP, he was also product development manager for IBM's InfoSage news-filtering and -delivery service.

David Pollak
Chief Technical Officer
David runs NetGuide Live's technical group and and is responsible for developing technology used in the creation and transmission of our site. Prior to joining NetGuide Live, David was cofounder of Lomax Publishing, an Internet start-up that produced a personalized interactive magazine delivered over the Web. Prior to Lomax, David was president of Athena Design, maker of the best-selling, award-winning Mesa spreadsheet application and objects.

Michael Schroeder
Director of Operations
As Director of Operations for Netguide Live, Mike is responsible for administrative and financial management. Mike joined CMP in 1992 to spearhead efforts in desktop publishing and production automation. He has supervised development and operations of CMP's TechWeb, the premier technical site on the Web, and has led Internet initiatives as director of publishing technology since 1995. Before coming to CMP, Schroeder was an editor and editorial technology manager for Newsday, the Orange County Register and the Los Angeles Herald Examiner. He graduated from the University of Southern California and is completing work on his MBA from Long Island University.
