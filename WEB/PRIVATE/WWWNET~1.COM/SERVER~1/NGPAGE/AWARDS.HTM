Don't miss the first NetGuide Awards, NetGuide's first showcase honoring the most innovative and interesting Internet products and Web sites worldwide. All nominations are based on the thousands of suggestions we received from our readers. Thanks to everyone who participated! Even if you missed the nomination process, you can still make your voice heard by voting in the People's Choice category. The awards are generating such a high level of enthusiasm that voting will be extended until Friday, Feb. 28, at 11:59 PM (PST) -- so cast your vote now!

The awards ceremony will be held March 13 at the El Rey Theater in Los Angeles. We'll bring it to you with live video, audio, and an online chatroom provided by Legacy Software. Stay tuned for details! If you are an AOL member, you can join our online discussions about the Awards by typing in the keyword "Netguide."
