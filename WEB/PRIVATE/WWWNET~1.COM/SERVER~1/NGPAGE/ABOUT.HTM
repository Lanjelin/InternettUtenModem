 		Why we're here

CMP Media Inc.'s NetGuide Live aims to be the most useful site on the World Wide Web by giving readers a comprehensive daily guide to what's live, what's new, and what's changing online. To fulfill that mission, NetGuide Live features powerful search tools, including the ability to explore the tens of millions of pages of information on the World Wide Web. NetGuide Live has put together a "Best of the Web" catalog of Web site profiles, complete with information on issues like parental control, and has created a system of categories of information to allow readers to browse through site profiles arranged by subject. NetGuide Live has created Live Events, a guide to online programming that tells readers about dozens of scheduled events every day while tracking changes on nearly 100 of the most important sites on the Web. NetGuide Live directs users to the information and activities they want and does it quickly, easily and enjoyably.
