How can I get sports updates?
You're not interested in knowing every detail of your favorite team -- like which jerseys they're going to wear for their away game. But if Michael Jordan was suddenly traded to the Denver Nuggets for LaPhonso Ellis, you'd want to be the first to know.

A good way to get updated sports information is to sign up with a mailing list. The information you want comes directly to you via e-mail. You just find one that suits your sport, say golf, enter in your name and e-mail address, and you're all set.

You may want to think twice about the frequency of your e-mail update. Learning about the players' standings in the big tournament is fine once a week or so, but it's not something you'd like to find in your inbox every morning -- unless you're a true sports nut.
