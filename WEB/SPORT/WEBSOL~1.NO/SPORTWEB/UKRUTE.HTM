How to get there!

CAR OR COACH:

From Oslo the traveltime is approx. 3.5 hrs. If you start in Trondheim the traveltime will be approx. 5hrs. The E6 is of high standard and there are a number of good servicefacilities along the way, such as gas-stations and inns.

You exit the E6 when you reach a sign that says:

Harpefoss in SørFron municipal distr.

For those of you who travel in groups we can offer conference equipped coaches.

 TRAIN:

Arrival by train at Vinstra is an efficient and pleasant arrival. The traveltime from Oslo or Trondheim is 3 hrs. For trainschedules call +47 22 17 14 00 (NSB - Norwegian Rail)

The expresstrains are supplied with kiosk and restaurant in pleasant 1 or 2 class coaches.

If you order your traintickets at Gålå Høgfiellshotell og Hytter, phone. 61 29 81 09, NSB will offer a 50% discount!

The car-trip up into the mountains lasts for 5 min. Pls give us a call before your arrival.

 PLANE:

The large international airports ; Oslo Airport Fornebu, Gardermoen Airport near Lillestrøm and Værnes Airport, Trondheim are all close to traintransfer, an other solution is to go by a smaller plane to Frya Airport at Ringebu.

The travelagencies will supply you with the necessary help you should require.
