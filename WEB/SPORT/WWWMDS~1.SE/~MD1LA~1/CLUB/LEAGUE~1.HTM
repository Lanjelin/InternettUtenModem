1896 - 1924 the swedish champions were decided after a cup tournament
            between the best teams in regional leagues.
1925 - 1981 the winner of Allsvenskan became swedish champions.
1982 - 1990 the top 4 (or 8) teams in Allsvenskan went to a play-off and 
            the winners of the play-off became champions.
1991 - 1992 the top 6 teams in Allsvenskan went to a championship league 
            and the team who won that league became champions.
1993 - ???? the winner of Allsvenskan becomes swedish champions.
