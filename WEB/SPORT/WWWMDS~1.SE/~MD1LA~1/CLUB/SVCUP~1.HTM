IFK season-by-season in the swedish cup



NOTE! The Swedish cup has been badly treaten during its lifetime.
It was first (1941-1953) played as a pre season tournament (when
Sweden played spring->autumn) and was canceled between 1954 and
1966 due to poor interrest from the clubs(!) In January 1967 SFF
decided that Sweden needed a cup to have a team in EC2 in August
that same year. This was the start of what must be the most
uninterresting cup in the whole world. Neither the fans nor the 
clubs have the stamina to follow all changes made every season
to make the cup more attractive. From 1968->1990 the cup started
in the summer that year and was finished in the spring the 
following year. In 1991 there were 2 cupfinals (*) (the 1991 and the
1991/92 cup) due to another change of the tournament, wich is a much
to long story to tell here...
Since nobody in Sweden actually cares about this tournament I
give you as complete statistics of IFK's preformance as possible,
just because it should be here...



OBS! Svenska cupen måste vara den mest ointressanta i hela världen.
Mellan 1941-1953 spelades den som en försäsongsturnering, och ställdes
in mellan 1954-1966 pga. ointresse från elitklubbarna(!) I januari 1967
insåg SFF att Sverige måste ha en representant i Cupvinnarcupen som
startadde i Augusti samma år. På så sätt inleddes den moderna delen av
världens mest ointressanta cup. Varken klubbarna eller fansen orkar bry
sig om alla ändringar som görs varje år för att få folk intresserade av
den. Mellan 1968-1990 inleddes cupen på sommaren med final året efter på
våren. 1991 spelades 2(!) cupfinaler(*) pga en ny omläggning som
naturligtvis också blev ett fiasko!
Eftersom ingen i Sverige egentligen bryr sig om cupen har IFK helt
enkelt avstått från att medverka under flera år på 1970-talet. Här
följer så komplett statistik över IFKs härjningar som jag kunnat hitta.
