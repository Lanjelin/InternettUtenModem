MT[I] will use the e-mail list to distribute information and news to taurine aficionados who use the internet. The list will not be maintained online, so it will not be available to hackers. Each piece of mail will be sent to only one recipient (i.e., no cc: mailings), so if a piece of mail is accidentally misdirected, the list will still remain private.

The list will, of course, be loaned/sold to no one.

If you would like to distribute taurine information using MT[I]'s list, e-mail us for instructions. Nothing will be mailed to list members without being read by MT[I] first (to ensure that, for example, list members are not barraged with animal-rights tracts).
