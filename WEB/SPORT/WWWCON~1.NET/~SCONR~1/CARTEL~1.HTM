Taurine Carteles

Carteles are the posters used to advertise corridas. They exist in many forms, with national and regional variations. If you'd like to know more about this graphic arts genre, you might want to take a look at EL CARTEL TAURINO (by Rafael Zaldívar, published by Espasa Calpe, Spain -- #26 in their "colección La Tauromaquia") which, in addition to hundreds of beautiful cartel reproductions, contains a lengthy exposition and bibliography.

Feria de Nîmes----Home page for this annual provençal festival contains, among other things, .gifs of the feria carteles from the past 10 years' celebrations.

Praça de Toiros Ericira----a cartel (from a small coastal town, near Lisbon) that shows a rejóneador, a matador, and (If we can make it out clearly) a group of forcados
