La décennie Ojeda, Lucien Clergue, Jean-Marie Magnan (Note: anytime you see Lucien Clergue's name affixed to a publication you can expect it to be beautifully illustrated. Clergue is (in the Webmeister's opinion, anyway) the best French taurine photographer, a man of great artistry (witness his beautiful, abstract, color photos) and afición (he's a one-time torero).
Une faena de Curro Romero, Lucien Clergue , Jean-Marie Magnan
De Federico García Lorca à Maurice Ohana--LLanto por Ignacio Sánchez Mejías, texts in French and Spanish by José Bergamín, François Zumbiehl, Frédéric Deval, Christine Prost, Francis Marmande, Nicolas Zourabichivili, Emilio Casares Rodicio. Actes Sud, 1992, 139 pages, 21 x 11 cm. (Maurice Ohana is a French musician who wrote a music for the LLanto) The texts are very good. Bergamin gives a very precise description of Mejías death.)

Miroir de la tauromachie, Michel Leiris with illustrations by André Masson. Fata Morgana 1989- 74 pages 22 x 14 cm. (Michel Leiris, a french poet, wrote a lot of texts about great painters.)

Nimeño II, torero de France, Lucien Clergue, Alain Montcouquiol, Jean Cau

Le syndrome du Toro, textes de N. Lutchmaya, dessins du Matador de Toros André Viard. Editions de Camargue.30 x 21 cm.180 pages. (Plenty of humor and very interesting texts. For "aficionados experimentados."
