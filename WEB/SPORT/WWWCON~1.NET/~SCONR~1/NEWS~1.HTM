Newspapers & Periodicals
w/ taurine coverage

Each of these references is to a newspaper or magazine that has coverage of current taurine events. When you visit one on-line, please take the time to leave them an e-mail message thanking them for providing this service -- and, asking them to keep up the good work (and, if you first learned about them here on Mundo Taurino [Internet], please let them know that as well).

Note that some but not all magazines and newspapers have electronic versions -- when one does, its name will be a link to take you to that site.

Where a magazine or newspaper's description contains evaluative comments, they
may or may not be the opinion(s) of MT[I]. They will often have been
supplied by the person who forwarded the posted information (and this
is not always indicated).

Toro Bravo
A classy looking quarterly magazine founded in 1905 with colour covers and black and white interiors, high quality paper, lots of photographs. Examples of recent articles: ganaderias in France in 1996; celebration of 80 years of the U.C.T.L. (Union de Criadores de Toros de Lidia, the sponsor of the magazine); a piece on bull breeding techniques with suitably gruesome photos. Well- organized with an insert with subscription details in four languages (including English). Published by Ediciones del Toro, S.L. Annual subscriptions are (in Spain) 5,000 ptas by bank draft or 7,000 ptas by other type of payment. Overseas rates are 9,000 ptas and 11,500 ptas respectively. The address is Ediciones del Toro, Revista 'Toro Bravo', c/Eduardo Dato 7, 28010 Madrid. Tel: 91 448 88 64. Fax: 91 594 39 59.
