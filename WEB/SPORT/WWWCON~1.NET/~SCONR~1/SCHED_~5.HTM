Lima

October 27 to November 24, 1996
The Feria del Señor de los Milagros
50th Anniversary of Lima's beautiful Plaza de Toros
Bullfights -- in Plaza de Acho:

  • October 27 (Sunday) -- 1st Corrida de Abono
    Bulls from Javier Garfias
    For Alejandro Silveti, Vicente Barrera, and Rafael Gasteñeta
  • November 1 (Friday) -- International Novillada
    Novillos from Paijan
    For Carlos Pacheco, Curro Díaz, and Cesar de Madrid
  • November 3 (Sunday) -- 2d Corrida de Abono
    Bulls from Real de Saltillo
    José Mari Manzanares, Vicente Barrera, and Freddy Villafuerte
  • November 10 (Sunday) -- 3d Corrida de Abono
    Bulls from Marco Garfias
    For José Mari Manzanares, Miguel Rodríguez, and Pedrito de Portugal
  • November 17 (Sunday) -- 4th Corrida de Abono
    Bulls from Santa Rosa de Lima
    For Miguel Báez 'Litri,' Jesulín de Ubrique, and Rafael Gastañeta
  • November 24 (Sunday) -- 5th Corrida de Abono
    Bulls from Fernando de la Mora
    For Miguel Báez 'Litri,' Jesulín de Ubrique, and Pedrito de Portugal
