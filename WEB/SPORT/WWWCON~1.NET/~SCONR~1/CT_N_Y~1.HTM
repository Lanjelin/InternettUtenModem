MEETINGS:

CTNY meets monthly, on the second thursday of the month. Each meeting consists of videotapes, occasional slide presentations, updates and narration provided by in-person experiences of club members. This is followed by dinner in our own room in a spanish restaurant. A private bar is available.

The meeting temporada begins in October and runs through June. In between CTNY has "tertulias," which are sporadic, and deal with details of bullfighting as explained by professionals (club members), with bullrunning (club members) and with various aspects of spanish culture(for example, dance). The tertulias are held in members' homes.
