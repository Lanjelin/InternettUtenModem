Sources for Corrida
(and other taurine) Videos


Be careful about what you buy.
It is illegal to distribute/sell programs that are
home-recorded from broadcast television.

Remember that there are several, mutually-incompatible video formats (e.g., NTSC, the predominant format in the Americas; SECAM, the predominant format in France; and PAL, the predominant format in Spain). If you purchase a video in the wrong format, it will not play in your VCR, and you may have to pay for its conversion into your country's format before you can view it.
MT[I] cannot vouch for the integrity of anyone on this resource list -- unless we do that explicitly (usually because we have ourselves dealt with the vendor) -- nor for the quality of anything they sell. And MT[I] cannot guarantee your satisfaction with anything you purchase.
