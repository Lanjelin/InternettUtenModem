Taurine Documentaries & Shorts
--USA--

Until taurine film/video files appear directly on the net , these sections will simply be a listing of taurine-themed commercially available documentaries and short-subject films. Those entries with links will take you to other sites that (1) describe and/or (2) offer the item for sale.

Documentaries & Short Subjects

  • AMERICAN BULLFIGHTER -- ?(19??) "... takes the viewers on a wild and wooly ride over the course of three years in the life and times of rodeo's unsung heroes: the bullfighting clowns." Yes, we know that the "bullfighting" rodeo clowns are not part of what a tauromaquian purists would consider "bullfighting." We're 'inclusionists' however -- our view of the "mundo" taurino is quite broad. 60 minutes.
  • ARRUZA -- (1967) Biographical film about the Carlos Arruza (whom the video jacket calls "the greatest matador that ever lived" ... well, take it with a lick of salt), made by Budd Boetticher, Hollywood's #1 taurine-feature director. 91 minutes. Available from EMBASSY HOME ENTERTAINMENT
  • BABBIT INSTRUCTIONAL RESOURCES-- Videos designed for language-instruction classrooms, focusing on the culture of the target country. Many videos have bullfight sequences and relate bullfight history. Ordering information is included at the bottom of the Babbit page. EL ARTE DE LA TAUROMAQUIA - English Title - THE ART OF TAUROMACHY (The History of Bullfighting from Remote Times to the Present). (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 30 min., Advanced Level, 1996). Including " the history of bullfighting from early man to the present. We see bulls on the cave walls of Altamira and Laxcaux. The symbolism of the bullfight is stressed (light vs. dark) as well as the importance of the bull in the representation of power and invincibility. The video traces the progression of the bullfight from medieval times to the modern forms of today. Scenes are shown of the famous "Fiesta of San Fermín" in Pamplona. The video ends as we see the sequence of a modern bullfight and the significance of each act or "tercio." A must for the "aficionado" of the bullfight." PAMPLONA / VIVA SAN FERMIN - English Title - FIESTA OF SAN FERMIN (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 50 min., Intermediate/Advanced Level, 1995). This visual covers one of Spain's most famous festivals - San Fermín - which takes place in July. We begin with the "chupinazo" and follow the many parades and floats. We can't miss the "gigantes y cabezudos," the gracefully dancing figures that everyone loves. We witness the exciting "encierros" and follow up with the many activities held during the week - the "corrida vasca," the "fiesta campera," the "cómico taurino," and the "aizkolaris" or "woodchoppers." The finale, of course, in the bullfight every afternoon. There are two or three interludes which give a brief background in history and we see some of the picturesque towns of Navarra. SEMANA SANTA EN SEVILLA - English Title - HOLY WEEK IN SEVILLE (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 16 min., Intermediate Level, 1995). Including " a short bullfight sequence in the Plaza de la Maestranza." SEVILLA EN FERIA - English Title - APRIL FAIR IN SEVILLE (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 16 min., Intermediate Level, 1995). Including " a bullfight at "La Maestranza." LOS MAYAS DE YUCATAN - English Title - THE MAYAS OF YUCATAN (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 16 min., Intermediate Level, 1989). Including " a country bullfight not too unlike one described by John Stevens in 1840." MEXICO / CIUDAD FASCINADORA - English Title - FASCINATING MEXICO CITY (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 16 min., Intermediate Level, 1995). Including " a bullfight at the "Plaza México," the world's largest bullring." SALTILLO DE FIESTA - English Title - FIESTA IN SALTILLO (Video Instructional Unit in Spanish and/or English, 12 min., Elementary Level, 1995). Including " one of the best bullfights of the famous matador, Alfredo Leal."
  • FERDINANDO EL TORO (1966) 8 min.,16mm short subject, educational for spanish-language classes. "A way of life, and part of a given culture is portrayed in this version of Munro Leaf's kindly satire on bullfighting. The Spanish version is a smooth and easily paced narration, designed for the most effective listening comprehension, enjoyment, and interest on the part of the foreign language student." ("kindly" satire???)
  • MY KINGDOM FOR ...-- "Luisitano is the main star of this documdrama about the exquisite Portuguese horse. The display of haute ecole and rejoneo horsemanship is balanced within a framework of a story revolving around the producer of the video, film director Budd Boetticher and his wife Mary, and their love for this fiery Iberian horse. 80 min."
