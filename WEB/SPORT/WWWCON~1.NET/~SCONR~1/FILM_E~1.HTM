Taurine Films
--España--

Until taurine film/video files appear directly on the net , these sections will simply be a listing of taurine-themed commercially available videos. Those entries with links will take you to other sites that (1) describe and/or (2) offer the item for sale.

Feature Films

  • BELMONTE, BULLFIGHTER-- (1995)
    "Juan Belmonte, the most famous Spanish bullfighter, is a hero. The film describes his legendary story from a boy craving for the bullring to a well-know bullfighter. However, Belmonte lives in the complication of excitement and puzzle. Joselito's death makes him realize the fate of a bullfighter. at last he commits suicide in peace." Starring Achero Amnas, Monica Molina, Lautaro Murua. Directed by Juan S. Bollain. (105 min. color)
  • JAMON, JAMON-- (1992)
    An award-winning (1993 Venice Film Festival) comedy set in a provincial Spanish town -- with one of the male leads and his friends going bullfighting in the nude at midnight. According to Roger Ebert, "It takes a certain kind of moviegoer, I suppose, to enjoy a film like this; of course it's in bad taste, of course it's vulgar, of course it flies in the face of all that is seemly, and, of course, that is the idea."
  • MATADOR-- (1986)
    If you don't know Pedro Almodovar's films, you're really missing something. Here's Antonio Banderas before he became an American film phenomenon, in a taurine murder mystery so bizarre we won't even take a 'stab' at outlining the plot. This one is not to be missed. 107 minutes. Color. Available with English subtitles from CINEVISTA.
  • SANGRE Y ARENA-- (1989)
    How many times are they going to make cinematic versions of this novel? Here's the most recent Spanish version, starring Christopher Rydell as the matador, Juan, and Sharon Stone (!) as Dona Sol who steals him away from the only good woman in his life.
