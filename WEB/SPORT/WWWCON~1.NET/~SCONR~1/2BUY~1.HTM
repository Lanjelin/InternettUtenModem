Taurine Items Wanted

This page is here for the convenience of any Mundo Taurino [Internet] user who is looking to buy any kind of taurinalia -- books, carteles, trajes, surplus corrida tickets, anything at all.

To use the page, send e-mail to MT[I] -- you can do this from the bottom of any MT[I] page -- including (1) your e-mail address, and (2) a description of the item(s) you are looking for. It is not necessary for you to include your name.

This is NOT a commercial service, and is being provided only as a convenience to those who frequent MT[I]. Please remember that MT[I] cannot vouch for the integrity of anyone who responds to your listing, nor for the quality of any item(s) they may offer you, nor can MT[I] guarantee your satisfaction with anything you might purchase. Be careful
