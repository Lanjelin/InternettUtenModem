MEETINGS:

  • CTCV meets on the second Thursday of each month.
  • Snail-mail address: Club Taurino de Chula Vista, P.O. Box 1062, Chula Vista, CA 92011

PUBLICATION:

  • CTCV publishes a monthly newsletter, "Toriles."
  • Gerald Campos, the "Toriles" editor, may be contacted via e-mail should you have a newsletter inquiry, or an article for submission/consideration.
  • "Toriles" is also available on the net (downloadable, in .pdf format) -- from Gerald Campos' taurine web site, Bullfighting at its Finest (or from Lo mejor de los Toros, the Spanish-language version).

MEMBERSHIP:

  • CTCV membership is open to all, resident and non-resident alike.
  • Membership fees are $25US per year (individual membership), $40 per year (household membership). Both include a one-year subscription to "Toriles."
  • Non-members may subscribe to "Toriles" for $15US per year.
