Other Web-"galleries"

Vidal Jimenez -- neo-surrealist taurine paintings
Several of this Spanish artist's oil-on-canvas paintings illustrated in a well-designed gallery (includes a short summary of the areas of his work and exhibitions over the last 26 years).

Nancy Mouat -- bronze sculptures
Three of this US (Missouri) artist's taurine bronzes are displayed: Pase Clasico ($2400US); Con Bravura ($1275US); and Que Torero ($925US). (Although Que Torero is not displayed directly, it is the same as Pase Clasico without the bull.) Pieces may be ordered (and information requested) from Nancy Mouat via e-mai

Ramón Reveles -- Works of this Mexican artist (on permanent display at Plaza México) are reproduced in a web gallery maintained by the ¡Toros en México! web site. From the first page of the site's "frames" version, use the "arte" link to enter the gallery.

la asociación EL TORO de Madrid -- several "galleries"
This lovely taurine web site (just follow the "arte taurino" link from the menu on any of their pages) includes a section dedicated to all varieties of taurine art. Well worth more than one visit.

TORO Y TIERRA -- A "virtual exhibition" of paintings
by the Dutch artist (among other things, she's a sculptor, painter, art teacher, writer, and active in Dutch taurine organizations), Monica Rotgans, who specializes in cattle, bulls and landscapes. In addition to the paintings displayed here, more of Ms. Rotgans' work can be seen in back issues of the all-too-short-lived Spanish art/culture magazine, Taurología.

Bruno Simon Valdez
An web site dedicated to this US, southwestern artist's taurine works.
