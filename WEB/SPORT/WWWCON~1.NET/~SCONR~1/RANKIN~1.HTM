Taurine Rankings

México (Tijuana) -- local 'escalafon' for toreros appearing in Tijuana's plazas -- with photos of the ranked matadors, provided by Bullfighting at its Finest.. Additional information may be available on Torero, Torero!!, in annotations to the "Carteles" calendar.

Resumen de las Novilladas -- (en español) -- results and commentary on the novilladas of the current (50th Anniversary) temporada in Plaza México, the largest bullring in the world. Commentary provided by Don Alfredo Flórez.

Spanish temporada -- provided by the magazine, 6 Toros 6. Go to the current issue, then down the page to the "Escalafon," where you'll find links to separate rankings for matadors, novilleros, and rejoneadores.
