Until taurine film/video files appear on the net , these sections will simply be a listing of taurine-themed commercially available videos. Any entry with a link will take you to a site where the film is described and/or where it is offered for sale.

We receive MANY requests for information about sources for videotapes of recent-temporada corridas. Please remember that it is illegal to distribute/sell programs that are home-recorded from broadcast television. Although there IS a market for such tapes, we can neither help you sell them, nor help you find someone who does. Doing that could jeopardize the existence of MT[I], and might subject you to some legal sanction.
