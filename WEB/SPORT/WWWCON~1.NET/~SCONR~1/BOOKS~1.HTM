Taurine Books

This page and its links is focused on taurine books. By far the most information along these lines can be obtained from the various organizations of "taurine bibliophiles." Information here can't do more than (ojalá) whet your apetitite in the most superficial way.

  • Bibliography
    Seventeen fiction and non-fiction classics.
  • Books in Print
    Focus on books currently available from publishers worldwide
  • DeWeese-Parkinson, Cathy & Lynn
    A used-book search/sales operation that includes a specialized catalog of taurine books.
  • Libreria Estanislao Rodriguez
    San Bernardo, 27 - 28015 Madrid - Spain
    Write and ask for their current catalog, "Catalogo de Libros, Folletos, Revistas y Carteles de asunto taurino" -- a LARGE listing of used books, old bullfight magazines (most of both in Spanish), and carteles for sale at their bookstore.
  • MT[I] Library
    Books and Periodicals at MT[I]-HQ
