Books-in-Print
(Paises Hispanohablantes)

The Spanish Ministry of Education and Culture has made it possible to search an enormous database of books published in Spain since 1966. Listed books may, or may not be currently in print. You can search the database from this MT[I] page, or directly from the Ministry's website. We will make an effort to supplement the searchable database with a list of Books in Print that, for one reason or another (for example, those published in other countries), do not appear in the Ministry's database.

Search Criteria:

  • Capitalization does not matter.
  • Multiple search terms may be entered.
  • This is a Spanish-language database -- don't use English words in the "subject" field.
  • To come back after your search, use your browser's "back" button.
