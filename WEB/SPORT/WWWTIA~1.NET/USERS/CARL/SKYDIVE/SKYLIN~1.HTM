Here are some great skydiving sites.


Skydiving by Susan L.
Wightman -- is a cool site with professional photos and graphics.  If you've
got a quick connection, or time to wait for the downloads, this site is a
"must visit"!


Sky God Wannabe -- is a cool
site by John McKee.  He's got lots of photos and videos, even of his 71 year
old mother
doing her FIRST tandem dive!


Rob's Home Page
Skydive -- has some good skydiving information.


Joe's Fun Page -- A few
great pics, some low res movies.  He includes a photo and movie of the
largest formation dive ever performed in New England (54 people)!  This
page also has SCUBA, rock climbing, white water rafting, hiking, skiing,
inline skating, hacky sack, etc.  Don't expect huge sections for each topic,
but you can expect quality.


Skydive! Archive -- Skydive!
Archive contains lots and lots of info and links.  It's really geared
toward the experienced diver, but would be interesting to visit.  If
there's any info you need, here's a good place to look.


Skydive WWW -- Great
site for experieced divers.  It's got all sorts of info about what's going
on and what's coming up.  They also have links to other skydiving sites.
