Night Jump

by

Paul Gurteen
i did my first night jump last weekend. it was superb. in the UK, for your first nj you can only do a solo exit and 10 second delay but that was enough to bring back that feeling you get when you do your first ever jump. jumping out into absolute darkness with only a set of car headlights to tell you where the dz is. we pointed the car into wind to let people know which direction to land although i lost my bearings at one point and landed down wind. luckily i damaged nothing but a bit of pride. i strongly recommend doing a night jump if you get the chance and haven't already!
