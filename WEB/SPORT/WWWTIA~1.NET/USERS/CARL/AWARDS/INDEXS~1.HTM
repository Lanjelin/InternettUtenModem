Here's what they had to say:

What is an adventure? Stop by Adventure Living to find out! The author
created it so people from all over the world can share their adventures via
stories, photos, information, event listings and links. While some of the
stories are from the U.S., others come from visitors around the world!

In some ways, life around here needs a little more adventure, and in others,
much, much less. This was a nice little getaway this weekend. We hope you
agree.
