Competition with many exciting prizes!
With the help of the pictures and a few clues you can get to Pippi,s Villekulla Cottage. Click on the correct picture and you will reach to a new level. You,ll have to find your way through three different levels of pictures in order to be able to send us a e-mail. Each month we will draw a new winner who will be awarded exciting prizes from Astrid Lindgrens World. (NB. We´ll accept only one entry per person per month!)

Grand final in May!
You can take part in this competition every month up to April 1997. The final takes place in May and you can take part only if you have participated in this competition. We,ll send a special competition form via e-mail to all who register on the response form which you,ll find shortly. The final will take the form of a number of questions about Astrid Lindgren and her books and films. There will be some extra-special prizes for the winners including an overnight stay in our new chalet village and admission tickets for the whole family.
