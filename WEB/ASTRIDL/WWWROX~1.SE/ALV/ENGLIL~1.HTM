Little Troublemakerstreet
Aunty Berg's red house with white lace curtains and the yellow house of the Nymans with the flag pole in front are both to be found in Troublemakerstreet. Lotta, the Nymans youngest daughter, a headstrong but sweet brat and a menace to her brother and sister, kept stating that she was not too little and she could definitely manage everything as well as they could.
