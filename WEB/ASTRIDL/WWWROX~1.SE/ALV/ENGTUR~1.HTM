Astrid Lindgrens World,
 at a comfortable distans from Stockholm, Göteborg and Malmö.
ALW is situated in Vimmerby, the birthplace of Astrid Lindgrens, which can be reached by car in about 3,5 hours from Stockholm, Göteborg or Malmö. There are also weekend busses from these towns. If you choose to go by train you transfer in Linköping or Nässjö/Hultsfred. Or you can fly domestic to Hultsfred/Vimmerby airport.

Astrid Lindgrens Småland ...
 ... is a name for the cooperating tourist information bureaus of the municipalities surrounding Vimmerby. They will give you suggestions for various alternative accomodations such as hotels, cabins, private rooms and, of course, the varius camping sites. You will also get information about other attractions in the surroundings of and in Vimmerby.
