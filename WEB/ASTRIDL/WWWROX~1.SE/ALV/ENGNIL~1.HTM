Simon Small
This is the place where the grown-ups can feel like children again, and the children can imagine themselves to be pixies - all in giant-size format. The important thing is to do what Simon Small did - touch the nail on the door and say the password "killevippen".
Here is a tip, take a photo of Mum and Dad when they try to climb on to a chair or into bed - then they´ll se how easy it is to be small!
