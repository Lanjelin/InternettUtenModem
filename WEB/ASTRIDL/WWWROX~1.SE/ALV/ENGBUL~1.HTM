Noisy Village
In Noisy Village it,s always time for fun. Lasse and Bosse, Lisa and Olle, Brita and Anna are always playing and having fun, so it,s amazing that they don,t die from laughing! This summer we open and new pond with rafts which all the children can play with. There is also an new stream where we can play with bark boats and have a competition with all our new friends. The external design of Noisy Village was in inspired by Sevedstorp, where the Noisy Village films were shot, but it its her own childhood that Astrid Lindgrens has remembered. You can easily visit the film set in Sevedstorp, about 15 km from Vimmerby.
