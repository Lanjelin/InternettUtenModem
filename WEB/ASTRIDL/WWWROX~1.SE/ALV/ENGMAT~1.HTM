Shopping - food - picnic in Astrid Lindgrens Värld
Within Astrid Lindgrens World there are several restaurants, coffee shops and stores. There is for example the shop where Pippi bought 18 kilos of sweets and her soda pop tree where you too can buy something to drink. For those who bring a picnic basket there are many fine lawns and nice picnic areas and there are also grills for your barbeque. But look out for Karlsson on the roof! He is a greedy rascal who takes a bite when ever he sees something he fancies.
