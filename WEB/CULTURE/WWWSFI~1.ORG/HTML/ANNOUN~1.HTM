 This page will publish abbreviated versions of all STARFLEET Announcements from the (sfi-announce@sfi.org) Announcements List Server and will remain listed on this page for approximately 30 days. They are listed in the order they are received with the most current message on top. If you are interested in a specific a copy of the full text of any announcement below, please check the archives below or send e-mail to Allyson M.W. Dyar (NetServices@sfi.org).

    MARCH 1997

  • 9703.06: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith clearifies the confusion over 'fleet membership prices overseas. Please read the message for details.

  • 9703.06: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces from Operations the those ships that are on standby status.

  • 9703.03: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces that the Communique (CQ) will be in the mails by Friday, the 7th.

  • 9703.02: Announcements Only ListServer sfi-announce@sfi.org is now on-line. To subscribe: send a message to listserver@sfi.org and in the body of the message put "subscribe sfi-announce your name" for example: subscribe sfi-announce Michael Q Fleeter -- please direct any questions to NetServices@sfi.org

  • 9703.01: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces the appointment of Commodore Mike Malotte the new Region 12 RC.

    FEBRUARY 1997

  • 9702.27: Fireside IRC Chat Log from the first Chat on 26 February is now available for download from ftp.sfi.org/FiresideChats/fsc9702.txt or you may request a copy by from NetServices NetServices@sfi.org

  • 9702.26: NetServices now offers an Office of Web Consultation http://www.netset.com/~death/owc/. This department will answer Questions about HTML code and links provided to learn more about HTML on the web. This office is run by Lieutenant Michael Stanley, death@netset.com

  • 9702.26: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith reiterates his administration's policy towards official use of the Internet.

  • 9702.23: Cmdr Carl Lewis, Overseas Coupon Project, East Coast Coordinator announces "The Great Coupon War Of 1997" where Ships on the East Coast are pitted against Ships on the West coast as to who will collect the most coupons. This "challenge" is open to any club or organization in or outside 'fleet. For more information, please contact Cmdr Carl Lewis at OCP-East@sfi.org.

  • 9702.21: reminder--Promotion Request forms are due by the 28th February to Captain Reid Stanley at promotions@sfi.org or snail mail: 4740 Whitwil Blvd, Charleston, SC 29418-5007 (please mark SFI on the corner of the envelope!). E-mail requests will be accepted.

  • 9702.20: update--The first "Fireside IRC Chat" with Fleet Admiral Michael Smith will be held 26 Feb 1997 starting at (9pm eastern time, 6pm pacific time). Subsequent Chats will be held on the last Thursday of the month. For information on how to access IRC, please link to the 'fleet home page www.sfi.org and choose "cyberspace." If you have any questions, please ask the moderator, Commodore Allyson M.W. Dyar at NetServices@sfi.org

  • 9702.19: International Conference (IC) 97 Flyer has been revised. You can check it out on the web at www.webwarren.com/ic97/ and follow the links to download the flyer.

  • 9702.18: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces that the position of Inspector General is now open for applications as the former IG, Jack Hopkins wishes to step down. Those members interested in the position should send their 'fleet & real-life resumes to Commodore Michael Malotte, Director of STARFLEET Personnel, 1717 South 5th Street, Leavenworth, Kansas 66048 or e-mail personnel@sfi.org. It is suggested that you read the entire message for further information on the position.

  • 9702.16: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces the appointment of Captain Kurt Roithinger as the new Regional Coordinator, Region 5; with his appointment as RC, he is now promoted to Fleet Captain.

  • 9702.16: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces promotions.

  • 9702.15: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces that STARFLEET now has an official presence in Region 20. Professor Steve Molyneux stephen.molyneux@cableinet.co.uk of the University of Wolverhampton, in England, is STARFLEET's official point of contact in Region 20.

  • 9702.13: IC '97 Programming Committee would like to know what 'fleet members want to see at this year's International Conference. Send your ideas to the IC '97 Programming Chair Marla Bush at zeonia@ix.netcom.com

  • 9702.12: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces that the Belleville 'fleet has been closed and he has received the account balance.

  • 9702.12: FCapt Mandi Herrmann, Vice Chief, CompOps will be taking any questions concerning Computer Operations whilst Commodore Sal Lizard will be off-line busy with the technical aspects of CompOps. If you have any questions for CompOps, please address them to compops@sfi.org

  • 9702.11: Captain Candie Arnold candie5@juno.com is compiling a roster of Regional and Local "Chocolate Officers." Please read the message for the specific information she is looking for.

  • 9702.09: Commodore Allyson M.W. Dyar NetServices@sfi.org announces the reorganization of the web pages. Halcyon is now being used as a back-up and all the web pages now reside on the sfi.org web server. WebMasters, if you link to any page *but* the top level page www.sfi.org, please check the message in the 01-10 February 1997 Archive or request a copy of the message from her for the new directory structure for the web pages.

  • 9702.06: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces that OCC will be reopened shortly as she's recently been sent a cheque for supplies, etc.

  • 9702.05: update--Fleet Admiral Michael Smith announces monthly "Fireside IRC Chats" with the first will be held 26 Februrary 1997. There is now a poll being taken to decide what day the chats will occur. Please write to Chief of Staff Tony Rowley cs-cos@sfi.org with your preference.

  • 9702.05: Vice Commander, STARFLEET, Admiral Chuck Freas discusses vouchers.

  • 9702.04: Captain Jeffery Higdon higdon@usit.net announces the formation of the The MidSouth Squadron that consist of the following Starships of the Region 1, Sector 1, Are located in the geographic areas of West Tennessee and Western Kentucky.

  • 9702.04: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith discusses the Command Status Report (CSR) and the annual Chapter Charter Fees (CCF).

  • 9702.01: Captain Les Rickard updates Operations -- if you can't use the on-line MSR, you can use a text copy which must be e-mailed in and not sent as an attachment. This document is available off of the dedicated Operations Page or the Documents Page or from the archive "01-03 February 1997" above.

  • 9702.01: Commodore Carolyn Donner announces that OCC via mail is still on hold, however, OCC ON-Line is still operational. (See 9702.06 for an update!)

  • 9702.01: Fleet Admiral Michael Smith has another update on the state of the 'fleet including progress on obtaining all materials from the former CS, Dan McGinnis as well as other fleet related projects.
