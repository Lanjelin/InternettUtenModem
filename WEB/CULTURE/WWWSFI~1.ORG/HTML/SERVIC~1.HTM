 ARMED SERVICES PROGRAM -- To all "Real Life" Active Duty Military members of Starfleet, please contact this office for more information about this Program and how it can serve you and your special needs. We have a few special services to offer you at this time which we hope to add too as we grow in numbers. Among these services are a Quartermasters office for program specific items, and help with setting up a chapter of your own on your Base. For additional information contact us at ASPHQ@aol.com (SFASP@aol.com). Our Hailing Frequencies will remain open.

THE OVERSEAS COUPON PROGRAM (OCP) is a service provided by members of various Star Trek fan organizations (STARFLEET and others) to military families stationed overseas. It is designed to ease the burden of buying food and baby supplies in countries with high inflation rates. This is a desparately needed program. Your help is needed. For more information, link to the OCP Home Page or e-mail Carl Lewis (lewisc@awod.com).

STARFLEET INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE COMMITTEE (SICC). Every year STARFLEET has an International Conference (IC). It is hosted by different ships and or Region(s) around Starfleet. It is the duty of SICC to promote,and solict Bids, then award the the next IC from the Starfleet Chapters that submitted. The Committee consists of 6-8 members from regions around the Fleet. Contact Michael Henigan (sfichq@macconnect.com) if you want more information the conferences or bidding procedures.

The 1997 International Conference will take place Aug 22-24 1997, Cherry Hill, NJ. For more info, contact Tony Rowley (ridire@netaxs.com).
