Experience Yoga Nidra

- a cassette tape you can use in your daily life

All you need is to lie down and follow the instructions on the tape
- completely still and with closed eyes!

Two deep relaxations, based on Swami Satyananda's classic Yoga Nidra, created and guided by Swami Janakananda, who has also composed the background of nature sounds. Music by Roop Verma. As a background and introduction there is a 18 page booklet inserted in the tape box.
