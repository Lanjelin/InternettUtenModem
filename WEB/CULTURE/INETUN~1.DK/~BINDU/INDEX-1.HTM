Welcome to

Scandinavian Yoga and Meditation School is a group of yoga and meditation teachers, who teach yoga and meditation from the tantric tradition.

We also publish Bindu, a periodical on Yoga, Tantra and Meditation. You may read some of the issues here, or order a free copy of Bindu.

In 1970 the first school started in Copenhagen. Today we also have schools in Stockholm, Århus, Elsinore, Oslo, Bergen, Hannover and Helsinki, with courses running once a week to more intensive week- and weekend courses.

At Håå international Course Center in Southern Sweden we hold our most intensive and deep going courses, ranging from 10- to 14-day courses to the yearly 3-month Course with Swami Janakananda, where the courses height is a period of 33 days silence while learning the tantric Kriya Yoga with more then 20 kriyas. The teaching is mainly in English, but also in German and the Scandinavian languages.
