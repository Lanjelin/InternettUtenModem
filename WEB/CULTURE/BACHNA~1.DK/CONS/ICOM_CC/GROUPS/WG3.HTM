WORKING GROUP NR.3
"ETHNOGRAPHIC MATERIALS"

The Working Group"ETHNOGRAPHIC MATERIALS" addresses the problems connected with the conservation and restoration of a vast and miscellaneous number of objects, made from a huge variety of either organic or inorganic materials, which very often are found exclusively in their place of origin.
