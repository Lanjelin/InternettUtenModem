ICOM-CC

WORKING GROUPS AND CO-ORDINATORS

The Working Groups of the ICOM Committee for Conservation are:

  • Nr. 1 "PAINTING I" - Conservation and restoration of Paintings
  • Nr. 2 "PAINTING II" - Scientific study of Paintings (Methods and Techniques)
      Research areas under "Painting I" and "Painting II":
    • Flexible Supports
    • Rigid Supports
    • Modern and Contemporary Art
    • Icons
    • Far Eastern Paintings
  • Nr. 3 "ETHNOGRAPHIC MATERIALS"
  • Nr. 4 "DOCUMENTATION"
  • Nr. 5 "POLYCHROMED SCULPTURE"
  • Nr. 6 "SCIENTIFIC EXAMINATION OF WORKS OF ART"
  • Nr. 7 "WET ORGANIC ARCHAEOLOGICAL MATERIALS"
  • Nr. 8 "PHOTOGRAPHIC RECORDS"
  • Nr. 9 "TEXTILES"
  • Nr. 10 "STONE"
  • Nr. 11 "THEORY AND HISTORY OF RESTORATION"
  • Nr. 12 "PREVENTIVE CONSERVATION"
      Research Areas under "Preventive Conservation"
    • Lighting and Climate Control
    • Control of Biodeterioration
    • Care of Works of Art in Transit
  • Nr. 13 "NATURAL HISTORY COLLECTIONS"
  • Nr. 14 "GRAPHIC DOCUMENTS"
  • Nr. 15 "MURAL PAINTINGS AND MOSAICS" and "ROCK ART"
    • "Mural Paintings and Mosaics"
    • "Rock Art"
  • Nr. 16 "RESINS: CHARACTERIZATION AND EVALUATION"
  • Nr. 17 "CONSERVATION OF LEATHERCRAFT AND RELATED OBJECTS"
  • Nr. 18 "GLASS, CERAMICS AND RELATED MATERIALS"
  • Nr. 19 "TRAINING IN CONSERVATION AND RESTORATION"
  • Nr. 20 "METALS"
  • Nr. 21 "FORNITURE" and "LACQUERS"
    • "Forniture"
    • "Lacquers"
  • Interim Working Group "MODERN MATERIALS"
