WORKING GROUP NR. 7
"WET ORGANIC ARCHEOLOGICAL MATERIALS"

The Workin Group "WET ORGANIC ARCHEOLOGICAL MATERIALS" is concerned with the conservation and restoration of organic archeological objects found in fresh or salt water, or during excavation in very damp sites.
