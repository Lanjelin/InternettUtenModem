WORKING GROUPS Nr. 1 "PAINTING I" and Nr. 2 "PAINTING II"
Research Area
"FLEXIBLE SUPPORTS"

"FLEXIBLE SUPPORTS"covers paintings carried out on supports of natural organic textile fabrics such as hemp, cotton, flax, silk, etc, or on support of synthetic or inorganic woven fibres, such as nylon, polyester, glass etc. In addition to these more common ones, paintings on other kinds of supports such as leather and hide are also included.

Mikkel Scharff, Co-ordinator of the Working Group "PAINTING I" is also the Assistant Co-ordinator of the Research Area "FLEXIBLE SUPPORTS"
