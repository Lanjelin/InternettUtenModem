WORKING GROUP NR. 2
"PAINTING II"
(SCIENTIFIC STUDY OF PAINTINGS - METHODS AND TECHNIQUES)

The Working Group "PAINTING II" is concerned with the study and research of techniques used in painting and of the methods employed by artists
The Working Group "PAINTING II" covers the same Research Areas as "PAINTING I", i. e.

  • Flexible Supports
  • Rigid Supports
  • Modern and Contemporary Art
  • Icons
  • Far Eastern Paintings
