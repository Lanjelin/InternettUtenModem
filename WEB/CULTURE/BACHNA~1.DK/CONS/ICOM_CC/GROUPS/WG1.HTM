WORKING GROUP NR. 1
"PAINTING I"
(CONSERVATION AND RESTORATION OF PAINTINGS)

"PAINTING I" groups together a number of different areas of research which share a common denominator: the conservation and restoration of movable, two dimentional painted surfaces.
The research areas are:

  • Flexible Supports
  • Rigid Supports
  • Modern and Contemporary Art
  • Icons
  • Far-Eastern Paintings
