METALS SECTION
Conservation of metal objects including archaeological finds, outdoor statues and architectural ironmongery. Conservation of ceramics and glass, including architectural ceramics and mosaic. Field conservation during excavations. Copying of objects. Mounting objects for exhibition. Research project: The evaluation of the corrosive properties of the underground environment.
