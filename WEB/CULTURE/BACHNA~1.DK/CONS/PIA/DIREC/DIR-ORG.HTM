ORGANIC MATERIALS SECTION:
Conservation of waterlogged wood by use of polyethylene glycol and sugar impregnation, freeze drying and drying from organic solvents. Field conservation, especially in waterlogged ground. Conservation of wet leather and archaeological textiles. Research: diffusion of substances through wood. The skin and leather group work mainly on ethnographic objects.
