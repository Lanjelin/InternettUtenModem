The two mythological ravens, Hugin & Munin (Thaught & Memory), helpers of the God Odin. It is said Odin sends them off every daybreak to check the state of the world, yet they manage to return before breakfast. Settling on his shoulders, the ravens whisper the news into the one eyed God's ears.
Animation based on existing raven-design at the National Museum's department for children. Created by Gabor Matolay.
