The Future & Our History
As the originator of the automobile, Mercedes-Benz knows the meaning of history. Since Karl Benz's "motorised carriage" made its maiden voyage in Mannheim in 1886, Mercedes-Benz has continued to play a leading role in world automobile developments.
 Here you can trace the development of Mercedes-Benz from its earliest days in the last century up to its current range of models. Then take a look into the future to see what will be appearing in tomorrow's showrooms, with research projects from the Mercedes think-tanks.
