User Guidance and Navigation
in our wide-based service is as easy as you can imagine. After choosing a language on the welcome page you come onto the homepage itself that provides direct links to the program's individual features and sections.
The small icons at the head and corresponding text links at the foot of any page lead to the most important basic functions.
Besides providing easy navigation these icons also serve as a useful orientation aid.


The Icons and what they mean

The Mercedes-Benz Icon brings you straight back to the homepage.

The chapter-specific icons not only aid orientation, but also lead directly back to the current submenu.

You can flick through all the pages in a section using the 'forward' arrow.

The upward arrow is useful when using links within a program section - it links back up to the next menu above.

The magnifying glass represents our search-function. It allows you to browse easily through the complete content of our server.

The envelope symbol connects you to our dialogue features (email, ordering).

That's how easy it is to get around our program - you've found our help function already!
