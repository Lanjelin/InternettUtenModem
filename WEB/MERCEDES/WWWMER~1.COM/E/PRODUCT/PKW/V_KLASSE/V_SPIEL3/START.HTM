Winter special
The V-class city-tour

It's wintertime. What you have to do is make your way around the streets of a small town picking up some useful objects for Christmas. The challenge is to collect the items in the right order while covering the shortest distance possible.

Check the full rules for the details, than click on the GO-button to start.

 Once you have started, you can't stop the V-class moving until you have picked everything up and left the town by the correct exit. By using the steering wheel or the 'arrow' cursor keys on your keyboard you can steer left and right at intersections. That's all you have to do, so now fasten yourself in, power her up and good luck.
