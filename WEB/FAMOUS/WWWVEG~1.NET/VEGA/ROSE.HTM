This is an edited version of the travel journal Brian kept in the fall of 1990. It provides a fascinating glimpse of a moment in time when Berlin and Prague were beginning to come out of the shadow of recent history, and a moment when Brian's photo work in Berlin coincided with Suzanne's tour of eastern Europe. It's full of all kinds of anecdotes, curious coincidences, and bits of this and that.
