Welcome to Suzanne Vega's first ever online event!

Live Chat!

Suzanne will be doing her first ever LIVE online chat! It's scheduled for September 9, 1996 at 7pm EST, and will be hosted by Interport. For complete details on participating in this event, follow the link to Interport's website at http://www.interport.net.

Q/A Session

In addition to the live chat, on September 9th, Suzanne will be stopping by the website to reply to your questions and comments. Just drop a note to: VegaNet@aol.com. We'll list the questions as they're received, and on the 10th of September, we'll post Suzanne's replies - in both text and audio!

If Suzanne records a reply to your question, you'll win an autographed copy of her new album, Nine Objects Of Desire!
