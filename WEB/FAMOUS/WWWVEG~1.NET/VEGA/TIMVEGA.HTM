About Tim Vega

Tim Vega has been one of the principal artists working around the New York club scene for six or seven years now. His ubiquitous work for Blues Traveler, the Spin Doctors, Xanax 25, Wetlands Preserve, and many other bands and clubs is as easily identified with the local scene as John Popper's harp virtuosity or Chris Barron's scatted neo-classical lyrics.

More than that, Tim's mere presence is everywhere to be found. A large man, he works nowadays as Wetlands' doorman, and, back in the day, he was a Wetlands/Nightingale Bar regular, just the same as dozens of others lucky enough to have seen the scene grow. He designed the Blues Traveler logo. He has done dozens of t-shirt designs and posters for local bands and clubs. He even painted the bathrooms at Wetlands! - Dan Levy
