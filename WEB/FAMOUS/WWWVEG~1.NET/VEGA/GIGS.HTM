Past Performances

Suzanne at Starlight Theater in Los Angeles, August 2, 1996

Suzanne played a live show on August 2nd, 1996 at the Starlight Theater in Los Angeles, California. The show featured Sarah Mc Lachlan, Paula Cole and Suzanne Vega in a pared-down, acoustic setting.

Suzanne At the Berkeley Greek Theater in Berkeley, CA, August 3, 1996

Suzanne joined Sarah McLachlan and Paula Cole for an evening of acoustic music.

Ben & Jerry's Newport Folk Festival, August 10 - 11, 1996

Suzanne appeared at Ben & Jerry's Newport Folk Festival, August 10th at Ft. Adams State Park along with Joan Armatrading, John Hiatt, Ani DiFranco, Nashville Bluegrass Band, Greg Brown, and Peter Rowan And Crucial Country with Jerry Douglas and Tony Rice.

Suzanne at Chastain Park Amphitheatre in Atlanta, July 15,1996

Suzanne performed at Chastain Park Amphitheatre in Atlanta, Georgia. The show which is being presented by 99X featured the Chieftains and Suzanne.

Suzanne On The Tonight Show!

Suzanne appeared on NBC's The Tonight Show with Jay Leno on Wednesday, May 22, 1996. She performed "Caramel".

SUZANNE AT COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY, MARCH 29, 1996

Suzanne did a show at Columbia University on March 29 as part of Women's History Month.

SUZANNE AT KSCA's XMAS SHOW - LOS ANGELES, DECEMBER 9th

Suzanne performed 2 songs as part of KSCA's (101.9fm) Xmas Show at the Universal Ampitheater in Los Angeles - December 9th, 1995. The show will be broadcast, but as of this update we don't know if it will be live or on tape - Check local listing for more info.

SUZANNE AT THE STEPHEN TALK HOUSE - AUGUST 17 & 18

Suzanne performed at the Stephen Talk House on Thursday, August 17th at 9 pm, and Friday, August 18th, at 8 pm. See the "Message Board" section for reviews and setlists.
