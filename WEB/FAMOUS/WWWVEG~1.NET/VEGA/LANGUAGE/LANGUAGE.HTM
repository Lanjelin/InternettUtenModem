Welcome to Language -- The Suzane Vega Magazine -- and to our Home Page on the WWW!

The Dutch-based Suzanne Vega Info Center (SVIC) was founded at the end of November 1987. In February 1988, we published our first issue of Language. Since then we have published 15 issues. We're no longer publishing the magazine, but back issues (see below) are all available. Our purpose was to provide an interesting magazine and to be an Information Center for all the people who are interested in the music of Suzanne Vega.

For three volumes (9 issues) Language was a Dutch magazine with an English summary, but starting with Volume 4 in 1991 Language has been written in English.

If you're interested in ordering back issues, each at $4.00 plus applicable postage, please mention volume and number.For further information, write to the Info Center. When ordering, please include stamps or an International Reply Coupon.

Karien

The Suzanne Vega Info Center is a non-profit organization acknowledged by the Dutch Fanclub Federation and Suzanne Vega's managament (AGF Entertainment Ltd.)
