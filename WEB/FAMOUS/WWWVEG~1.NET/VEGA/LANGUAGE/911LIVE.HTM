SUZANNE VEGA -- LIVE IN HOLLAND!

English Summary

A review of Huub's first concert of Suzanne Vega in Groningen. Marc told Suzanne about the audience, "He told me you were very cold and reserved." But the same Marc was stimulating the 'cold' audience to be very enthusiastic during Men in a War. Only one critic is thinking that the concert is soporific. Other highlights were Small Blue Thing and The Queen and the Soldier. During Tom's Diner Suzanne lost her own lyrics but this painful occurence is at least an evidence that even today some artists have the courage to play without a music tape. Huub was very satisfied with his first concert! 


Huub Ritzema
