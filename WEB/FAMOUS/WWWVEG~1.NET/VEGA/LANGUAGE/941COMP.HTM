COMPETITION

YOU'RE SITTING in your room, punished with terrible boredom? It's raining again outside and you don't know what to do? So why don't you enter this fabulous Suzanne Vega competition?

     Jump right in
     And don't you know
     That the time is now
     And you can't be slow
How to enter? Just send in any kind of work that was inspired by Suzanne Vega. It may be a drawing, a poem, a short story, a cassette with cover songs or your own compositions. Write an essay or a satire. Everything is allowed and welcomed!

First prize: The CD Suzanne Vega in Concert with nine tracks from San Juan Capistrano 17 February 1993. Second prize: The CD single In Liverpool.

The winners will be chosen at the end of February [1995, editor's comment]. So don't hesitate -- send your works to:

     Philipp Hofmann
     Berenskamp 21
     33611 Bielefeld
     Germany



--Philipp Hofmann
