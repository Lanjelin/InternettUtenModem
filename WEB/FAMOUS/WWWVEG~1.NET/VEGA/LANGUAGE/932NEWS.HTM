NEWS AGENCY

* After the last leg of the tour, in New Zealand and Australia (June/July 1993), Suzanne Vega wants to start a new writing period to come up with material for a new album that she would like to record fairly quickly, e.g., early '94. She will very likely work again with Mitchell Froom.

* There is no official live recording of the tour, but the management has taken a few of the tracks that they recorded for the Westwood One radio network in America, and have packaged them for a special live EP for release in Japan, only as a tour- related promotional package. The recording was done at The Coach House in San Juan Capistrano, CA on February 17, 1993. They have included the following tracks: Blood Makes Noise, Marlene On The Wall, In Liverpool, Left Of Center, (If You Were) In My Movie and When Heroes Go Down.

* There is a songbook for the album 99.9 Fø. Published by Warner Bros Publications Inc. One song will be published in this issue.

* In Italy the monthly magazine Il Grifo of 24th of May 1993 published a cartoon-story by Danijel Zezelj. The title of the story is 'Sophia, The Sunny Side Of The Moon'. Zezelj used lyrics from 'When Heroes Go Down' and 'Bad Wisdom' in his work.

* The mystery as mentioned on page 12 of LANGUAGE 1992-2 has been solved by an italian guy. It was published in Buscadero 129-12.
