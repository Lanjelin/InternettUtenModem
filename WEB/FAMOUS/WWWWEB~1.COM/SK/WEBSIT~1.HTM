International Kierkegaard Information

Contains: Information about Kierkegaard Societies and Institutions world-wide,together with details about current Kierkegaard conferences, meetings, courses, also Kierkegaard activities in Australia, plus the electronic edition of The International Kierkegaard Newsletter, the annual scholarly paper containing information about the latest books, articles and international information on Søren Kierkegaard. (Created and maintained by Julia Watkin).

D. Anthony Storm's page on Kierkegaard
This site provides a catalogue of Kierkegaard's writings with commentaries on the works, a longer essay on Plato's and Kierkegaard's Method of Composition Compared, a shorter essay on Kierkegaard's Pseudonymity and Polyonymity and more. Still under construction. Created by D. Anthony Storm.
