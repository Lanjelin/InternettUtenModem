Publish your Paper

Kierkegaard Web Library gives you the opportunity to have your Kierkegaard-paper published on the Internet.
Kierkegaard web Library offers you:
  • Free web-space for the paper.
  • If you are unfamiliar with HTML-formatting, you can submit your paper in ASCII-format.
  • The Feedback Forum gives you an opportunity to get some feedback from the readers.
  • At any time you can choose to remove your paper from the library.
    • Be aware that there is no reviewing or proof reading of the submitted papers. The individual author alone is responsible for the contents of the paper.
    Attach your paper to an e-mail and send it to Kierkegaard Web Library. Add a short abstract (max 100 words).
