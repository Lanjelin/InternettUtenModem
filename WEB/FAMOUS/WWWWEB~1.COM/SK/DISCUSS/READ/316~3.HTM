"What says the Firechief?" (Hvad siger Brand-Majoren) is from
"The Moment" (Oeieblikket) no. 6, published august 23. 1855.
English translation:

"Kierkegaard's Attack upon "Christendom"", 1854-55, tr. Walter Lowrie. Princeton University Press, 1944.
