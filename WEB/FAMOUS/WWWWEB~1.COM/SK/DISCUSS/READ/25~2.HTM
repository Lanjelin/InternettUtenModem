The Cosmic Encyclopedia & Cryptic Poetry



Influenced by Georges Bataille, this work composes
essays, poetry, and dialectical correspondence.
It ia a 37-year work of which I have completed 16
years spanning over 35 volumes. See ABSTRACT,
OUTLINE, & BIO.

The work sees the blossoming of human experience
from the burdening exploration across a variety
of human thresholds. It is the probing of a
sentient life as it is traversing across the
parallels and rhythms of human existence.
