At 19:23:15 on 09/07/96, gc beaton (writers@eastend.com.au) wrote:

Hi - I'm new to this.

Just wondering what other people thought about the way Jostein Gaarder
introduced the thinking of K in his book Sophies World.

I was really impressed - the first time I'd read something that
approached an adequate introduction (or at least an introduction
to an introduction) to K. Too often writers try to apply a rationalist
or objective approach to K's essentially existential *subjective*
thinking.

I appreciated Gaarder's understated historical approach and the way
he related philosophy to literature.
