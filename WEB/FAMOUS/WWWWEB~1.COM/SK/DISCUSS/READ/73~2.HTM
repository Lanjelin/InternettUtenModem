The Subject: faithful vs. unfaithful man
At 14:29:02 on 08/27/96, rock (rock@emoryuniv.edu) wrote:

I have to live by faith, by what K. calls 'dependence'. It is a moment to moment choice I make. I chose to see myself as utterly dependent. I realize that I could never breathe my breath or make my heart beat with any regularity by my own force of will. That both humbles me and pisses me off. I try to make a practice of not being unfaithful to what I realize is my true condition. I try to cheer up in spite of my utter finitude; to make a game of my lack of ultimate control. Is it wonderful to be alive? I chose 'yes'. Faith is giving substance to something (or someone) which is -as yet-UNSEEN.
