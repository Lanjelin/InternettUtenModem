>Mr. Cortese, another theology tidbit: No Catholic with a day of religious education would idolize Mary. The source of your confusion may be the doctrine of the communion of saints, shared by most mainstream Christian denominations (anyone who recites the Nicene or Apostles' Creed). That is, those on earth can ask the souls in paradise to pray for them, and can pray for the souls in Purgatory (lets talk about Purgatory another time -- it may be unique to the Roman Catholics). There is a large difference between asking for prayers from someone, and idolatry of that person.
