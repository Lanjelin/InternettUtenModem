August 18, 1996

Tom Skerritt to Join the Cast of Contact

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Tom Skerritt is in talks to star opposite Jodie Foster, Matthew McConaughey and James Woods in "Contact" for director Robert Zemeckis. The film is based on Carl Sagan's book.
