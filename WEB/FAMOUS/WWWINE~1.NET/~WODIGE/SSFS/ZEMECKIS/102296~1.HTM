October 22, 1996

Zemeckis announces next directing project

From The Hollywood Reporter website:

Oscar-winning director Robert Zemeckis ("Forrest Gump") is reteaming with "Who Framed Roger Rabbit" scribes Peter Seamen and Jeffrey Price to develop "Bad Trout" for Universal Pictures. The film, which Zemeckis will direct as well as produce, concerns a series of strange events at a presidential fishing retreat. No start date or cast are set, but sources said Zemeckis expects to make the film his next movie after "Contact," his current picture for Warner Bros.
