Who we are and how to join us.

The Steven Spielberg Film Society is, as our motto states, a small group of people with a vision in common. Not only are we fans of Steven Spielberg, but film as a medium, art, and entertainment.

Every February, May, August, and November the society publishes a newletter co-edited by Judy Hubbard and Don Archer. The newletter contains columns from the two editors, articles by members of the society, and previously published articles that society members have sent in.
