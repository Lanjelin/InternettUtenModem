January 6, 1997

'Connie & Maury' giving NATPE a sneak preview

From the Hollywood Reporter:

They may not have a syndicator yet, but Connie Chung and Maury Povich will be heading to the big NATPE syndication sales bash in New Orleans next week with DreamWorks SKG to give stations a taste of their new "Connie & Maury" news show for fall 1998. DreamWorks, which is producing the half-hour strip for access, has been in talks with various distributors but has yet to ink a deal. The company will be at the market with a room at the New Orleans Convention Center for a flurry of meetings and talks with stations. Heading the DreamWorks push will be the co-heads of television, Bob Jacquemin and Ken Solomon. Industry observers had assumed DreamWorks would want to lock in a major distributor before going to the National Association of Television Program Executives convention with the idea of doing business for 1998. "We have had talks with distributors, but we believe the next step is to actually produce the show," Jacquemin said. (Steve Brennan)
