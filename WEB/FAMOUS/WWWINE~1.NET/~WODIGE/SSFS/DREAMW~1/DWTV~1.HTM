DreamWorks Television is the television division of DreamWorks SKG. The first show produced was the half-hour sitcom Champs, which ran for only a few episodes on ABC.

Currently there are four shows from DreamWorks Television on the air. Arsenio, a sitcom starring Arsenio Hall, premiered March 5 on ABC. High Incident a cop drama, co-created by Steven Spielberg, is in its second season on ABC. Spin City, starring Michael J. Fox, is a half hour sitcom airing on ABC. Ink, starring Ted Danson and Mary Steenburgen, is a half hour sitcom airing on CBS. Keep up to date on all these shows through the news at the bottom of the page and DreamWorks News.
