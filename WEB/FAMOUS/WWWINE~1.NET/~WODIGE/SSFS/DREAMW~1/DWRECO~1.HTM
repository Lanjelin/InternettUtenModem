DreamWorks Records is the music division of DreamWorks SKG. The division is managed by former Warner Bros. executives Lenny Waronker, Mo Ostin, and Michael Ostin. Albums are to be released through Geffen Records, an affiliate of MCA. The DreamWorks Records logo was designed by the famous artist Roy Lichtenstein.

Currently there are three titles from DreamWorks Records available, more should follow shortly. New releases as well as news will be announced both at the bottom of the page and at the DreamWorks News Page.
