February 20, 1997

Weitzes ink pact at DreamWorks

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Paul and Chris Weitz, screenwriters of the upcoming DreamWorks SKG animated feature "Ants," have signed a nonexclusive, one-year, first-look deal with the studio. The brothers first teamed as screenwriters on the spec comedy "Dances With Dinosaurs," which was purchased by Columbia Pictures. They have since worked on projects for Warner Bros. and Paramount. In addition to "Ants," which stars the voice of Woody Allen, they are at work on a screenplay adaptation of the popular comic book series "Badrock" for New Line Cinema. (Cathy Dunkley)
