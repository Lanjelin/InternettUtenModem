January 20, 1997

Good news for Playa Vista project?

From the Hollywood Reporter:

Playa Vista, a project usually preceded in news stories by the term "much-troubled," may soon have good news. Sources suggest a source should be on board by the end of the month for $180 million in financing for the commercial/residential project, to be anchored by the DreamWorks SKG studio. But negotiations on details, which hinge on issues of investor equity and other sticky matters, could delay a final financing document into next month. Developers are represented in those talks by a three-member panel: Maguire Thomas Partners head Rob Maguire; his business associate, Jim Thomas, recently placed at the project helm in an effort to advance the stalled talks; and John Goolsby, representing Howard Hughes Corp., which owns the land. (Carl DiOrio)
