February 11, 1997

Ad man Sameth moves to DreamWorks

From The Hollywood Reporter:

David Sameth, former co-head of marketing and vp creative affairs for MCA/Universal Pictures Prods., has joined DreamWorks SKG as head of creative advertising. He reports to Terry Press, head of marketing. For Sameth, the post marks a return to studio marketing after spending the past two years as an indie film producer. He served as executive producer on Gramercy Pictures' "I'm Not Rappaport." His association with DreamWorks principal Steven Spielberg goes back to his work on such Amblin projects as "An American Tail" and "The Land Before Time" in addition to "Back to the Future 2 & 3," "Jurassic Park" and "Schindler's List." (Kirk Honeycutt)
