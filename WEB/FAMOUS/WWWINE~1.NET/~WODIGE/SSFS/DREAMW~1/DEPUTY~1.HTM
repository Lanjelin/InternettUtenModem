PLOT:

Set in the Southwest, "The Deputy" centers on a simple, noble sheriff's deputy who is assigned to protect a mobster in the witness protection program, then begins to form a complicated relationship with the man's wife.

January 20, 1997: DreamWorks signs Maria Maggenti to adapt the novel. Kate Capshaw is attached to star.
