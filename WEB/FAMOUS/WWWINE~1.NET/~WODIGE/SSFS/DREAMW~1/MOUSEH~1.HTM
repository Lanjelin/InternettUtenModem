PLOT:

The story of two brothers, Ernie and Lars Smuntz, who inherit a run-down country house from their stingy father -- only to discover that their new abode is actually a historic mansion worth a king's ransom. When they are all set to cash in, they discover that there is someone else on the property who isn't so eager to sell: the world's smartest and meanest mouse who has no intention of relocating without a fight.


DEVELOPMENTS:
