DEVELOPMENTS:

Started June 1, 1995

Much of the computer animation will be done on Silicon Graphics work stations. But then that is pretty typical for film work. Much of the film will be traditionally animated but there are some pretty dramatic elements which are particularly well suited to computer animation.
