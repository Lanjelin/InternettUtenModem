February 12, 1997

Disney's Simon to DreamWorks animation unit

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Walt Disney Television International exec David Simon has joined DreamWorks SKG's Television Animation division. Simon will be the operating head of the division, working in conjunction with Gary Krisel, who is the overall head of DreamWorks TV Animation. Based in London for more than eight years, Simon was responsible for creating Walt Disney Co.'s international TV production division, Buena Vista Prods. International. Under his watch, the division developed and produced more than 40 individually customized TV programs per week. More recently, he headed the newly formed broadcast division for Walt Disney TV International where he successfully launched and managed Disney Channels in Taiwan, Great Britain and Australia.
