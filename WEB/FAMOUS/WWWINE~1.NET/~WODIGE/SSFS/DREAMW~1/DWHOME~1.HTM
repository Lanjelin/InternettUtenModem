DreamWorks SKG began in October of 1994 when producer/director Steven Spielberg, former Disney Chair Jeffrey Katzenberg, and film/music producer David Geffen announced their plans to build a new entertainment studio. Since then, the company has announced its plans to build a major studio, completed production on its first film, released several recorded albums, produced several television shows and has a budding interactive deparment.
