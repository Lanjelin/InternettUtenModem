January 15, 1997

Kaplan wooed for Chung strip

From the Hollywood Reporter:

DreamWorks SKG has entered the sweepstakes for veteran ABC News producer Rick Kaplan. Sources said Tuesday that Kaplan is in talks with the company about becoming the executive producer of the new syndicated program starring Connie Chung and Maury Povich. Kaplan, who is now executive producer, special projects for ABC, is also in negotiations with CNN about taking a senior programming position. He is weighing both offers as he is expected to leave ABC after a 17-year career at the network. "The only thing that would be surprise me is if he stayed at ABC," a network insider said. Kaplan is largely credited with creating ABC's "Nightline," which he produced for five years. (Stephen Battaglio)
