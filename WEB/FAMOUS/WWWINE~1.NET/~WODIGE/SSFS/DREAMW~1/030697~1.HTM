March 6, 1997

Leoni becomes 'Impact' player in sci-fi picture

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Téa Leoni is set to star in "Deep Impact," a co-production between Paramount and DreamWorks SKG. The film, inspired by George Pal's 1951 sci-fi classic "When Worlds Collide" and Arthur C. Clark's book "The Hammer of Gods," is scheduled to begin production April 21. "Impact" concerns the discovery of a comet on a collision course with Earth and the ensuing massive international race against time to save the human race. The Richard Zanuck-David Brown production, written by Michael Tolkin and Bruce Joel Rubin, will be helmed by Mimi Leder, who recently wrapped DreamWorks' "The Peacemaker." Leoni stars in NBC's "The Naked Truth." Her recent film credits include "Bad Boys." She is repped by Risa Shapiro at ICM. (Cathy Dunkley)
