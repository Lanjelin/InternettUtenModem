February 5, 1997

'Igor' - Animated DreamWorks TV project

From The Hollywood Reporter:

NEW YORK -- The Fox Kids Network unveiled a 1997-98 Saturday morning children's schedule Tuesday with seven new shows that include the first animated series from DreamWorks Television -- "Igor" -- an animated comedic take on the Frankenstein legend. The other new series in the Saturday lineup include "Mowgli: The New Adventures of the Jungle Book"; "Ninja Turtles: The Next Mutation"; "Sam & Max"; "Silver Surfer"; and "Home to Rent." (Stephen Battaglio)
