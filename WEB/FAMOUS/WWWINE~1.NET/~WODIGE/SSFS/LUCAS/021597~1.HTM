February 15, 1997

'Star Wars' Reclaims Title

From the Reuters/Variety Entertainment Summary:

``Star Wars'' regained the crown as the top-grossing film in North America when it became the first film to break the $400 million box office barrier Thursday (Feb. 13). The 1977 production ceded its crown to ``E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial'' in 1982. It reached the new height of $400.1 million exactly two weeks after its latest reissue. ``E.T.'' has grossed $399.8 million. ''Star Wars'' opened to record business in May 1977, grossing about $279 million to dethrone ``Jaws'' as the domestic box office champ. A reissue the following year added about $44 million to its gross, and the overseas box office put another $200 million in the coffers.
