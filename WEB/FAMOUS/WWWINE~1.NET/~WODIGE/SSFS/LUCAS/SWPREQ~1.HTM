PLOT (concerning Episodes II and III also):

Straight from George Lucas (Star Wars Insider Issue #26): "It's a story about Ben Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker and how we got to the point where Obi-Wan Kenobi was waiting in the middle of the desert for something to happen. It's also about how Darth Vader got to be who he is, and how the Emperor came to power. It starts out with the Emperor not in power, then it keeps progressing. It'll be one twelve-hour movie when it's all finished."

"In the first film, [the main characters] are very young, although Obi-Wan is thirtyish. In Episodes Two and Three, Anakin is around twenty. Anakin is about the same age as Luke in Episode Four, and Obi-Wan is about forty then. The second two films take place almost continuously, a couple of years in between. At the end of the third film, Anakin is twenty-two, so A New Hope is about twenty years later when Obi-Wan is in his sixties."

Producer RickMcCallum says that C-3PO and R2-D2 will be in the prequels, but in a different form. Yoda will also be in the prequels.

Current Star Wars Prequel Rumor: (To change frequently! These are not necessarily true, but they're popping up everywhere...so who knows??)
Ewan McGregor will play Young Obi-Wan and Natalie Portman will play the Queen.
Morgan Freeman and Kate Winslet are also rumored for roles.

Febraury 15, 1997: Lucas talks on Entertainment Tonight. The lowdown:

  • There will be six episodes (three prequels, three sequels to be completed later)
  • Prequels involve Anakin as a child, falling in love, tempation and fall from grace.
  • Anakin does not get the Vader suit until Obi-Wan injures him in combat (see the Star Wars novelization)
  • James Earl Jones said he will be the voice of Vader in Episode 3.

Sept. 27, 1996---Lucas confirms that he will direct Star Wars: Episode I
Sept. 22, 1996---Star Wars Prequel distribution question (article from Time Magazine)
Sept. 9, 1996----Star Wars Prequels to be shot in Leavesden Studios

  • The first will be shot separately and numbers 2 and 3 together.
  • The storyboards are being drawn for some of the larger action sequences.
  • Some vehicle designs have been approved, creature designs are underway.
  • Second and Third-unit shooting begins in August of 1997.
    Principle photography begins in fall 1997.
  • Prelimenary casting has begun for young Anakin Skywalker and "the young queen."
  • Locations have been scouted in Morocco and Tunisia. Filming will take place in Central Asia and Portugal as well.
  • The first script has been turned in according to McCallum.
