Harrison Ford (Indiana Jones)

PLOT:

Indy's fourth adventure. Rumors had said first it was about the lost city of Atlantis but then a script "Indiana Jones and the Sons of Darkness" was released over the Internet about Noah's Ark (both ideas have proven false).


DEVELOPMENTS:

December 1996 --- Producer Rick McCallum says "we have a great script but it is difficult to determine a time to shoot due to George, Steven, and Harrison's schedules."

August 1996 --- Producer Rick McCallum says "we're still working on the script...Harrison Ford has several films lined up and Steven has his new company, Dreamworks, to deal with...There has been some press saying it isn't going to happen, but it's not true."
