February 18, 1997

Burns' latest mission: 'Ryan' for Spielberg

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Edward Burns is set to star in Steven Spielberg's "Saving Private Ryan" opposite Tom Hanks for DreamWorks SKG and Paramount Pictures. Burns will play a key member of the rescue squad in the World War II drama about a soldier forced to go behind enemy lines to save a trapped private during the first days of the Allied invasion of Europe. "Ryan" is being produced by Mark Gordon ("Speed") and Gary Levinsohn and was written for the screen by Robert Rodat and Frank Darabont. Burns wrote, starred in and directed "The Brothers McMullen" and "She's the One." He is repped by Robert Newman and Joe Funicello at ICM. (Cathy Dunkley)
