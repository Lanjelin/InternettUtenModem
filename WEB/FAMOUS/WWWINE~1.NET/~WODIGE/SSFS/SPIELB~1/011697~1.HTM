January 16, 1997

Storyopolis spins tales with Spielberg, Harlin

From the Hollywood Reporter:

A new book to be published in association with the Starbright Foundation and its patron, Steven Spielberg; an animated movie that will be co-produced with Renny Harlin and Geena Davis; and a feature film that has Anthony Hopkins attached to star as Napoleon are among several new projects unveiled Wednesday by Storyopolis, the family entertainment company founded by software billionaire Paul Allen. Through his connection with Starbright, Spielberg is supervising the development of an untitled book based on a fairy tale that will be authored by several different celebrities, "with each celebrity telling it from a different character's point of view in a 'Rashomon'-like way," said senior vp Fonda Snyder. Snyder is a principal of the company along with Allen, president Abbie Phillips and executive vp Dawn Heinrichs. (Stephen Galloway)
