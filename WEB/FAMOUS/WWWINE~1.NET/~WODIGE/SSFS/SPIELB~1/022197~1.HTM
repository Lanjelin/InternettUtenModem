February 21, 1997

'Amistad,' 'Gains' seem to be in the same boat

From The Hollywood Reporter:

Someone has beaten Steven Spielberg to the punch. A film having its premiere in Africa this weekend bears several striking similarities to Spielberg's "Amistad," which entered production this week. "Ill Gotten Gains," which will debut at the PanAfrican Film Festival in Ouagadougou in the West African country of Burkina Faso (formerly Upper Volta), concerns a fictional uprising on a slave ship in 1869. "Amistad" relates the true story of a revolt on the Spanish slave ship Amistad by a "cargo" of 53 Africans in 1839. Even more startling is the fact that both films share an actor and the same ship for a set. Djimon Hounsou, a top model in Paris who has appeared in "Stargate" and the upcoming "Deep Rising" for Hollywood Pictures, has major roles in both films. (Kirk Honeycutt)
