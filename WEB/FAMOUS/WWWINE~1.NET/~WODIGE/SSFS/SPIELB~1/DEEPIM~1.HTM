PLOT:

A contemporary action thriller that follows three characters through the chaos that ensues when a group of scientists discover that a comet is on a deadly collision course with Earth. Inspired by George Pal's 1951 sci-fi classic "When Worlds Collide" and Arthur C. Clark's book "The Hammer of Gods."
