January 14, 1997

Spielberg eyes British outpost for 'Ryan' shoot

From the Hollywood Reporter:

LONDON -- Steven Spielberg's World War II drama "Saving Private Ryan," starring Tom Hanks, looks set to shoot in the United Kingdom, possibly in a disused government Ministry of Defense property. Scouts on the Paramount Pictures and DreamWorks co-production are currently searching for locations and studio space in the United Kingdom, a British Film Commission spokeswoman confirmed Friday. If it shoots here, "Saving Private Ryan" will join a growing list of major U.S. films preparing to shoot in the United Kingdom this year, including New Line Cinema's "Lost in Space" and Warner Bros.' "The Avengers." (Louise Bateman)
