PLOT:

From the Sony movie website:
THE MASK OF ZORRO is a sweeping adventure of love and honor, of tragedy and triumph set against the Spanish conquest of Mexico. It's twenty years since Don Diego de la Vega (Anthony Hopkins) fought oppression as the legendary romantic hero Zorro, and now he must find a successor to stop Raphael Montero (Stuart Wilson), the corrupt, once-powerful official who robbed him of everything he loved, and left him to rot in a Mexican prison. Antonio Banderas stars as Alejandro Murietta, a drifter with a troubled past whom Don Diego transforms into the fearless romantic hero he once was. Armed with his mask, sword, whip and jet-black stallion Tornado, the new Zorro embarks on a mission to stop Montero's plot to buy California, set right twenty years of wrongs and at last bring justice to the people enslaved by Montero's greed.


DEVELOPMENTS:

March 6, 1997: Misfortunes on the set, including the flu.

February 6, 1997: Shooting begins January 27 in Mexico City. Stuart Wilson and Matthew Letscher are also in the cast. Expanded crew.

February 4, 1997: Release date December 1997 and expanded plot summary/character names.

December 17, 1996: Catherine Zeta Jones joins the cast.

November 26, 1996: Anthony Hopkins signed to star as older Zorro.

November 12, 1996: Shooting begins in January. Tri-Star looking for a partner in financing the $58 million film.
