February 13, 1997

'Flintstones' slate another big screen run

From The Hollywood Reporter:

It's yabba-dabba doo time again at Amblin Entertainment, where the saber-tooth pencils are being sharpened for another whack at the Bedrock folks. While a sequel to "The Flintstones" has been technically in the cards since the first film was released, Fred and Wilma's second outing now appears chiseled in stone. Seems top screenwriters Jim Cash and Jack Epps Jr. ("Top Gun," "Dick Tracy," "Turner & Hooch," "Sister Act") are chipping away at a new screenplay. Amblin will produce for Universal Pictures. Sources indicated that "Flintstones" helmer Brian Levant has been approached to direct the new film. (Cathy Dunkley)
