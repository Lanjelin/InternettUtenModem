PLOT:

This World War II action-drama presents Hanks as a soldier who must go behind enemy lines to save a trapped private.

DEVELOPMENTS:

February 18, 1997: Edward Burns signs on to play a member of the rescue squad.

January 14, 1997: Will shoot this year in the United Kingdom, possibly in a disused government Ministry of Defense property.

November 7, 1996: Will be in production after Spielberg finishes "The Lost World" and "Amistad."

August 11, 1996: The film is officially announced, it is rumor no more.
