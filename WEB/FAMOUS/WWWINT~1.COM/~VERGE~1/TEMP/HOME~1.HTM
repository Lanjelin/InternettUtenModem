This is meant to be a tribute to the great director Ingmar Bergman of Sweden.

Biography
Includes biographical data, profile, commentary, and bibliography sections.

Career
Bergman's amazingly multi-faceted and prolific career is covered, with

as well as information on his special repertory company of actors.

Gallery
The gallery is an index to all of the inline images in this page, as well as
links to other images available on the 'Net.

News & FAQs
This section includes the latest Bergman news, as well as
what's new on this page, our best attempts to answer your questions, and
(eventually, we hope) a cyberspace forum.
