February 1997


Göteborg Film Festival

Swedish film buffs are in for a feast, as the Göteborg Film Festival gets underway on 31 January and runs to 9 February. Check out the Festival's homepage for more details. Special note: in the Festival's Cinemix '97 programme, Bibi Andersson will conduct a seminar, "Cinematics in a State of Siege," about Bosnian film, on Monday, 3 February. Her film, Meeting in Sarajevo [Möte i Sarajevo] (made with Mustafa Mustafic and Staffan Roos), will also be screened.



October 1996


Bergman festival in Montréal

A retrospective of Bergman's films is being presented at the Conservatoire d'art cinématographique de Montréal, from October 4 to November 30.


Eva Bergman on Swedish TV

Bergman's daughter, Eva, who directs at the Backa Theatre in Gothenburg, will have three of her productions broadcast on Swedish television this fall.



September 1996


Bergman screening at the Toronto International Film Festival

Director Bille August will be presenting Bergman's Cries and Whispers (1972) in the Festival's Dialogues series, where directors screen and discuss selected films of "profound personal importance." Says August: "I saw Cries and Whispers the year after I finished film school, and it affected me very deeply, opening a new realm of possibility for the cinema. Bergman said in his autobiography, The Magic Lantern: 'When film is not a document, it is a dream.' Cries and Whispers is one of Bergman's best films, perhaps his best, because it erases the line between dream and reality, between dream and fiction. You cannot achieve anything greater in film."
