Whatever happened to Bengt Ekerot?
Bengt Ekerot, the actor who was so memorable as Death in The Seventh Seal (1957), worked only twice with Bergman in films (the other occasion was The Magician (1958)). This, despite Bergman's evidently high regard for the actor—he has referred to Ekerot as a "genius." Ekerot directed at the Royal Dramatic Theatre and in radio. In the 1960s, he continued acting sporadically in films, including several appearances for the director Jan Halldoff. Ekerot died in 1971, aged 51. If anyone has more information about Bengt Ekerot, please let us know.
