We hope that this homepage will be useful to casual Bergman fans as well as Bergman scholars. For the latter, we hope to provide a comprehensive bibliography of published literature available on Bergman and his works. It will be compiled through our own research and through various film indexes. In particular, we highly recommend Birgitta Steene's Ingmar Bergman: a guide to references and resources—an invaluable source of information for any student of Bergman.
