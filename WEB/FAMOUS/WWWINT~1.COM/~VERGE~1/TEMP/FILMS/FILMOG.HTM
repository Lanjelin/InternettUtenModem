The following is a complete chronological listing of theatrical films, as well as Swedish television films which received theatrical release elsewhere, on which Bergman worked as director and/or writer.

For each film, there is a page containing such information as production data, credits, cast, commentary, and bibliography. As well, we have attempted to give our own five-star ratings for those films that we have seen. (Please remember that our ratings are meant only to serve as a short-hand guide!)

An additional listing of alternate English-language titles is also available, in our online version.
