Original title: Smultronstället ["The wild strawberry patch"]

Distribution: Svensk Filmindustri

Premiere: 26 December 1957 (Röda Kvarn, Stockholm, Uppsala, Hälsingborg; Fontänen, Stockholm, Malmö)

Running time: 91 minutes

Language: Swedish

Filmed: on location at Lake Vättern, at the University town of Lund, and at Dalarö and Ängö in the Stockholm archipelago, and at Råsunda Studios; from 2 July to 27 August 1957.
