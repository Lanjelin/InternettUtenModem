Film parody:
A 15-minute film parody of The Seventh Seal (and Wild Strawberries), entitled Da duwe [The dove], was made in 1972 by Sidney Davis, George Coe, and Anthony Lover (Coe-Davis Ltd. Productions; Pyramid Distributors).
