October - and still dryfly

This month midges, bibioes and other two-wings are the main hatches
and the reason for rising graylings. The hatches and the rises may last
all day long, and even a bit after sunset. An actual fly:


Wingmidge


 Photo: OT Ljøstad

My 11. choice among the 12 flies I use

Tying/Materials:
  • Hook:Partridge GRS3A/L3A, size # 16-22
  • Tread:Black flymaster 6/0
  • Tale:Some black cockhacklefibres
  • Body:Sparingly dubbed black Fly-Rite
  • Wing:Small amount of white polyyarn. Tyed in nearly as a roof.
  • Frontbody:a bit thicker than the rest.
  • Hackle:Black cockhackle, tied around the hook.
