Holmbo Camping
N 2450 Rena
Norway
 Owner: Kari Østhagen, Tel +47 62 44 45 37.

The camp is located 12 km (8 miles) east of Rena center, follow road 215.
It has great view to Rena river,
running 10 meters away!. Open all year.

The camp consists of 10 cabins with 1-3 rooms and 2-4 beds, and caravan- and tentareas.
40 current-places. Sanitary and fishfreezing facillities.
Kiosk, sale of fishing license and state fishing fee.
Boat and canoe for hire.

Distance from Oslo: 175 km (108 miles).
