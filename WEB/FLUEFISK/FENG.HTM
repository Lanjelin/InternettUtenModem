Myself



I'm a flyfishing-maniac at the age of 36. I live 2 minutes
footwalk from the river Glomma, and makes 100 fishing trips during the year.
I spend a lot of time catching grayling and trout in the rivers of Glomma/Rena,
but fishes as well for pike in Rendalen, trout in Finnmark,
sea-trout in Oslofjorden and the swedish coast, and trout in Femundsmarka.
I am employeed as a computer-consultant, mostly programming.
At this moment I'm working with a fishing-guide about Glomma on CD-ROM.
I am sorry for the absence of quality of some of the pictures.
They will be replaced during this autumn.

Images on the CD-ROM will be of top quality.
