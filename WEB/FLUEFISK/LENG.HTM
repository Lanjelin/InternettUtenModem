Landfastøyen Gård(farm) and Camping
2560 Alvdal
 Owner: Anne Marie and Rein Landfastøyen. Tel: +47 62 48 97 54.

The Camp is situated 13 km south of Alvdal center, at Glommas westside.
Follow signs from RV 3, 15 km south of Alvdal center.

The camp has two different areas:
Area 1, in conjunction to the farm and has 5 cabins with 2-7 beds.
Currentsites for 10 campingcars. Sanitary with washingroom, shower and toilets
also for wheelchairs.
Area 2, riverside Glomma, appr. 200 m. from the farm. Large area for campingcars
and tents, but no current. Water and dryclosette and short to the fishingareas.

Sale of fishinglicense for Glomma, but also other areas. Boat for hire.
Lot of hiking and sightseeing possibillities.

Further information, Contact: Anne Marie & Rein Landfastøyen, 2560 Alvdal.
Tel. +47 6248 9754
