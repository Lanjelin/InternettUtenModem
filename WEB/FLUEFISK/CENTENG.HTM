The hotel is located in Elverum's main street, close to Glomma river
and the museums.
96 rooms with 150/200 beds. 7 group meeting rooms and 10 conference
rooms, accommodating up to 300 persons.
Restaurants, bar and night-club.
Restaturant outdoor in summer.

From 1996 Hotel Central is a Rica Partner Hotel, which means that all
of Rica's advantages may be used.

Distance from Oslo: 135 km (85 miles).
