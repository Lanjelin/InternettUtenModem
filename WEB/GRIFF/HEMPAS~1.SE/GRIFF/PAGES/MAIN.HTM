Welcome to MY home page...make yourself at home and chase all the cats you find here...

Maybe you haven't seen me before...
Let me introduce myself...
I am Griff - a surfin' border collie. I'm five years old and I love sheep, cattle and chasing cats. I live in southern Sweden (Falkenberg). Unfortunately I have to share my house with three cats and two humans. The humans aren't that bad: they give me food and candy and play with me.
The cats however...
The cats' food taste pretty good - and I think that that is the only good thing about having cats in the house. And of course that I can chase them day AND night...and ... and...err
Well I guess I've covered the subject "WHAT CATS ARE GOOD FOR". I don't understand why my humans want the silly creatures inside. But as you all know humans can be very stupid...
I hope you'll have a Happy Easter (and that you'll have lots of cats to chase...) See ya!
Griff
