HOTEL IGDLO

Hotel Igdlo is a modern hotel with 15 rooms, all with private toilets and shower, telephone and television. From the outdoor terrase you can sit and enjoy a meal while watching the whales and seals swimming close by.

The hotel has a restaurant which seats 80 people. It offers an international coursine as well as traditional Greenlandic food.

The hotel has conference facilities with AV equipment, please see the special pricelist
