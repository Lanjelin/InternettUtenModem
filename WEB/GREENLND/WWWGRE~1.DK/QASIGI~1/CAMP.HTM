    Camping in the Qasigiannguit area

    Now there is a chance for all those intersted to go camping in Qasigiannguit and experience the Greenlandic nature at close hand.
    The campsite lies in the outskirts of Qasigiannguit, where the tall mountains rises to the sky and the fiord runs out into the Diskobay. There is a small cabin at the site with kitchen and toilet facilities. Showers can be taken at the Hotel Igdlo.Qasigiannguit Turistservice rents out equipment and they are also in charge of bookings.

    Hiking in Qasigiannguit

    Qasigiannguit is ideal for hiking, both short and longer trips.
    Some of the daytrips are marked with painted dots, mostly the tracks are easy to follow.The Icecap can be reached in a couple of days hike from Orpisoq. The Touristoffice have descriptions of several tours, you can get copies by contacting the office.
