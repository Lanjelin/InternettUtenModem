Accommodation in Qasigiannguit

Now there is a chance for all those interested to go camping in Qasigiannguit and experience the Greenlandic nature at close hand.

The camp site lies in the outskirts of Qasigiannguit, where the tall mountains rises to the sky and the fiord runs out into the Diskobay. There is a small cabin at the site with kitchen and toilet facilities. Showers can be taken at the Hotel Igdlo.

Qasigiannguit Touristservice (located at Hotel Igdlo) rents out equipment, see the price list, and they are also in charge of bookings.

You can also rent rooms at the local school from 20th June to 31st July. The price for a double room is DKK 500 pr. night. You share bathroom and kitchen with three other rooms.

The possibilities for excursions in the area are plenty. Hiking in the mountains, fishing in lakes, rivers and at sea, sail trips to deserted villages and for those who wants adventure the Inland Ice can be reached in a couple of days hike. If you want a guide on the trip this can be arranged.
