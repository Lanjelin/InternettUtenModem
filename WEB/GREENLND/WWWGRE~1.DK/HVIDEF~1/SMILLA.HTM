Photos from Ilulissat

This is the stunning Arctic world you experience in the movie Miss Smilla's Sense of Snow. All pictures shot on location in Greenland are from the breathtaking scenery around our town.

Hotel Hvide Falk was proud to host most of the crew, when film director Bille August made the movie based on Peter Høghs novel Miss Smilla's Sense of Snow.

Come visit Hotel Hvide Falk and Ilulissat. Miss Smilla's world unplugged.
