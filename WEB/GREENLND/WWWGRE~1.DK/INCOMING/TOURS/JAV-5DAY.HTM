ILULISSAT - The town with the World's most productive glacier as neighbour

Once we have seen the 100 meter high majestic icebergs, our mind will never be the same again.
Scientificly it has not been proved, but several have told us that when you sail among the icebergs, many of the daily life problems become insignificant.
We take a fraction of the enormous strength and courage these icebergs radiate with us back home, and uses this strength in our daily life.

We believe that the experience of the icebergs, make us better decision-makers
