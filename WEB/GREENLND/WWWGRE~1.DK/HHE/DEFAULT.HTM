Challinging Arctic surroundings
Nuuk (Godthåb) is the center of the financial and political life of Greenland. The situation of the town, at the entrance to a very large fiord area, is perfect for excursions into the exciting nature of Greenland. In Nuuk itself you may get some very interesting experiences, for example in the old part of the town from colonial times. The modern national museum of Greenland exhibits extremely well preserved, 500 years-old Inuit mummies.
