Arctic Adventure ApS. is the biggest privately owned operator of tourist programmes and tours into Greenland.

Our headoffice is situated in Copenhagen, Denmark, but regional offices in Greenland are operated during winter- and summerseason.

Arctic Adventure tours are operated during season and:

  • all departures are open for individual booking and are sold through out the world by local agents
  • all departures are guaranteed
  • all departures are guided with multilingual guides


    Please note: Arctic Adventure ApS are only selling tours through local agents around the world, we are not selling directly to the public.

    You are always welcome to contact Arctic Adventure if you have any questions or if you do not find any selling agent in your country.
