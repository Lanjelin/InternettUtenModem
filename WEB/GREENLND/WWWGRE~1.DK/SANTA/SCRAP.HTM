The Santa Summit
17 live Santa's from all over the World met last year in Greenland to the Santa Summit. How do Santa's prepare for the season?		 Santa of the Year
During the Santa Summit the recipient of the Santa of the Year Award was announced: President Nelson Mandela.
Santa recieved by Mandela
December last year President Nelson Mandela recieved the Santa of the Year Award in his office in Pretoria: 100.000 US Dollars for charity work among homeless children in South Africa.		 Last Years Greetings
Santa never forgets all the beautiful things of life. One of them was the more than 1,500 Christmas greetings in last years guestbook. Wishes from all over the world. Santa keeps the book.
