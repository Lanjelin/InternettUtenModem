Santa's Christmas Contest


Santa names the winner of a free
cruise holiday in Greenland for two persons!
Sponsored by Adventure Cruise Greenland & Greenlandair Inc. & SAS

Lea Lyster
9 years old, Denmark


Santa Claus looks forward to welcome Lea to his Arctic homeland
on a one-week cruise for two persons along the coast of Greenland!
Flights from Copenhagen, domestic flight and one weeks cruise
with full board for two persons is included in Santa's invitation.

Lea will sail from Kangerlussuaq to Ilulissat onboard the M/S Disko
and enjoy a brand new way to experience Greenland with
Adventure Cruise Greenland

Santa Claus enjoys every drawing he received during December
and it wasn't easy for him to make up his mind. Actually, he had to ask
a few children from Nuuk to help him pick the very best drawing.
Together they studied all drawings several times - then they agreed
to name Lea Lyster the winner.

Santa wants to thank every participant by sending them a little gift.
