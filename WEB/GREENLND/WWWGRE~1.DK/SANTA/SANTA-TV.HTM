News coverage and Christmas entertainment programs

A camara crew follows Santa Summit. TV stations can receive fresh shootings during the summit via EBU. Please contact us for details.

Two 29.30 minutes productions from the Santa Summit will be avaible on the 30th November for Christmas prime-time entertainment. See below.

Program title: Santa Summit in Greenland

PROGRAMME TITLE: Santa Summit in Greenland
PROGRAMME TYPE: Factual Entertainment
LENGTH: 29.30 minutes
MEDIA: Betacam SP with stereo sound.
PRODUCTION CO.: Zoom Film & TV Ltd. Denmark
PRODUCER: Santa Claus of Greenland Foundation

Santa Summit in Greenland follows the events which will take place during the worlds first Santa Summit Meeting in Ilulissat (Jakobshavn), 250 km north of the arctic circle in Greenland.

A camera crew will follow 20 Santa Claus’s from all corners of the globe through a 3 day programme packed with exciting events in the breathtaking surroundings of snow covered Ilulissat (Jakobshavn) and the Disko Bay where the most productive ice- glacier in the northern hemisphere pounds out 20 million tons of ice every day.

  • Santa Summit in Greenland is an entertaining Christmas programme for the whole family and highlights of this programme include:
  • The unveiling of Santa’s giant sleigh
  • Santa School. Even Santa Claus needs a refresher course now and then!
  • Compelling footage of the Santa Claus delegation sailing through the icebergs and flying by Sikorsky Helicopter over the ice-floe and landing at the ice-glacier mouth
  • The first Official World Santa Games. The Santa Claus’s compete for the gold medal in disciplines such as Tree decoration, Ho-ho-ho’ing and the Santa Race featuring a dramatic climb up a specially built chimney. The children of Ilulissat (Jakobshavn) are the spectators and judges in the games.
  • Short excerpt of Christmas concert in Ilulissat church with Traditional Greenland Choir and the Australian tenor Constantino Di Angelo.
See below for tecnical information.

Program title: A Christmas Concert in the Arctic

PROGRAMME TITLE: A Christmas Concert in the Arctic
PROGRAMME TYPE: Music
LENGTH: 29.30 minutes
MEDIA: Betacam SP with stereo sound.
PRODUCTION CO.: Zoom Film & TV Ltd. Denmark
PRODUCER: Santa Claus of Greenland Foundation

The Christmas Concert in the Arctic could not have found a more appropriate setting than that of the beautiful old church in Ilulissat (Jakobshavn) Greenland, which is one of the most northerly and picturesque towns in the world.

This performance will be recorded live and will feature the marvellous tenor voice of Constantino Di Angelo from Australia singing classic Christmas songs backed by the harmonious 4 part harmony of the traditional Greenland choir in their exquisitely woven and colourful traditional costumes.

To complete the Christmas atmosphere the programme includes footage of the magnificent scenery of snow and ice in Greenland, with exclusive shots from the most productive ice glacier in the world in Ilulissat (Jakobshavn), which produces the majestic ice-bergs that float out into the Disko Bay and from there all the way to Newfoundland.
