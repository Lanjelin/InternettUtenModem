Nelson Mandela Children's Fund

"The Nelson Mandela Children's Fund is a trust established for charitable and educational objectives, in the interest of the public.

The Trust focuses on the plight of youth in need and in particular on:

  • Those who are homeless
  • Those who have not had the benefit of formal education
  • Those in detention or prison

    It's genesis was a unique event which took place on 16 June 1994 when one man, our President Nelson Mandela, pledged one-third of his salary for five years as his contribution to found and perpetuate the fund."

    (Quote from the official booklet about the fund.)
