Santa's Guest Book 1995
with Christmas messages



People from all around the World contributed to the wishing list of Planet Earth

During the Christmas month 1995 Santa Claus of Greenland asked children of all ages and all nationalities to write in his guest book. To tell him about their Christmas wishes and their hopes for the future of our planet.

Santa Claus did his very best to fullfil the wishes during his global tour with Rudolf and the fellow-reindeers in front of his sledge. But even Santa needs help to make some of the wishes come true. Now it's your time to take part in the effort to save our planet and to give a decent life to all of it's children. That's how you learn that Santa is alive.

Thanks to all of you who wrote in Santa's Guest Book. And a happy new year to all citizens of Planet Earth from Santa Claus of Greenland!

Santa's busy round the year. Stay tuned - bookmark this site and come back!
