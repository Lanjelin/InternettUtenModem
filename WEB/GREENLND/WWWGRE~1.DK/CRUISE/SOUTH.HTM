Take a theme cruise with M/S IOFFE in southern and eastern Greenland


See glaciers, history and culture from the sea on a three, six or eight-day cruise from Narsarsuaq. Visit the town of Narsaq and take a close look at the huge glaciers in Ikersuaq/Bredefjord.
See magnificent icebergs and green mountain sides scattered with sheep-farming stations and visit picturesque towns and settlements.

If you choose to spend six or eight days on board, you will travel via Prince Christian Sound to some of the most isolated areas on the east coast of Greenland, which few people have visited before. On the way, M/S IOFFE calls at towns such as Qaqortoq, Nanortalik and Greenland's most southerly point, Cape Farewell. We visit areas where Eric the Red first set foot on Greenland, as well as historic Norse ruins and hot springs. Throughout the cruise the ship's guides will expand on the theme of the day, which varies from "An introduction to Greenland" and "Greenland's past and future" to " Glaciology, ice and icebergs" or "The east coast - nature and history".

The cruise is like an expedition - no one has control over the ice and weather conditions but, with local guides and sturdy rubber dinghies, we won't miss any opportunities for adventure.

On a theme cruise on M/S IOFFE you won't just have fantastic adventures, you'll also take a great deal of knowledge about the world's largest island home with you.
