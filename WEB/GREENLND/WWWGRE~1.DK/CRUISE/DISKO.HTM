M/S Disko

Built as an Arctic ice-class ship in 1968 at the Svendborg shipyard, Denmark, the M/S Disko is a piece of Greenland's history, having served as a scheduled passenger and freight - carrying coastal vessel until 1995. After an extensive refit and modernisation in 1996/87, M/S Disko will sail as a cruise ship, calling at some of the many important tourist attractions along Greenland's west coast and around Disko Bay.

M/S Disko is fitted with modern, state-of-the-art equipment throughout and fulfils all official requirements, particularly those which relate to safety.
