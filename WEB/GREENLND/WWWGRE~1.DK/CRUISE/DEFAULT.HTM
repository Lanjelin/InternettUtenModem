Greenland Guide Index | Greenland Tourism Info-desk


The aim of an adventure Cruise in Greenlandic waters is not to cover as many sea miles as humanly possible. Scenic adventures take top priority. You won't be invited to the "Captain's table". There are no spinning roulette wheels. There is no big band playing "My way". And you can leave your dinner suits and evening gowns at home.

What you actually get is something completely different - a comfortable stay on board a ship where the perfect blend of adventures in both traditional and modern Greenland await you like pearls on a string. The cruise passes through wild and undisturbed scenery, where you truly sense that you are travelling along the coast of the world's largest island, where landing sometimes only take place using one of the ship's rubber dinghies. You can go on exiting excursions when you go ashore in towns and meet modern Greenland. You will encounter traditional Greenland in the villages.

From the ship you'll see bird cliffs, whales, the Midnight Sun, glaciers and enormous icebergs, giving you memories of an Adventure Cruise in Greenland, "the land of the people", which will stay with you for ever.

The price of the cruise includes transfers from and to the airport on arrival and departure and full board while on the ship. The ship's permanent guides will be your hosts for the duration of the cruise. Every day "lessons" will be held about Greenland's scenery, culture, etc, and information about the day's route and what the next sea mile holds is given out on a daily basis.

There are guided tours of the towns and villages along the way and it is also possible to take part in additional excursions (for an extra fee) as a supplement to the standard programme. Depending on the destination, there will be hiking trips to the trips to the inland ice, visits to tanneries, Greenlandic buffets, kayak sailing, helicopter trips, demonstrations of traditional life, etc.

Welcome aboard.

Adventure awaits. Don't miss out!
