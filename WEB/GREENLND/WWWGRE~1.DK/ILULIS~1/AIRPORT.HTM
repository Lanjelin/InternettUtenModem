Ilulissat Airport
Greenland


Ilulissat Airport invites you to pay a visit!

Ilulissat Airport is situated 1.5 Nm NE of the town of Ilulissat.

The Airport is constructed as a STOL airport and was inaugurated 1984. Since then it has served as the main gate to the Disko Bay area and to the northern districts of Uummannaq and Upernavik.

The airport is connected daily by Greenlandair with DH7, SK61 and a number of other planes, and it is the base of Air Alpha Greenland who operates a number of smaller helicopters. Other planes visit the airport frequently.
