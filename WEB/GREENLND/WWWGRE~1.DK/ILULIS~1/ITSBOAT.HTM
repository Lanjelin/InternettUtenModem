General:
During the ice-free period we arrange boat trips on our own boat which is approved for passenger transport and has an experienced skipper. Coffee and tea are served on all trips and packed lunch can be brought along. We recommend that you wear warm, windproof clothing, gloves and a cap or hat as it is very cold on the water.

BOAT TRIP TO ICE FJORD

This boat trip takes you to the outlet of Ilulissat Ice Fjord where the gigantic icebergs are grounded. On the trip you can enjoy the spectacle of these colossi in all their fantastic forms and colours and the guide will talk about glaciers and icebergs. There should also be a good chance of spotting some seals as they pop their heads out of the water to draw breath and look around them inquisitively.
Tour number: JAVO4

MIDNIGHT TRIP

The same trip arranged at midnight during the period of the midnight sun, 24 May to 24 July.
Tour number: JAVO5

BOAT TRIP TO OQAATSUT VILLAGE

This trip passes through the ice-filled Disko Bay 15 km to the north to Oqaatsut where we go a shore. We have a couple of hours to explore the village which has a population of 70, who all live by trapping and fishing. At Oqaatsut we can enjoy a Greenlandic "coffee-mik", where the local women's association will make coffee, tea and cakes for the visitors (this costs slightly more - see price list). We have a good chance of spotting seals on route.
Tour JAVO6

BOAT TRIP TO OQAATSUT WITH BARBECUE

Similar to the preceding trip, but after staying an hour in the village we sail further north and go a shore. Here we will have a Greenlandic barbecue where you can taste the season's specialities: seal, whale, dried meat, dried strips of halibut, ammasatter, smoked halibut, crowberries and so on. After about one hour for lunch we sail back to Ilulissat.
Tour number: JAVO7

BOAT TRIP TO ILIMANAQ VILLAGE

This triptakes us to the attractive village of llimanaq, south of Ilulissat. On route we pass the extraordinary ice fjord and there should be a good chance of sighting seals. We go a shore at llimanaq where we have about two hours to look around, experience the life of the village and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.
Tour number: JAVO8.

OTHER BOAT TRIPS

We also arrange boat trips to other towns, villages or locations in Disko Bay, fishing trips, bird-watching trips, seal hunting etc. Ask for more information at ILULISSAT TOURIST-SERVICE.
