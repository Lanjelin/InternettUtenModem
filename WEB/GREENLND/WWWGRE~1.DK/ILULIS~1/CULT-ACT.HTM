Cultural activities Ilulissat 1997


Dog-sledge races are events which again and again during the winter and spring gather crowds of children and people of all ages who enthusiastically enjoy the excitement of the contest. In February, March and April a series of dog-sledge races are run by children and adults. It is certainly worth seeing a dog-sledge race where you have the possibility of feeling the excitement and at the same time joining the locals in cheering on the participants.
