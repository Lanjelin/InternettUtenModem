Welcome!

Ilulissat Tourist-Service bids you welcome to Ilulissat, the centre of Disko Bay. We hope you will enjoy your stay here in town with its beautiful surroundings.
Ilulissat Tourist-Service will be pleased to help you with tourist information and arrange tours on foot, by dog sledge, boat, helicopter or bus - according to the season and your wishes.
We will do our very best to make sure you take some wonderful memories home with you from Greenland.
You are also welcome to pop into our shop where we offer a wide variety of Greenland’s handicraft products, books, postcards, leather goods, sealskin coats and much more. Ilulissat Tourist-Service can also take care of your hotel bookings or reserve alternative accommodation such as hostels, accommodation in sports halls, school dormitories, private houses and overnight stays in villages and cabins in the surrounding area. llulissat Tourist-Service collaborates with many travel agencies and functions as the local operator for the tours mentioned above and other arrangements.
