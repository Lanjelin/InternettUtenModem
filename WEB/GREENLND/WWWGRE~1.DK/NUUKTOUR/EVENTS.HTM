F E B R U A R Y Inauguration of the cultural center
Greenland's Cultural Center was inaugurated with glamour on February 15 when Nordic Ministers attended the opening ceremony with lots of music, art exhibitions, concerts etc.


M A R C H Snow Sculpture Festival
The exciting and inspiring annual snow sculpture festival where many of the town´s citizents and international artists compete for the best snow sculpture. This year´s snowsculpture festival takes place March 15 - 18.


J U L Y Nuuk Maraton
Nuuk Maraton is the highlight for runners in Greenland and foreigners. Greenland is a very special running country. The air is clean and nature is close at hand - a delight for the soul and the body!


A U G U S TNuuk Festival
In cooperation with local interest groups, Nuuk Municipality arranges an annual Nuuk Festival. People are attracted by the happy atmosphere, music, food and entertainment in all variantions
