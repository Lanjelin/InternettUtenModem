Bookings and participation in tours and events organized by Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism are subject to following conditions. Please note that visitors who want angling must take out a fishing licence. Fishing licences are obtainable at Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism.

1. RESERVATION / PAYMENT
For reservations, apply directly to Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism or through your travel agent. To confirm your reservation, payment in full must be made at time of booking and accepted by Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism
2. TICKETS AND FARES
Prices quoted in Tour Programme 1997 are for one person. For participation in tours and arrangements a valid ticket is required. Tour ticket will be delivered upon receipt of payment or a voucher at Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism.
3. CHANGE OF PROGRAMME
The right is reserved to change plans during to course of any tour should conditions
( e.g. weather conditions) so warrant. For any refund apply directly to Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism.
4. POSTPONEMENT / CANCELLATION
Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism reserves the right to cancel certain tours due to an insufficient number and bookings. The right is reserved to cancel or postpone any touring arrangement should weather conditions or failure of maschinery so warrant.
5. CANCELLATION BY PARTICIPIANT AND BOOKING REFUNDS
Should a tour be cancelled by Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism the amount paid is fully refunded. If tour reservations are cancelled no later than 7 days prior to the date of the tour/tours in question, the applicable fare will be refunded excluding a cancellation fee of DKK 50,- if such cancellation is made less than 7 days prior to the date of the tour/tours in question only 50 % of the paid amount is refundable.
6. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE
Tour members participate in tours and excursions at their own risk. When transported by boat, car or aircraft tour members are subject to cumpolsory insurance. Tikilluarit A/S * Nuuk Tourism shall be exempt from all liability in respect of any condition arising in connection with reservations or participation in tours and excursions, including postponements, delays or cancellations of touring arrangements.
