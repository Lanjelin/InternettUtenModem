Maniitsoq has approx. 3,000 inhabitans.
Including the settlements 4,000 people live in
the municipality of Maniitsoq.

Maniitsoq is also known by it's
former Danish name, Sukkertoppen

Outdoor facilities include:
Apussuit Skiing Center
Maniitsoq Skiing Center
Kayak Center - to open 1997
Boat Charter

Accommodation:
Hotel Maniitsoq
The Seamens Home Maniitsoq
Camping ground to open 1997

Connections to Maniitsoq:
By air:
Scheduled helicopter flights Maniitsoq-Nuuk
operated by Greenlandair
(Airport for fixed-wing airplanes to open 1999)
By sea:
Ferry route Nuuk-Maniitsoq-Sisimiut
operated by KNI Pilersuisoq
