Switchboard
The telephone number of the Airport Authority's switchboard is (+299) 11300; once connected, you can ask to be put through to the extension number you require.
When calling from abroad, please note the time difference to Greenland.
Bank
It should be pointed out that there are no banks in Kangerlussuaq, so please make sure you have ready cash, cheques or credit cards. The hotel, shop, restaurant and souvenir shop, etc. all accept credit cards.
Church
There is no priest or vicar in residence, but whenever one is visiting, details of church services will be posted on the board.

Kangerlussuaq Tourism
Tel. no. (+299) 11098
When calling from abroad, please note the time difference to Greenland.
Monday to Friday Kangerlussuaq Tourism's representative can generally be reached at one of two places. Either at the Welcome and Tourist Information Desk in the Arrivals Hall or at the office on A corridor of Kangerlussuaq Hotel.
During the summer season: extended opening hours at the weekend are announced on the notice board in the Arrivals Hall and on Kangerlussuaq Tourism's door near the office.
It should be noted that during sightseeing and similar arrangements, the office is normally unmanned. If the representative is out, this is announced at the Information Desk, with details about when that person will be available again.
Kangerlussuaq Tourism offers tourist information, guided sightseeing excursions by foot, bus, boat, helicopter or snow-scooter and lectures and talks, etc.
Please note that all opening hours mentioned here are subject to change. Find out about any changes locally.
