Good Amenities for Angling

Excellent fishing waters in the Kangerlussuaq area

Rod fishing is allowed round the year, that is you may fish as soon as fishing waters are ice-free.

Fishing permits
Lake Ferguson
Fine lake-fishing for char sized 500-1,500 g, some larger fish up to 2-3 kg. Good fly-fishing amenities. Lake Ferguson is usually ice-free from mid-June.
Kangerlussuaq Fjord
During the period from the end of the close season to mid/late-July. Slightly unclear water at times. Very fine coastal fishing for char sized 1-2 kg with good chances of fish sized 2-3 kg; possibility of trophy fish up to 4-5 kg.
River Robinson
Mid-late July until end of close season. Strong-flowing river, slightly unclear water. Fish: as under coastal fishing.
Transport facilities
Arrangements for transportation to the fishing sites can be made by Kangerlussuaq Tourism. For your information, transportation to the fishing sites in the fjord and to Robinson River is by boat and takes 2-4 hours.

Fishing permits

Fishing permits can be purchased from Kangerlussuaq Tourism or from the police in Kangerlussuaq.
