SFJ-23: THREE DAY DOGSLEDGE TRIP TO SISIMIUT
From march to mid April

To Sisimiut.
Trip takes 3 days, 2 overnights in primitive cabins.
DKK 3750 per person incl. provisions.

Day 1: Arrival by SAS. Pack equipment and change into arctic gear at 12.00. Departure at 13.00 hours. Drive on the fjord ice, mountains and lakes. Arrive at the first primitive cabin at Amitsorsuaq at 18:00 hours. Dinner and overnight in the primitive cabin.

Day 2: Departure at 08:30. Lunch on the dogsledge route. Drive on land, rivers, lakes and up a steep mountain in the middle of the tour near Itivneq. The mountain climb requires a good physical condition. Arrive at Nerumaq cabin at 20:30. Dinner and overnight in the primitive cabin.

Day 3: Departure at 10:00. Drive through beautiful valleys, on lakes, rivers and land. Lunch on the dogsledge route. The last hour on sea and finally up a steem mountain. The climb is steep and sledges go fast downhill. One can choose to walk, or ride along. Arrive at Sisimiut at 18:00 hours.

There is no age requirements, but a good physical condition for the above-mentioned tour is a must. To experience the musk oxen and the ice cap in Kangerlussuaq, it is a good idea to arrive a day earlier.

For all dogsledge tours, it is generally expected that riders hop off the sledge and run along once in a while. This helps to keep the body and feet warm.
