The Nature
Breathtakingly magnificent, Greenland's natural beauty awakens your senses and stretches your mind.

If the group rooms' walls could talk, they would provide plenty of inspiration. On corporate strategy, budgeting, organisational development, taxation, building renovation, sun spots, sales- campaign planning and disturbances of the human circulatory system. These are just a few of the topics for which Kangerlussuaq has provided conference facilities.
Widely differing subjects, but they have one thing in common: the participants' enthusiasm.

Something happens to people when fresh air fills their lungs and they are surrounded by a landscape that is at once so overwhelming and so fantastic in its detail that it is quite simply indescribable.
The thrill deep inside when your husky-drawn sledge moves off. The kick of a snow scooter starting up.
Your pride when you're the only one on the team to find a rare flower like the white Alpine campion. The awe that fills your soul when you stand at the edge of the inland ice.
And your colleagues' admiration when you hole in one on the world's most northern golf course. Kangerlussuaq Airport is on Strømfjord, at the mouth of Watson River.





A fascinating area with plenty of scope for excursions.

Kangerlussuaq Tourism offers sight-seeings and excursions in the dramatic surroundings all year round. Or you may hire bikes or cars and go on your own. Check out the possibilities.
