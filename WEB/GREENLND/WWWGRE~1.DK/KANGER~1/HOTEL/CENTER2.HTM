Facilities of the Congress Centre





Kangerlussuaq Hotel and conference centre has all the necessary facilities for courses - and much more besides.
Kangerlussuaq Hotel adjoins the airport terminal. It is a modern building with 336 rooms, bar, cafeteria and gourmet restaurant.
A popular meeting place, for conference participants, airline passengers in transit and the local population.
On the other side of the 3- kilometre runway is the main base area, with two hotels, nightclub, 10-pin bowling centre, swimming pool, church, base museum, Arctic research centre, staff accommodation and, last but not least the conference centre and congress auditorium.




The conference centre has a very wide range facilities, including overhead and slide projectors, video equipment, PCs and other audio- visual equipment. There are meeting rooms and group rooms of various sizes, with flip-overs, whiteboards and, if needed, projection screens.
And, very important, good catering - from morning coffee to gala banquets.
The conference centre's technical and administrative personnel are ready to help and offer advice. They look forward to welcoming you to Kangerlussuaq.
