Announcement of
Ujarassiorit 1996 data release

   The Minerals Office hereby announces that the Ujarassiorit public minerals hunt for 1996 is completed.

963 samples from localities all over Greenland were received. Of these samples, 364 were mineralised or otherwise interesting, and sent to a commercial laboratory in Canada for analysis for a variety of trace and major elements.

The analysis results for 1996 plus a short description of the sample type and locality can now be requested free of charge from the Minerals Office, as a database file on floppy disk or attached to an e-mail.

In early March 1997 these data are to be published by GEUS (the Geological Survey of Denmark and Greenland), as an Open File Report containing various locality and anomaly maps.
