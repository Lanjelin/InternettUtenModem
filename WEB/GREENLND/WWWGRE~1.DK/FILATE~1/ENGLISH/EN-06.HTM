Stamp Machine Booklets in subscription

Now it is possible to subscribe to the popular machine booklets. On January 14th we issued the first machine booklet with a combination of 3 whale stamps from 1996.

If you wish to subscribe to all future machine booklets - please contact Filatelia. You can subscribe to mint machine booklets (item No. 42000) or cancelled machine booklets (item No. 43000 - with Nuuk day cancel).
