As neighbour to Canada and USA you find Greenland - a huge island with app. 90% of its area covered by inland ice. Primarily along the west coast of Greenland app. 50,000 people are living on an ice-free area with the size of Norway. You will become acquainted with Greenland's history, flora, fauna, landscape, industry, and culture by collecting Greenlandic stamps.

As a subscriber you have special advantages. 4 times a year you will receive your subscription and a statement of your purchases and balance with us. Choose between several exciting subscription items. Fill in and send the below form already today. You can print out the page and either fax or mail the form to us. You can, of course, at any time change or terminate your subscription with us.

A basic subscription for Greenlandic stamps costs app. DKK 160.00 per year - or DKK 13.00 per month. All stamps are sold at face value and in subscription they are sent to you with no extra costs for mailing. Further you will 4 times a year receive a free copy of our popular collector's magazine Greenland Collector.
