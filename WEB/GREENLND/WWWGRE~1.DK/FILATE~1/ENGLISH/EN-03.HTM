Greenlandic stamps

Greenlandic stamps are tiny works of art showing all sides of Greenland. They have often been praised by foreign media. A number of critics have stressed the high artistic and aesthetic level of the stamps which makes it a pleasure to frank ones letters.

Greenlandic stamps can be collected in may different ways. As single stamps - mint or cancelled - blocks of four, first day covers, souvenir sheets, stamp booklets, or year packs. The choices are multiple.

Maxicards

Some issues also contain maxicards. A maxicards is a beautiful postcard franked with the new issued stamp. Cancelled on the day of issue. An exciting and beautiful collector's item.

Maxicards are only for sale in connection with the issue. Therefore it is advantageous to order these in subscription. You may also choose to order maxicards from issue to issue.
