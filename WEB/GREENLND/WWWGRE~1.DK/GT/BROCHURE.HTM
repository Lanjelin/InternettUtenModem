Greenland awaits you with adventures
that are out of this world

Your visit to the masterpiece of Mother Nature: Gigantic icebergs, calving glaciers, baying sled dogs, sailing trips amongst the whales, visits to the inland ice, helicopter tours. You haven't seen the world until you've seen Greenland!

We offer to send you a hard copy of our new (and very impressive) brochure with a 36 pages introduction to adventures that are out of this world.

 In addition to this we will send you a copy of the pocket-sized "Facts about Greenland" as well. 100 pages packed with information usefull to visitors: Destinations, food, fishing permits, language, population, towns and settlements, how to get around, northern lights, the midnight sun.

It won't be long until you need it!
