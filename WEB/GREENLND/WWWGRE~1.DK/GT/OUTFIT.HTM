Greenland Outfitters help you
If you want to have a unique holiday or something special, the Greenland Outfitters are ready to assist you getting an eventful holiday in the Greenlandic nature. Several Outfitters have developed their own special tours which they are the only ones to offer in the area.

Greenland is a magnificient country
The pure and unspoiled nature is indescribable. The experiences varies from the quiet life in a settlement, hiking and fishing tours to demanding expeditions on the inland ice - where you really get to try your strength against nature. The possibilities are innumerable.

The Greenland Outfitters guides offers:
o Hunting
o Fishing
o Kayaking
o Simple accommodation
o Sailing

o Skiing and sport activities

o Snow Mobile
o Hiking

Greenland Outfitters are people with considerable knowledge in the fields they have been authorized as Outfitters.

Greenland Outfitters live in Greenland
All Greenland Outfitters live in Greenland and therefore they know a lot regarding history, culture and nature.
Apart from the outfitter activity most of the Outfitters have a principal occupation as for example fishermen, hunters, teachers, shop owners, reindeer breeding etc. This can give your stay an extra dimension of the life in Greenland.

Language
Regarding languages the skills of the Outfitters varies from beeing able to make oneself understood in a foreign language to talk the language fluently. The Outfitters count Greenlanders, Danes, Icelanders, Frenchmen and Italians. As a minimum all the Outfitters can make themselves understood in English The Outfitters knowledge and experience often more than compensates for lacking English proficiency. As an example this regards the fishermen and hunters.

Service, quality and safety is guaranteed
Going on adventure with an Outfitter guarantees good personal service and that the tour is carefully planned to the last detail with safety as top priority, that the equipment is in order and that everything will be done to make the tour a special experience.

All Greenland Outfitters have attended an intensive Outfitter course, a first aid course and have a VHF radio certificate. They know the nature, the special weather- and climate conditions and know how to behave in cases of storm or bad weather and emergencies.

Insurance
Authorized Greenland Outfitters are covered by a colletive liability insurance for travel agency business. The insurance covers business responsibility, product/service responsibility and loss of wealth according to Danish law of package tours (from EU directive on package tours).

Nature, environment and culture
For Outfitters the conservation of the Greenlandic nature is important. Tours arranged by Outfitters shows consideration for the vulnerable arctic environment and unnecessary pollution is tried avoided. The local population is also taken into consideration as well as the preservation of Greenlandic historical values.

Greenland Outfitter authorization
Only Outfitters with the necessary qualifications and experience can achieve one or more authorizations within one of the 10 specialties. The authorizations are issued by the tourist trade and Greenland Tourism a/s. The authorizations are valid one year at a time.

Close cooperation with tourist offices and regional tourist companies
Outfitters work in close cooperation with other Outfitters, tourist offices and regional tourist companies in Greenland.

In cooperation with the tourist offices and regional tourist companies Outfitters can arrange tours for minor groups or package tours for important tour operators. The duration of the tours can be tailored from a few hours to expeditions of several weeks.

Special arrangements
More and more people require unique experiences during their stay in Greenland This can be special arranged fishing tours, tours with rubber dinghies, mountainbiking, hiking tours, kayaking, expeditions, dog sled expeditions of long duration, glacier climbing, survival courses etc. At such tours it is invaluable to arrange and carry through the tours with an experienced Outfitter who knows the local conditions.

If you have not planned your activities in advance in the towns you plan to visit and require something special - then ask for a Greenland Outfitter at the local tourist office.

Prices
Tours arranged by Outfitters differ in price. The prices will depend on the length, the content, the transportation form, the equipment and the service requested on the tours. Normally the prices follow the directional prices at the tourist offices.

Greenland Outfitters do not yet exist everywhere in Greenland
The Outfitter association is under structure, therefore you cannot find Outfitters everywhere




Many Outfitters do not have regular office hours or telefax. Instead they have an agreement with the local tourist offices regarding replies to inquiries or bookings.

A Greenland Outfitter can always be identified on the logo which you will see on caps, name plates and stickers.
