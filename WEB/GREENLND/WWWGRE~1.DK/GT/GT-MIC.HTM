Conference or salesmen's meeting in
surroundings from another world?

Greenland is a whopper of an experience - and now it's within your reach financially, too.

Greenland awaits you with unique natural scenery and plenty of opportunities for fresh inspiration and experiences from another world.

Put some distance - in most literal sense of the word - between you and the daily grind, and replenish your think-tanks with fresh ideas in wonderful Greenland.
A conference in Greenland will put things in their proper perspective, and new thoughts will be well thought after a drive behind 12 baying huskies.
Or after an encounter with a shoal of whales at play, perhaps in the glow of the midnight sun.
Or after a helicopter trip to the ice cap.
Or after your encounter with tranquility and the immense azure sky.
Or ... yes, the possibilities are endless in Greenland. And, what's more, virtually all hotels and conference facilities are less than 10 years old.
