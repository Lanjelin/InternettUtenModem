  Airlines and Ships

Getting to Greenland is easier than ever with direct flights from Canada, Denmark and Iceland. Once you're here you can sail along the coast or fly by helicopters and domestic planes.

Ships:
KNI Pilersuisoq Shipping Division operates a fleet of modern ice-class passenger ships along the west coast.		 Adventure Cruise Greenland is the new holiday option in 1997. Cruise around the Disko Bay or through the fiords of South Greenland.

Airlines:
Greenlandair Inc. has routes from Canada and from Iceland to Greenland and operates an extensive network of domestic routes. Also helicopter charter division.

  Mineral Resources

The hunt for mineral resources is booming in Greenland like never before. Companies from around the world are present in an increasing number to search for oil, gas, gold, diamonds, zink etc.
 The Minerals Office, Greenland Home Rule Government, offers assistance to mineral companies and takes part in research projects.
