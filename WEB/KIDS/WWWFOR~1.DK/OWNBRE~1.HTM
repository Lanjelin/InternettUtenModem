Denmark has one of the largest comics productions in Europe, and most of the nationallly written and drawn books are published by Carlsen Comics. Among others Valhalla, Nofret, The Dimension Detective, Gøngehøvdingen, Occupied Denmark, Kurt Dunder, Quark and many more.
Some of them has even got their own web sites on the internet, where you can explore them further.
