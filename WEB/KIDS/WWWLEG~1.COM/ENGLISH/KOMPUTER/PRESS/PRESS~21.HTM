Facts and figures about LEGOLAND Windsor

Size of site:
150 acres of wooded landscape. The five themed activity areas occupy around 40 acres of the site

Theming:
Only truly themed park in the UK - themed to LEGO bricks

How many bricks:
Around 30 mio. standard LEGO bricks in the Park, and 20 mio. in Miniland alone e.g. Tower Bridge has 100.000 bricks and took 500 man hours to build; Dinosaur, the largest model in the Park, has nearly ¼ mio. bricks.

Estimate time for visit:
5-6 hours

Investment:
£85 mio. self-funded investment

Visitor attendance:
1.4 mio. the first year

Audience:
Children of all ages with specific focus on children aged 2 - 13

Opening:
10.00 am - 6 pm daily (8.00 pm July/August) 29 March - 29 September 1996 as well as weekends and term week in October

Why Windsor:
A beautiful site, 9 mio. families within our target age range, within a two-hour drive time

Location:
The Park is situated two miles from Windsor on the Windsor to Bracknell/Ascot road

Catering:
Mövenpick Marché are in charge of catering on-site. There are five restaurants/cafes in the Park and further 11 catering stalls

Highlights:
17 rides, incl. two driving schools, Boating School, Fairy Tale Brook, Spinning Spider, Pirate Falls
21 attractions incl. the Gold Wash, Explorers Institute, Magic Theatre, Waterworks, Miniland five live actions shows, The Brick Builders Rock & Soul Revue, LEGOLAND Harbour Adventures, Welcome Show, The Puppet Theatre and Brickadilly's Circus Show

Number of employees:
75 full-time and 600 seasonal staff

Telephone numbers:
