Green light for establishment of LEGOLAND Carlsbad

- The LEGO Group's third family park open to the public in spring 1999

The board of LEGO A/S has now approved the plans to build the third LEGOLAND park. It will open in spring 1999 in Carlsbad, north of San Diego in Southern California.

The project is already well into the planning phase but the final decision to embark on the construction awaited results from the first season at LEGOLAND Windsor.

The British LEGOLAND park, which opened on 29th March this year, has fully lived up to expectations. It has proved extremely popular with visitors, with a total of approx. 1.4 million forecast for the season, and has been very well received by the local and international media, the local community etc.

LEGOLAND Carlsbad will cover an area of approx. 100,000 mý and approx. 1.8 million visitors annually are budgeted. When the park is in operation it will employ approx. 700 people.
