LEGO® SCALA is a brand-new doll's house with infinite variations.

You can decide the layout and furnishings yourself. The various doll's houses, decor and charmingly detailed furniture and accessories can be combined, changed and extended according to your own taste.

A family consisting of different dolls will be the heart of your play with LEGO SCALA, and together with the house the dolls offer you many hours of entertaining play.

The NEW LEGO SCALA product combines your favorite doll's house and role play with the flexibility and creativity that characterize the LEGO products, and the product can be integrated with other parts from the LEGO assortment.
