Hundreds of Creative Toys for the 3 to 16 Year-Olds



FreeStyle
FreeStyle sets provide all kinds of regular and decorated bricks for free creative building.

Belville
Belville is designed for family role play in and around a fabulous house and ranch.

Town
Town features cars, police station, race track, trucks, airplanes and lots of people to build a realistic town, full of action.

Time Cruisers
Time Cruisers are really far-out time machines for creative builders and travellers.

Castle
Castle is for heroic building and fighting with dragons, knights and magic forces.

Pirates
Pirates have ships, forts, admirals and masked people, crocodiles, sharks and a treasure to fight for.

Western (in North America: Wild West) - a brand new product line
Western is an evergreen among toys with fort, gold mine, prison, stagecoach, covered wagon - and dynamite.

Aquazone
Aquazone is the deep blue where Aquanauts search to find the survival crystal - and Aquasharks try to stop them.

Space
Space is exploring new galaxies and meeting aliens - and building on a higher level.

Trains
Trains are the fastest 9V electric trains around. Build them and discover all the cool details.

Ships
Ships are big models that are fun to play with on land, too.

Model Team
Model Teams are magnificent models to build and display. They are larger than all other LEGO® SYSTEM models.
