From 1 1/2 years - LEGO® DUPLO® Building sets

From 2 years - LEGO DUPLO Play sets

From 3 years - LEGO DUPLO Toolo sets

At this age, experimenting is fun and challenging. Stacking things and knocking them down are wonderful - and good for developing motor skills. LEGO DUPLO building sets are designed for this type of play and will last forever.

 When children begin to experience life outside their own home, they love to copy it when they play. Therefore, you will find a fire brigade, police station, cars, houses, airplanes, trains and lots of animals in the LEGO DUPLO themed sets for this age group.

 At a certain point, most children want to copy everything mum and dad do - or try to do. To help them we have developed the Toolo tool boxes with elements, screwdriver and wrench, so that children can learn to construct - without getting hurt.
