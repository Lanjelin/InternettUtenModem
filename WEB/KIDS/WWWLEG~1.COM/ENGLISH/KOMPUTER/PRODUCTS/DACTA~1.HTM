Sets for Group Play

 These sets are designed for use in infant, preschool and other child school institutions. They encourage children to play and interact through a variety of creative building and role-play challenges. The open-ended materials help develop motor skills, problem-solving skills, creativity, and spatial relations. Besides being lots of fun, the broad range of LEGO® DACTA group play sets will support the development of the child as a whole.

Sets for Schools

What is the new LEGO DACTA Intelligent House?

LEGO DACTA educational sets provide a valuable curriculum-relevant resource for teachers of science, technology, computer education and math. Through building and experimentation, students not only learn about scientific methods, they also use them. These sets form a comprehensive, flexible and progressive range, suitable for students of all ages.
