LEGO® PRIMO Baby Toys

LEGO PRIMO Baby toys are new and unique products from the LEGO Group, suitable for the very youngest child. Each toy is - through the use of bright colours, sounds and shapes - designed to stimulate the senses and motoric skills of your baby, and encourage him/her to experiment, learn and develop.

LEGO PRIMO Baby toys give hours of fun play with toys of leading quality and safety, and the play changes and grows with your child. The baby toys are furthermore meant to be combined with LEGO PRIMO Stack 就 Learn products - for more fun and learning!

LEGO PRIMO Stack 就 Learn

LEGO PRIMO Stack 就 Learn is large-scale building for small children! With the Stack 就 Learn bricks and the many special elements, your child can from the age of 6 months start having lots of fun while stacking and playing.

With Stack 就 Learn there are no limits for your child! Everything is possible, and each product encourages experimentation, learning and development - "The sooner you start, the further you go!".

And, of course, Stack 就 Learn can be combined with LEGO DUPLO for even more fun!
