Starter sets for children 7 years or more

With these sets, you will learn how motors, pistons and gears work. You learn to build with new technical elements, and step by step building instructions will guide you safely through.

Advanced sets for children 9 years or more

With these sets you can build new technical functions into your models. Make them work via a Control Centre, glow in the dark with the Fiber Optic System, or run by means of a special motor set. Building instructions for at least 2 cool models are included in each set.
