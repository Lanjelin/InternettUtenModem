What is the new LEGO® DACTA Intelligent House?

How do you design a house that automatically turns off the lights when the room is empty and turns on the fan when it gets too hot?

With the LEGO DACTA Intelligent House students use LEGO bricks, sensors, and easy to understand computer programs to replicate the conveniences and security systems found in today's modern homes.



The Intelligent House software features computer graphics that represent the actual model. Students are challenged, using the computer, to make the model work. The Intelligent House project disk contains eight student activities and five optional activities.



The LEGO DACTA Intelligent House is specially designed for classroom use:

  • Easy to get into, instant success
  • Appeals to both boys and girls
  • Cooperative learning
  • Student centered activities
  • No previous experience needed by the teacher
  • Easy classroom management
  • Icon based software
  • Available for PC and MAC computers
