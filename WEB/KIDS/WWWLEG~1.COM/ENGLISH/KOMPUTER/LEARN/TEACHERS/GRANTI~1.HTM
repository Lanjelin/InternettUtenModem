Steps in Obtaining a Grant

Use this information as a guide for developing your grant proposal. Develop your project idea based on your classroom needs; at the same time, investigate various sources of funds. Your LEGO Dacta representative can help you locate funding sources, determine the availability of training, and provide you with additional resource material and supporting literature. When you have found an appropriate funding source and you have your project well in mind, you are ready to write your grant proposal.

1. Develop Project

  • Brainstorm project ideas and decide which LEGO® DACTA® products meet your class needs.
  • Evaluate and select the most promising project idea. Collect supporting literature.
  • Plan your project in detail. Your LEGO Dacta representative* can arrange for training.

2. Locate Funding

  • Identify possible sources of funding (see document below). Your LEGO Dacta representative can help.*
  • Write to funding sources for proposal guidelines.
  • Study guidelines and select appropriate funding sources.

3. Write Grant Proposal

  • Write proposal in accordance with guidelines. Revise proposal after review by peers. Request letters of support from faculty, principal, chairperson, LEGO Dacta representative.
  • Have an outsider read the final draft. Submit proposal on or before deadline by certified mail.

* To obtain the name and telephone number of your LEGO Dacta representative, call 1-800-527-8339.
