About LEGO Dacta


Group Play Products

LEGO® DACTA Group Play products, for children as young as six months, are designed for use in day cares, preschools, kindergartens, and afterschool programs. The sets feature traditional DUPLO and LEGO materials specially packaged to ensure that at least four children can happily play together, either as a group or independently. With Group Play toys there are always enough pieces for each child to complete her masterpiece!

DUPLO Primo

DUPLO Primo sets, for children ages six to 24 months, feature animals that make rattle sounds, figures, mirrored pieces, and large stacking blocks with rounded corners. The brightly colored blocks are specially designed for tiny hands to hold and fit together.

DUPLO Basic

DUPLO Basic sets feature oversized blocks that fit easily together to develop motor skills through stacking, sorting, and free-form building. Youngsters never tire of playing with DUPLO blocks because it's different every time. For ages 1 1/2 and up.

DUPLO Theme

Designed to stimulate imaginative play, our DUPLO Home, Community, and Animal Sets feature traditional DUPLO blocks and lots of detailed pieces, including: furniture for the Home, rescue vehicles for the Community, and lions, tigers, and elephants for your very own Animal Park. For ages 2 and up.

DUPLO Toolo


DUPLO Toolo sets contain a special screwdriver and wrench, which preschoolers use to build in a very "grown-up" way. And there are no small parts because all of the screws are built-in. DUPLO Toolo sets, designed for children 3 and up, can be combined with traditional DUPLO blocks.

LEGO DACTA FreeStyle

LEGO DACTA FreeStyle sets stimulate free-form building in children three years and older. Lots of LEGO bricks and special pieces let children build anything their imagination suggests. How about a chicken on a skateboard? Or a car with a propeller? There's no wrong way to build--just the thrill of creating your own world. For ages 3 and up.

LEGO DACTA Theme

Featuring hundreds of LEGO bricks and specialty pieces, LEGO DACTA Theme Sets set the stage for imaginative play in your very own home town. For ages 5 and up.
