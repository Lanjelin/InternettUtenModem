LEGO® Learning Community

We, in LEGO Dacta, believe that our children's development and education are issues that can not be given too much attention. Therefore, we are in the process of extending this LEGO Dacta WWW site with a membership based learning community.

The LEGO Learning Community will provide educators, parents and children with a global meeting place, where the objective is to develop and define new standards for learning. It will be a place where we can exchange experiences, and discuss how learning can be made more exciting for our children, with the goal of making children love to learn and rediscover the fun and excitement of Science and Technology.
