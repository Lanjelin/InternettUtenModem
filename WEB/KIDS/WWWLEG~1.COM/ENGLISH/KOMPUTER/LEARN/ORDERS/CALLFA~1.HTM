Ordering Info

Check Orders

Order by Mail

  • Fill out the order form, including method of payment, and send it along with your check to LEGO Dacta, Custumer Service, 555 Taylor Rd., Enfield, CT 06083-1600.
Note: All prices are in US$.
If you live in Canada, please contact us at 1-800-527-8339 for dealer information.
If you live outside the of North America, please contact the LEGO Dacta distributor nearest you.

MasterCard/Visa Orders

Order by Mail

  • Fill out the order form, including method of payment, and send it along with your check to LEGO Dacta, Custumer Service, 555 Taylor Rd., Enfield, CT 06083-1600.

  • Please list all digits from your credit card, expiration date and include your signature.

Order by Phone

  • MasterCard and Visa credit card holders may call in their orders weekdays from 8:30am to 5:00pm (Eastern Time) by calling 1-800-527-8339.

Order by FAX

  • MasterCard and Visa credit card holders may fax in their orders 24 hours a day to 1-860-763-2466

  • Clearly fill out the order form completely.

  • Please list all digits from your credit card, expiration date and include your signature.

  • Fill out your daytime telephone number in case we have any questions about your order.

  • IMPORTANT: Please do not send in your original order if it has already been faxed (to avoid duplication).

Note: All prices are in US$.
If you live in Canada, please contact us at 1-800-527-8339 for dealer information.
If you live outside the of North America, please contact the LEGO Dacta distributor nearest you.

Purchase Orders

Order by Mail

  • Fill out the order form, and send it along with your signed Purchase Order to LEGO Dacta, Customer Service, 555 Taylor Rd., Enfield, CT 06083-1600.

  • Please include your federal tax ID number if appropriate and indicate th PO number on the order form.

Order by Fax

  • Purchase orders may be faxed 24 hours a day to 860-763-2466.

  • Please indicate all items clearly, include your federal tax ID number, and all appropriate signatures.

  • IMPORTANT: Please do not send in your original PO if it has already been faxed (to avoid duplication).
Note: All prices are in US$.
If you live in Canada, please contact us at 1-800-527-8339 for dealer information.
If you live outside the of North America, please contact the LEGO Dacta distributor nearest you.
