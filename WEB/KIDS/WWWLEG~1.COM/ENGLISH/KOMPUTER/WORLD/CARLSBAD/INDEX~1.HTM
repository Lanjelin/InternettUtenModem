LEGOLAND® Carlsbad, Southern California

Key Park Facts

Expected Opening: Spring 1999

Projected Annual Attendance: 1.8 million

Average Daily Attendance: 6,000 visitors

Parking: 3,500 spaces

Park Acreage: 35 acres inner park, 45 acres parking and circulation and 48 acres outer park area



Community Benefits

In addition to creating a unique and special place for children and families, the LEGO Family Park is being carefully planned to "fit" well into the Carlsbad community. The Park offers Carlsbad and the region several benefits, including over 800 construction jobs, 600 new jobs in the Park and millions of dollars in regional economic benefits and new tax revenues.
