Enjoyment For Everyone, All Year Round

LEGOLAND Billund in Denmark is a child's paradise - a LEGOLAND Park for the entire family.

The ideal setting for holidays, courses and conferences. International-class rooms. The hotel has a central location next to the LEGOLAND Park. Close by, there are an 18-hole golf course, Givskud Lion Park and other exciting attractions. Billund has an international airport.

The starry sky in the excellent restaurant at Hotel LEGOLAND always sparkles, and there are heather views through its panoramic windows. In these beautiful surroundings, you can enjoy food prepared with a delicate touch from fresh raw materials.

We have single rooms and rooms for 2, 3 or 4 people, all with colour TV and telephone. For our business customers, there are rooms with good working and storage facilities. Telefax available. Some rooms have mini-bar and safety deposit box.

Rates include breakfast. Admission to the LEGOLAND Park valid during your entire stay is DKK 70,- per person. All activities are free except for the Traffic School.

Attentiveness and involvement are our watchwords. We have meeting rooms and an auditorium that can hold up to 300 people. We organise special events like banquets and tournaments. Our conference staff are at your service throughout the event. The video conference equipment is available in a video meeting room and in the auditorium.

The place to relax in Hotel LEGOLAND is the Lounge, with its open fire. Settle into one of the comfortable, easy chairs with a cup of coffee after your meal or walk. There are leisure activities in the LEGO room, and the billiards and table-tennis room. And Billund's swimming pool is close by.

The hotel has its own entrance to the LEGOLAND park. There is a very favourable admission price for our guests - and your ticket is valid throughout your stay.

Hotel LEGOLAND's Shuttle Bus

Departures from Billund Airport:

The shuttle leaves at intervals according to all flight arrivals from Monday to Friday between 06.15 - 13.30 and 15.00 - 23.30. Saturday: 06.00-13.00 and Sunday: 15.00-23.30. Should the shuttlebus not be parked in front of the airport building, please contact the information desk. You will be picked up immediately.

Location and Transport Connections
LEGOLAND Billund is:
- 100 km north of the German border
- 100 km south of Århus
- 250 km west of Copenhagen

Billund Airport - Denmark's second largest - adjoins the Park and there are many good roads connecting Billund to the Danish highway network. There are several daily flight connections to Copenhagen.

Billund Airport has direct international connections to/from:
