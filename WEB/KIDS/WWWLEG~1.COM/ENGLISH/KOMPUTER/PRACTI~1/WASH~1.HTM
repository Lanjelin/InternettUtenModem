Washing LEGO® Bricks

  • We recommend that DUPLO® and LEGO elements are washed by hand with a soft brush at a max. temperature of 40° C (104° F).
  • If the water is too hot, the elements may lose their shape and their clutching power will be affected.
  • Washing symbols are noted on boxes and in product brochures.
  • After washing, leave the bricks to dry on a cloth.
  • Do not wash electrical parts and magnets.
