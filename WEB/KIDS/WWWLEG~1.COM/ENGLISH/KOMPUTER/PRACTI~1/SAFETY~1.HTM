Toy Safety

DUPLO® and LEGO® elements are designed for children's play. For example, the elements must be able to withstand being in a child´s mouth, chewed, stepped on and used as a hammer.

The safety requirements for toys can be roughly divided into the following categories:

  • mechanical and physical properties
  • chemical properties
  • flammability
  • electrical properties

Each of the above four areas has a number of international requirements which every DUPLO and LEGO product must fulfil.
