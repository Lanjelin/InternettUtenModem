Missing Pieces

Children get irritated if they can't build a model because one or more of the special elements have disappeared. One of the good reasons for having well organised storage facilities for LEGO® bricks is that children can then find the LEGO elements that are actually in their rooms.

Unfortunately, many small elements are accidentally swallowed by vacuum cleaners. This is difficult to avoid completely, even if a LEGO collection is stored properly... Occasionally, losing one element can mean that a model cannot be completed or may not function at its best, though in most cases it will still work.

If one or more elements are missing, replacements are often available in the supplementary sets and consumer service bags obtainable from LEGO special departments. If this proves impossible, consumers are welcome to contact their national LEGO Sales Company or LEGO Distributor.
