Job@LEGO Futura Aps

Designers

For the Product Development Department in LEGO Futura ApS we look for even more imaginative, effervescent and happy employees.

The Job:

Concept development and development of elements, models and decorations within LEGO SYSTEM for preschool children.

Qualifications:

General Qualifications Applying to All Jobs:
As a creative employee in a development group your are dynamic, responsible and able to work towards a well defined object that you sometimes have to define yourself. You are able to efficiently communicate your ideas to others and you are equally perceptive of their ideas. Interest in and experience with preschool children is definitely an advantage.

Qualifications Applying to One of the Jobs in Particular:
You are full of good ideas for elements and gadgets that can do things. Probably your home is stuffed with all kinds of hobby models for which you have constructed supplementary elements and so on. In short, you are able to draw, cut, carve and turn almost anything.

We Offer:

We offer the opportunity of working on projects in small teams that all work together on developing new LEGO SYSTEM products for the entire world. We have all the equipment necessary for developing world-class toys covering everything from MAC computers to lathes and plasticine.

Who Are We:

We are a small project group working with new ideas for LEGO SYSTEM products.
