Data Communication Consultant

For the DataCom Group in our EDP Centre, LEGO System A/S we apply for a data communication consultant.

The job: Primarily you should work with WAN Circuit Switching and Packet Switching for our subsidiary companies and Remote Access, E-mail, electronic fax and Internet.

Qualifications: You are probably engineer, and you must thoroughly understand data communication in general and Wide Area Network technologies in particular, especially terms as TCP/IP, DEC net, X.25 and Frame Relay. You must have the faculty of proactively being able to see where the technological development moves. Furthermore, it would be advisable if you know of Win NT, X.400 and X.500.

You must have a good humour and a positive outlook and have the faculty of working independently and together with colleagues towards a common goal.

We offer: An exciting job in an environment in a violent process of change with a lot of new challenges.

Who are we: The DataCom Group in the EDP Centre is responsible for data communication solutions in the LEGO Group’s companies at home and abroad. The group is part of the EDP Centre’s operation department which consists of four groups with a total of 47 employees.
