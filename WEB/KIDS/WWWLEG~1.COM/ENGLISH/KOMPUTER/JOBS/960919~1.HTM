Web Community Entrepreneur

The world is changing quickly. Several jobs that will be in demand 10-15 years from now do not exist today. Children that are growing up right now, need to prepare their minds for how to deal with challenges that have not yet been defined.

"Therefore we need to stimulate children’s desire to learn, to experiment and be creative, to analyze and solve problems, to work together and collaborate across cultures and to critically utilize Science and Technology."

As we succeed in this endeavor, kids will be capable of developing their own skills, - when they need them.

learningcommunity@www.LEGO.com

In LEGO Dacta, the educational division of the LEGO Group, we have shaped a NEW learning concept that will offer kids (and other people with a young and curious mind) Toolkits and Microenvironments for exploration and creation of personal solutions to a series of relevant and intriguing challenges.

A vital part of this concept will be a LEGO Learning Community on the Internet.

"Therefore we are looking for a person with the skills, values and attitude listed below to take the lead in the design and implementation of this Community in cooperation with an international team of talented and creative partners."

Do YOU have this profile?

YOU have several successful Internet projects in your luggage YOU are a team player, able to get the best out of the people you work with YOU perform well under turbulent working conditions and tight deadlines YOU are flexible yet focused and determined YOU go for high value and do not compromise on quality YOU hold as a minimum a Bachelor´s degree YOU are fluent in English and are used to travel, also in the physical world YOU are prepared to spend at least 4-8 weeks in Billund, Denmark to get familiar with the LEGO Company in general, LEGO Dacta in particular, our values, vision, objectives and strategies.

... and do you connect to this?

"Children’s desire to learn is driven by curiosity, pleasure and activity. Children learn best when they are actively engaged in constructing a personally meaningful artifact - be it a robot, a poem, a sandcastle, a web page or a computer program."

NO: Exit!
YES: Continue!
