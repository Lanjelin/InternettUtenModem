SAP Consultants

Due to many challenging and coming tasks in progress deeply rooted in the so-called "Backbone Project", we are now applying for supplementary resources for the SAP organization.

The Job:

As SAP consultant you have the possibility of getting an all round knowledge on the standard system SAP R/3 - and the implementation of this system - in a fast and solid way. See the job as an investment.

You will be a resource in one of the many projects and "situated" in the project team in question you have the daily back up and access to sparring around you. Be it said: only your ambitions set limits to your involvement even from day 1.

Through the project course you will participate in clarification/determination of business processes and standards related to these, and you must parallel with this be responsible for setup and test of parts of SAP R/3. Also ABAP programming and activities as regards infrastructure will occur dependent on contents and possibilities of the project.

Qualifications:

The decisive is that you have ambitions and cannot stop pursuing an objective until it has been reached. It is also a demand that you think and act service-oriented as much of your time will take place away from your desk and in the customer’s half. Finally, an open and cheerful mind will suit the informal tone we use among ourselves.

We expect that you are able to analyse problems, find concrete solutions and realize them together with the team. We would appreciate if you have a further education but first of all we are looking for persons that can convince us that they match the above-mentioned.
