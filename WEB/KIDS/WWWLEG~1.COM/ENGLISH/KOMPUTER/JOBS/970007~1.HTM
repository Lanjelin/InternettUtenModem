Packaging designer for the LEGO Group

For International Merchandising & Packaging Construction (IMPAC) we need another designer in our R&D dept. for structual design de-velopment of packaging and storage solutions.

The job:

You will be working on design and development of new plastic solu-tions and paperboard packaging constructions. Furthermore you will be working on individual, creative packaging solutions for promo-tional purposes.

Your job will proceed from sketch plan to maturing the products for production in collaboration with internal as well as external suppliers from both Denmark and from abroad.

The stages of development will be from sketches, preferably over Cad/Mac drawings, to hand made samples.

Qualifications:

Of course you have creative skills and can visualize and present your thoughts and ideas to various expert groups. You can document creative results within the advertising and gra-phical trade or industrial design and/or be qualified as an ar-chitect or as a craftsman-designer. You must have economical sense for industrial development. Preferably you have knowledge of Mac. Of course you speak English, but if you understand a bit German and/or Danish this will not be amiss.

We can offer you good conditions of employment and good staff fa-cilities in a relaxed atmosphere.

In IMPAC we are 17 employees. We are working on development and maturing of new packaging concepts and on packaging and storage constructions for our yearly novelty product programmes.
