Commonly Requested Federal Services, designed to provide you with one-stop access to Federal services -- online transactions, forms, and publications available for downloading and printing, and information.

Just Released! Over thirty new services have been added to this feature. The Peace Corps, the Coast Guard and the U.S. Merchant Marines are a few of the new agencies adding content to Commonly Requested Federal Services for the first time. The General Services Administration has created an "electronic Blue Pages" offering a user-friendly display of the phone numbers and contact information for government services in an increasing number of geographic locations. Also, look for additional material from the Department of Housing and Urban Development, the Department of Health and Human Services, and more.
