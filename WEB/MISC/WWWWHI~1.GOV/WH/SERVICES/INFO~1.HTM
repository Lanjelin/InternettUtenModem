Welcome! Commonly Requested Federal Services is designed to provide you with one-stop access to Federal services -- online transactions, forms and publications available for downloading and printing, and information.

Our long term goal is to offer the American people the ability to conduct all of their business with the Federal government electronically. Commonly Requested Federal Services is evolving. Check back as new services, forms, and information are added. If you don't find what you need, use the feedback option to let us know what you want.
