Job Search and Job Offer for Buddhists

Our primary focus is aimed at new and innovative ways of assisting Buddhist practioneers in coping with the changing employment methods and job trends as we move into the information age. We offer a full spectrum of assistance and services for both the job seeker and employer aimed at taking advantage of internet technologies.

KKBN only acts as an intermediary in the 'matching-process'. There are no fees to be paid, however only approved Buddhist members can utilize the service.

  • Job seeker: Scan this page if you are looking for a job
  • Employers: Scan this page if you looking for a new employee
  • Registration with KKBN: Add your search or offer to the lists
  • Creative Job Search: An article by Kevin McDowel

  • Starting points for a job search on the Web:

    • Internet Sites for Job Seekers and Employers: A listing of over 650 On-Line Job-Search Resources and Services, maintained by the Purdue University Placement Service. The largest list of job resources on the net.
    • Virtual Yellow Pages: Opportunity World Section - lists various sites pertaining to Opportunity and related areas of interest, from Business Opportunities to Recruitment.
    • Cornell University Hotel School's Career Services Office
