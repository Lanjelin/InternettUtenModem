Contact

There are many different ways to get into contact with the Diamond Way Buddhism. A first impression is presented on these pages by teachings and meditations. If you prefer to meet a teacher, you should contact one of our centers worldwide.

In case you do not find a center closeup, feel free to contact us by email or phone us on our InfoLine.

Additionally you can consult various books or videos, which are available worldwide.
