Buddhist Listservers with e-mail

Subscribe to these to have all messages of discussion sent to one's e-mail address.
Note: Only send subscriptions from your own e-mail address.
Example:
      To:       listserv@iubvm.ucs.indiana.edu
      From:     Peter@V100.magic.com
      Subject:  
      Message:  SUBSCRIBE TIBET-L Peter Falkner
  • WTN-L: World Tibet Network News
    The WTN-L list distributes WTN News, a more or less daily newsletter on Tibet. WTN News contains news reports from the major news gathering agencies, government agencies, and non-governmental organizations. Both Chinese and Tibetan government reports are published freely. Very often WTN News has major stories on Tibet before they appear in local newspapers or on electronic media, and many stories that never make it to the major news media. WTN News is published by the Canada Tibet Committee.
    To join, send subscriptions to listserv@vm1.mcgill.ca
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE WTN-L [your first and last name]".

  • Buddha-L forum
    A discussion group covering many Buddhist topics.
    To join, send subscriptions to Listserv@ulkyvm.bitnet or
    listserv@ulkyvm.louisville.edu
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE BUDDHA-L [your name]".

  • Forum on Indian and Buddhist Studies
    Another discussion group covering many Buddhist and other topics.
    To join, send subscriptions to listserv@jpntuvm0.bitnet
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE BUDDHIST [your name]".

  • Insight
    This discussion group covering many Buddhist topics has an emphasis on Theravadin vipasana practice and it is affiliated with the organisation Dharmanet International.
    To join, send subscriptions to majordomo@pioneer.ci.net
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE INSIGHT".

  • Journal of Buddhist Ethics
    An online Buddhist academic journal.
    To join, send subscriptions to Listserv@psuvm.psu.edu or
    listserv@ulkyvm.louisville.edu
    The subject should be: "SUB JBE-L [your name]".

  • Tibet-L: Tibet Interest List
    A discussion group about both Tibet and related issues like Tibetan Buddhism.
    To join, send subscriptions to listserv@iubvm.ucs.indiana.edu
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE TIBET-L [your first and last name]".

  • de-buddhisten-l
    A mailinglist for German users in German.
    Die de-buddhisten-l-Mailingliste dient dem Austausch von buddhistisch orientierten Usern. Es wird über wichtige Ereignisse informiert. Der Aufbau von Kontakten über elektronische Netzwerke (v.a. Email) soll durch diese Mailingliste gefördert werden.
    Verwalter: de-buddhisten-l-owner@deceiver.org
    To join, send subscriptions to: majordomo@deceiver.org
    The message should be: "SUBSCRIBE de-buddhisten-l"
