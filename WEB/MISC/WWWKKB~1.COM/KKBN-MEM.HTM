Membership with our Centers

The Karma Kagyu Buddhist Centers work in many different countries and regions. Many of them are registered as a charitable trust. Members can participate in the Center's decision-making and are kept well informed of the Center's activities e.g. through newsletters. Membership fee depends on local situations but is always small.

You can also help the Centers by making a regular monthly donation (a pledge) towards them or by donating whatever you can, whenever you can. Donations are - in most countries - tax reductible.

If you are interested in a membership or pledge form or have other inquiries, send a message to that effect to the Center (for addresses look at our Center list).

Membership with the Karma Kagyu Buddhist Network

The KKBN serves to support our members and to connect them to other interested people. When you subscribe you gain access to the different services. General information is available for everyone who is interested through this WWW-service. Special services are reserved for the centers and functionaries.
