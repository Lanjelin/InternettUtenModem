The city of Karlsruhe was founded by Wilhelm von Baden-Durlach, who wanted to construct his new residence for "future emotional delight". Thirty-two roads lead out from the palace in all directions. For this, the city is called the "fan-shaped-city". One of the oldest technical universities in Germany is situated in Karlsruhe.
