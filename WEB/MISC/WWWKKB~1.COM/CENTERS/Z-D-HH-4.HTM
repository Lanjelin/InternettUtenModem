Today, there is an old factory hall on the ground which will house the large, flexible meditation hall after construction. A front house planned for restauration offers space for the Café and the 20 members of the the Buddhist living community running the center, paying average rents.

The price for the grounds is DM 1.8 million - appropriate for the location. The construction of the meditation hall will cost DM 200,000, the renovation of the front house DM 540,000 DM. Including tax and agent fees, the total price is DM 2.6 million. All prices have been checked by experts.

The North German non-profit-organisation will be able to spend DM 60,000 on the project. Additional DM 200,000 have been donated by supporters since 1993. However, much more is needed: to receive a loan which we can then pay back, an additional DM 300,000 are still missing.

If you want to help
