Buddhist Center Bonn, Germany

Our center is located in a central part of Bonn in a 160 year old house. It was founded in 1990 by Lama Ole Nydahl according to the wishes of H.H. the 16th Gyalwa Karmapa and is under the spiritual guidance of H.H. the 17th Karmapa Th! aye Dorje.

Though the center Bonn doesn't possess its own premises a member of our association has put a meditation room at the center's disposal. The meditation room under the roof of the house (see the pictureat the top) has a size of about 75! square meters (about 90 square yards).

The members of the center are lay practitioners, who are engaged in different professions. The center offers various kinds of activities: regular meditation practice, Dharma lectures, and teachings of lay-teachers and Lamas like Ole N! ydahl, who has been visiting our center each year, up to now.
