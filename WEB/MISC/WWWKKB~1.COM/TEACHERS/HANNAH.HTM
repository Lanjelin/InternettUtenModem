Hannah Nydahl

Hannah Nydahl has been a much sought after translator and interpretator of Tibetan Buddhist philosophy for the last 20 years. She divides her time between translating for the teaching lamas at the Karmapa International Buddhist Institute in New Delhi, India, participating in various Buddhist texts translation projects, organizing schedules and visits of high Rinpoches in the lineage, and travelling around the world with Lama Ole. Hannah is known by her students for her calm, perceptive and caring style.
