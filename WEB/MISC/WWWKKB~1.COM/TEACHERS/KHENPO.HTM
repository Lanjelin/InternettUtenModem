Khenpo Chodrak Tenphel Rinpoche

Khenpo Chodrak Rinpoche received his education under the direct guidance of the 16th Gyalwa Karmapa. He taught 10 years as one of the main teachers at the Nalanda Institute in Rumtek, Sikkim. Presently he and Topga Rinpoche are mainly responsible for the curriculum at the Karmapa International Buddhist Institute (KIBI) in New Delhi as well as for the education of the 17th Karmapa Thaye Dorje.
