Karma-Kagyu-Dachverband KKD e.V. GERMANY

Largest association of the Karma Kagyu tradition of Tibetan Buddhism in Germany: Joining together of presently 7 nonprofit regional associations and 50 city and rural centers as well as numerous meditation groups comprised of several thousand members in total. The centers work under the spiritual guidance of the KKD chairpersons Lama Jigme Tsewang Rinpoche and Ole Nydahl, founder of most of these centers. The Buddhist teachings are conveyed in a lively and clear way with an awareness of our way of life today. Each week there are many lectures, evening meditations, weekend seminars and extended courses with both Western and Tibetan teachers of the Karma Kagyu tradition. The centers of the KKD registered association have close and friendly relations with over 160 Kagyu centers in Europe, Russia, the US, South America and Australia which work under the patronage of H.H. 17th Karmapa Thaye Dorje. The KKD registered association is the editor of the magazine 'Kagyu Life' in which authentic teachings of the Kagyu tradition and reports about current events are published on a regular basis. 'Kagyu Life' magazine is also published in English, Polish, Danish, Russian, Ukrainian and Hungarian. Further information about the 50 KKD centers are available at the office Schwarzenberg.
