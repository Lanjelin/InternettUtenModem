Green Tara

Green Tara is the embodiment of the activity of all Buddhas. She is known as the great liberator, specializing in overcoming obstacles in whatever form they manifest in our lives. No deity in the Buddhist pantheon is more popular than Tara. She is especially known for her power to overcome the most difficult situations, giving protection against dangers and all kinds of fear.

The mantra of the Green Tara is:
OM TARE TUTARE TURE SO HA
