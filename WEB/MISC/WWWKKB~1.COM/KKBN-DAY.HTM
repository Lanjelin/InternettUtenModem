Losar

Tibetan New Year (Losar) is on February 8th, 1997.

The next Tibetan New Year (Losar) is on February 27th, 1998.

The Great Events

Celebration of the Great Events recall the Birth and the Four Great Acts of Lord Buddha Sakyamuni. According to the sacred words of the Buddha, performance of virtuous deeds on these auspicious occasions enables one to reap 100,000 moral merits.
  • Cho nga Chopa (the Miracles): February 22th
  • Kutampa (the Birthday): May 13th
  • Sangyepa (the Enlightenment): May 22nd
  • Chokhor Duchen (the First Teaching): July 8th
  • Lhabab Duchen (the Descent from Heavenly Realm): October 22nd
The Tsurphu Astrological tradition, established by Ranjung Dorje, the 3rd Karmapa, based on His own insight about astrology and the Kalachakra system, is especially reknowned for its accuracy in the forcast of lunar and solar events.
