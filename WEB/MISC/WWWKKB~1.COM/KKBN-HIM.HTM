Himalaya-Hilfe e.V.

Meaningful help - what could be easier?

It is the association's objective to offer assistance to people in need in the Himalaya region. In particular, children should get the opportunity for school and continuing education. Furthermore, medical treatment should be available to old and sick people without means.

In order to accomplish this we want to support schools and other educational institutions as well as hospitals and nursing wards. Also, we want to help individual people in their situations through personalized sponsorships.

All this requires money and sponsors. People who are able and wish to help in a meaningful way.

For us - especially as Buddhists - this represents a great opportunity to practice generosity.

Current projects

  • Sponsorships for monastery students, lay children, nuns and old people
  • Individual donations which support the association's endeavors.
