Knowledge in Action

Knowledge in Action is the journal of the Karmapa International Buddhist Institute. It will be published bi-annually and its purpose is to present articles on relevant topis of interest within the field of Buddhist Studies, as well as to share current information about centers, and events of interest. Letters, comments, suggestions and editorials from readers are welcome. In addtion, all articles submitted will be considered for publication. Please address all letters to:
