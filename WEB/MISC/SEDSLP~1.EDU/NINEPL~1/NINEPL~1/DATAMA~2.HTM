The Best Prospects for Life

     Name  Why
---------  -------
Earth      :-)
Mars       most Earth-like; more so in the past; ALH84001
Europa     may have liquid water
Enceladus  may have liquid water
Titan      complex chemistry and liquids likely
Io         complex chemistry, warmer than most
Jupiter    long shot: warm, plenty of organic material

The Mars meteor results notwithstanding, all of this remains very speculative. None of these prospects are really very good. Good arguments can be made why life (at least life as we know it) cannot exist on any of these bodies. (More speculation about life on Europa and Enceladus.)
