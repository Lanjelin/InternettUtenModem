Page To Go

 Easily save web pages (with all their graphics and links) right down to your computer. Makes collecting web info a snap! Web pages and sites are constantly changing - which is great. But it also can be frustrating when you need to refer back to specific pages - they probably won't be the same the next time you go to them. Page To Go is a way to electronically "tear a page off the web" and preserve it in time exactly the way it was live on the web.


Site To Go

 Take portions of a web site or even an entire site down to your desktop so you can browse through it later - even when you're off-line! Saves time and money (dial up customers can take a site then disconnect and stop paying while you're reading). If you had Web Buddy, you could be browsing through these pages on an airplane right now! Surfing through sites taken To Go looks and feels exactly the same as when you were connected live. All the links and graphics are reestablished for you locally on your computer.


Schedule

 Let Web Buddy automatically take pages and sites "To Go" while you're busy doing other things (or when the web isn't so busy!) You have a powerful computer on your desk. Why not have it do things FOR YOU. Have Web Buddy go out onto the web for you in the middle of the night and take pages and sites To Go. Then browse through them whenever you want. Have it deliver the pages from your favorite news site every morning, for instance!


Translate

 There's no shortage of information out on the web. But how are you going to easily use that formatted, graphical info in your own "desktop world?" With Web Buddy's Translate utility you can use any web page or Internet graphic in your favorite word processor or graphics program. The page looks the same as it did in your browser! Here's a way to share info with non-Internet users.


Bookmark

 Powerful, "customizable" bookmarking, too. When you create bookmarks to your favorite sites and pages with Web Buddy, you can immediately put those bookmarks into categories. This makes organizing bookmarks quick and easy. If you forgot a category, create one on the fly.


Organize

 Use "Web Buddy Central" to easily store and retrieve all your collected and categorized web pages, sites, graphics, bookmarks and more. No more searching through your entire hard drive to find info and files you saved or bookmarked off the web! (Click here for a screen shot of Web Buddy Central.)
