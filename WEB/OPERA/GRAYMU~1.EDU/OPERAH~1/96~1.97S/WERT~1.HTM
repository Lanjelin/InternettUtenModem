Werther
by Jules Massenet

Saturday, November 23
Monday, November 25, 1996
Performed in French with English surtitles

Werther is Massenet's tragic masterpiece. Based on Goethe's novel, the opera premiered in Vienna in 1892 to wide acclaim. Successful performances in Paris and London quickly established Werther as a lush, lyrical 19th century French romantic opera that would stand the test of time. The plot is a passionate story of unrequited love between Werther, a young poet, and Charlotte, whose mother's dying wish bethroths her to another. Direct by Opera Memphis' Karen Tiller.
