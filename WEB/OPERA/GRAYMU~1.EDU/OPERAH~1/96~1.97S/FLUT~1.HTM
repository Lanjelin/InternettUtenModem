The Magic Flute
by Mozart

Friday, February 28
Saturday, March 1, 1997
Performed in English

The genius of Mozart remains supreme in this fantasy masterpiece with its incomparable flow of heavenly music. The Magic Flute was a huge success from the beginning. The fairy tale begins with a handsome young prince lost in a valley. The bird-catcher Papageno and Prince Tamino go off on a quest to save the beautiful Pamina, daughter of the Queen of the Night, and find themselves on a magical journey. Opera Memphis favorites John Miles and Erin Windle star as Papageno and the Queen of the Night. Directed by Opera Memphis' Dale Morehouse, this performance will feature a new production commissioned by the Tennessee Bicentennial.
