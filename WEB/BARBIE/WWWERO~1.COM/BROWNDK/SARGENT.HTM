Sargent's Dolls
John Singer Sargent
(1856 - 1925)

A very rich and very distinguished doll collector had long admired the triple portrait of "The Wyndham Sisters" by John Singer Sargent in New York's Metropolitan museum of Art. Wouldn't it be wonderful, she thought, if Sargent would do a portrait like that of my dolls? Arrangements were made and Sargent made this portrait for the collector. And then she awoke. (Detail)
