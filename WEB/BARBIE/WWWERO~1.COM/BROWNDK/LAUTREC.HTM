Moulin Rouge
Henri Marie Raymond Toulouse-Lautrec
(1864 - 1901)

Another unfinished work, this one by Toulouse-Lautrec. Krutz in his definitive work, Unfinished Works of Art, says it is "clear that the work is unfinished as the dancers are not wearing underwear." But McGurk, in her scathing feminist critique of Krutz's work, points out that dancers at the Moulin Rouge never wore underwear. "Why else would Toulouse-Lautrec have spent so much time there?"
