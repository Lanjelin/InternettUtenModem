Venus d'Milo
Annonymous
(c. 1100 BC)

The Romans called her Venus and the Greeks called her Aphrodite but today she is the goddess of love in every language. Still, she spent some two thousand years at the bottom of the Agean Sea. Underwater. The sharks got her. We know this because she has no arms. Check her out the next time you are at the Louvre, Paris.
